daniellefergus say: Hi DWneedshelp,

I am sorry to hear that you are having a problem removing this Administrator account. Here are a few things you can try to remove the account.

Log into your Family Account.

Once you are logged in, go to your start menu and type computer management.
The computer management window will open.
Go down to the Local Users and Groups area and expand it
click on the users folder
After clicking on this you should see all the accounts that are listed on your computer.
Find your administrator account you want to remove click on it to highlight it and then right click. You should see Delete in that area to remove the account.

If that does not work, you can try these steps.

Go to your start menu and type command prompt.
When the program shows up, right click to run as administrator.
once the command window opens, you can type this command
net user Administrator /delete.

Please not that the name after net user has to be the EXACT name of the administrator account you would like to remove.
