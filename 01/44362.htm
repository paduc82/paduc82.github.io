liezl fal say:
Hi Kieran,

If you are getting an error message when connecting to a particular USB port, there is a possibility that the port is not receiving enough power. The best way to isolate the issue properly is to transfer the connection to another port. If the error message is the same, reboot your computer and try again. By rebooting, you will need to shut down your computer and disconnect the power supply. If you are using a laptop, then you should also remove the battery. The reason for disconnecting all power to your computer is to ensure that the motherboard is also reset. Leave it off for 10 minutes or so.

If the issue persists, you may update the driver of your USB Input device. You can go to the Device Manager of your computer and right click on the USB Input device under Human Interface Devices to do the update automatically.

We are here for your feedback.
