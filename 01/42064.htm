All the issues you are experiencing have been reported here and on other forums so you are in good company.

The screen flickering when scrolling past embedded videos on Edge (and some have reported this behavior on other browsers) is a known bug. My SB displays this behavior, although it is not consistent. The Hello camera issue you are experiencing is reported by many and is a much discussed issue on this and other forums. Did it happen when exiting from Sleep or Hibernate? The default setting for Hibernate is two hours (when on battery power). In my case (and others have reported similar behavior on this forum) the Hello camera ceases to work after the SB has been in Hibernate for a period of time, usually more than 30 minutes. I can readily recreate the issue by forcing the SB into Hibernate and waiting a half hour or so. When I bring the SB out of Hibernate the SB reports "Turning on camera..." but it never turns on. In my last call to MS support they indirectly suggested it was a firmware issue that MS was working on. I also have another well reported Sleep issue. Once the SB enters Sleep with power connected (so it doesn't go into Hibernate or Hibernate is disabled) after about one hour the SB will crash requiring the user to press the power button to restart it. The SB reboots and anything that was left open (apps, web pages) prior to entering Sleep will be gone as a result of the crash/reboot. This error is reported as a critical error in the Event log.

What you are experiencing are reported symptoms for the SB. I wish I could offer constructive help but like many of us who have the same or similar issues, I am waiting patiently on MS to provide updates to address these issues.
