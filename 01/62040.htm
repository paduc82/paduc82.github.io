computerwarrior say: I've had my 360 for over ten years.  The main issue that shuts down a system is the power supply. You say its not a power issue, but that is exactly what you're encountering.  If a power supply is degraded, the 5v an 12v peripherals can interfere with eachother for load.  If the HDMI is adding a load, that the Power Supply (PS) cannot handle, it will detect a fault and shutdown.  Mine is simply shutting down the NIC when I ad HDMI for it to display and boot up.
