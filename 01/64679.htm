  • What was the last thing you were doing on the Surface?

  • Check connections, and clean the connector on the power supply.

             - Unplug the power supply from the wall.

             - Clean the pins on the connector with a pencil eraser.

             - Clean the pins on the connector with a cotton swab wet with rubbing alcohol.

             - After you are done cleaning the pins, make sure all the pins on the connector are dry, plug the power supply into the wall, and then plug the connector back into your Surface.

             - Double-tap or double-click Microsoft ACPI-Compliant Control Method Battery, select the Driver tab, and select Uninstall > OK

              - For Windows Update, go to Settings > Update and Security > Windows Updates > Check for Updates

             - From Start > Power > Shutdown. Allow the device to charge for about 45 minutes before turning back on. This will allow Surface to charge as quickly as possible without interference.

                Go to Start > Settings > Update & security > Recovery.

                Under Reset this PC, select Get Started. Follow the on-screen instructions.

              - Best: USB Recovery

                 You will need to download and set up a recovery image on a USB drive. Used USB Recovery link to help you get started. Information on what size and type of drive is provided at the destination page.

Adjacent protector can make Xbox easier.  It is not designed for constant spikes (ie from a generator). Its near zero design is for a single spike.  Repeated spikes can, in rare cases, cause it to become the source of a fire.  Meanwhile, protection routinely designed in all electronics makes spikes from a generator irrelevant.
