tony seaside say:

I was stuck with this error in Windows 7. Eight recent (August 2012) updates were failing - 4 Important, 4 Recommended.

Try opening Control panel>Windows Update. On the left click on "Change Settings".

(Or Control Panel>System and Security>Windows Update "Turn automatic updating on or off").

Then change the "Important updates" drop down option to "Download updates but let me choose whether to install them".

Of the four check boxes below this I have the top two unchecked, and the bottom two checked (ticked).

Click [OK].

You may find you can now select updates one at a time to install. I installed the 4 marked "Important" in one go. Then the "Recommended" ones one at a time. All were successful.

Note you may have to go though the "Shut down and install updates" and resulting "Failure configuring updates" reboot process one more time before you can select them individually.
