how to attach a excel doccument to email in window 8 - Microsoft Community

dumbdumb218 say:

how to attach a excel doccument to email in window 8

I have written an email and I want to attach an excel document to it.  I am using Window 8 and my mail is different and I cannot find the button to attach the attachment.

jetta48 say:

If you are using the Windows 8 Mail app then:

 

1. Create a new message by clicking on the + sign in a circle near the top right.

2. Right-click on the page and the Attachments icon (a paperclip in a circle) will appear near the bottom left of the screen*.

3. Click on the Attachments icon, browse to your file, select it with a left-click then click Attach.

4. When you're ready to send it the Send icon is the flying envelope near the top right of the screen.

 

 

 *Tip: If you click on Add a message in the right pane before doing this you will get a wider choice of icons such as those for formatting and emoticons.
