a. user say:

Service Host doesn't use any bandwidth. As the name implies, it's merely a host for other services. Similarly, if your house guest takes long showers, you as the host are not to blame for the shortage of hot water.

You need to determine what services are being hosted and which one of those services is hogging bandwidth. Let's hope it isn't malware.

Also, to be honest, 1.5 MB/sec of bandwidth is going to make Windows (or MacOS) a frustrating experience. Were I in the same position I would prefer a tablet with Android or iOS. Those operating systems, and the apps they run, are optimized for low bandwidth applications.
