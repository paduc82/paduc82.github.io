Another possibility is that the mailbox is being accessed from the iPad client using IMAP or Exchange, which are both synchronizing protocols. This means that any action taken in the client will be reflected at the server. Delete a message in the iPad client and it will also be deleted at the server. It's a common habit to read inconsequential messages and then delete them. Is this what your mother-in-law does?
