    Shift + Delete deletes the selected file(s) without moving them to the Recycle Bin. You will need to use data recovery software to recover such file(s).

    When a file is deleted in this way it is effectively marked as unused space on your hard drive. Windows may therefore overwrite the file(s) during normal use making them irrecoverable. It is therefore important to use your PC as little as possible until they are recovered. There are several data recovery programs on the marked - do a web search for them.
