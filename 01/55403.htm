In Windows 10, your device is always kept up-to-date to have the latest features and fixes. Drivers are installed automatically so that you don't have to select which updates are needed.   5. Click the drop-down under Options and select Full, then click OK.
  6. In the Group Policy Editor, double-click Allow Telemetry.
  7. Select Not Configured and hit OK.
