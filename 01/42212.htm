You must verify the following

If the SD card is not recognized in Windows 10, but when inserting it in another computer it appears, in this case you must first check the SD card slot is that the reader cards works on another computer. To check if the problem results from the hardware part of the slot, you can insert the SD card into a USB card reader and connect it to the USB port to see if you can access the data in it. Of course, you can connect another SD card in the SD card slot to check the status.

2- Update Driver
As the drivers are updated from time to time, you need to go to the computer manufacturer's website to download and install the latest drivers for the SD card. Once this is done, restart your computer and check if it works.
  
3- Execute hardware troubleshooting
Connect the SD card and follow the steps below to execute the troubleshooting of hardware.
 
- Enter the troubleshooting in the search box and then press Enter
- Click on Hardware and sound> Hardware and devices.
- Follow the instructions on the screen. Once this is done, restart the computer and check the status.

vijay a. verma say:
Assuming that there is no hardware problem with your card -

1. Right click on Windows Start > Device Manager > Click the topmost entry i.e. your device > Click Action in Menu > Click Scan for hardware changes
Now you should be able to see your card

2, Right click on Windows Start > Device Manager > Expand Disk Drive > Locate the Drive > Right click on this > Uninstall > Reboot your computer
Now it should be detected

3. Use the Hardware & Devices Troubleshooter

Type Troubleshoot in Windows Start Search box > Click Troubleshoot > Click Hardware & Devices > Run the Troubleshooter

4. Whether card is assigned a Drive Letter or not

Right click on Windows Start > Click Disk Management > If you see your card is listed there but there is no Drive Letter > Right Click it > Click Change Drive Letter and Paths > Add > Click Assign the following drive Letter > Click in the Drive Letter List Box > Choose an appropriate Drive Letter > OK > OK
