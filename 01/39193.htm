How to back up and restore the registry in Windows

Yes, that is very relevant. All email has to pass through your ISP's servers, so account settings will have to conform to those servers' requirements. Is your ISP now TalkTalk Onetel?

There's little point in guessing at account settings; normally, a broadband provider will give you the settings you need when you first sign up.
