Make sure the device and your computer are the correct distance apart. The device information should include the correct distance.

The device might be too busy or have too many connections (for example, other devices might be connected to a Bluetooth enabled printer). Wait until the device is less busy, and then try again. Or, to reduce the number of connections, set the other devices so that they are not discoverable.

  1. Press Windows + X, click on Device Manager.
  2. Check for any exclamation marks in there.

Windows 10 has a built-in troubleshooter to check and fix issues with hardware and devices. I would suggest you to run this troubleshooter.

    1. Press Windows key +X, select Control panel.
    2. Change the view by option on the top right to Large icons.
    3. Click on troubleshooting and click on the view all option on the left panel.
