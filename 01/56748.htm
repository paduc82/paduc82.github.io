The error code 0x8007003B is an indicator for a possible cause of connection lost. Make sure to check your connection cable and switch again.

Another factor could be when the Windows Search service is enabled and the folders that are created, renamed, or deleted are marked as indexed locations.

Alternatively, you may temporarily stop the Windows Search service when doing the transfer of file. Here's how you do it:

Services > Windows Search > navigate to Startup type > click on the drop-down button > Disable > Stop button > OK button

Note: Service updates have been known to auto-start the Windows Search service if you disable the service before the updates are applied. Or just re-enable it after copying the files.
