anne ros say:

Hi,

Updates are regularly recommended to be installed so users can have the latest fixes and improvements of Windows. If a specific update is causing problem, you can uninstall it by following the steps below:

  1. Go to Settings.
  2. Click on Update & security.
  3. On the left-pane, click on Windows Update.
  4. Under Update status, click the Update history link.
  5. On Update history, look for the Cumulative Update for Windows 10 Version 1607 for x64-based Systems (KB4033637).
  6. Then click the Uninstall updates link at the upper-part of the page.
  7. A new pop-up window leading to Control Panel will appear. Select the update (KB4033637) and click the Uninstall button or right-click the selected update and click Uninstall.
  8. Click Yes to confirm the uninstall then restart your PC to complete the task.

Note: The process may take some time, depending on the update you're uninstalling.
