Sending multiple documents in one email - Microsoft Community

sbrown101 say:

Sending multiple documents in one email

How do you send several documents in one email
lemp say:

"Attach" the second and subsequent documents in exactly the same way you "attach" the first one.  The method differs depending on which of the many email clients/services you use.

There may well be some size limit to your email (including documents), either imposed by your email service on the size of an email you can send or on your recipient's email service on the size he or she can receive. 

In general, if you have large and/or multiple documents, it may be more efficient to upload your documents to an online "cloud storage" service (e.g., Microsoft's OneDrive, Google Docs, Dropbox, etc.) and just send download links via email.
