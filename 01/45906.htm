You get the challenge under two conditions:

- The application attempts to modify a protected registry key.

It is possible to avoid the first condition. To find out which condition you're dealing with you could do this:

  2. Give one of your user accounts read-only access to the programs folder.
  3. Check if the application runs as it should under this account.
