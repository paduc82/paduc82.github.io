¡firedog say:
The calendar and contacts list you see in Windows Live Mail are determined by the Microsoft Account you use to sign in with. This has nothing to do with any mail account you may have set up, default or otherwise. If you don't sign in, you see the default calendar and contacts list. These are only available on the computer where you access them.

Other calendars and contacts lists are available on any computer with an Internet connection, simply by signing in at http://calendar.live.com and http://contacts.live.com respectively. When you're signed in, contacts and calendar will synchronize with those on the web.

You can add new calendars in either situation.
