    Easy fix on this one:

    1. Open your Windows Explorer (folder explorer)


    2. On the left side, right-click each library (Documents, Pictures, Video, etc.) and choose Delete for each one.  Don't worry it won't delete your files.


    3. Then right-click the Libraries section name, and choose "Restore default libraries"

    Now it should work again.
