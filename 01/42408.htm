    hotmail takes precedence

    That account must be added to the Hotmail account under Sending/receiving email from other accounts.  One of the settings is to Leave a copy of messages on the server.  There are two choices: 

    make sure that Leave a copy is checked
    remove the account from Hotmail

    Either action requires you to go to Sending/receiving email from other accounts.
    If you do not need the emails from the other account in the Hotmail account, remove the account from Hotmail.

    If you are accessing your Hotmail account through outlook.com, click the Gear icon, More mail settings, Sending/receiving from other accounts.

    Removing the account from Hotmail does not delete the account.

    If you access a Hotmail account by logging in to outlook.com, you will see the same emails, contacts, ..........  It is just an updated view.
