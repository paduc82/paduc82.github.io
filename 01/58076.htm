shenan stanley say:

Your computer is not the issue here (thus, neither is your operating system.)

This is a problem with properly setting up your router.

First - you have a GT784WNV (not GT84WNV).

  1. Connect to the router via a wire.
  2. Open your web browser of choice (Internet Explorer, Mozilla Firefox, Google Chrome, Opera, etc.)
  3. Type in the following into the URL (address) bar and press ENTER:
  4. http://192.168.1.1
  5. That should load the web page for the modem/router.
  6. Enter your user name and password and click Continue.
    Your default user name is admin if you didn't change it when you configured your network. **

  ** If you cannot access the gateway’s internal interface, perform the following steps:

  1. Using a paperclip, press in and hold the Reset button for 30 seconds.
  2. After 30 seconds, release the Reset button and turn the power off.
  3. Leave the power off for 20 seconds.
  4. Turn your gateway back on.
  5. Reboot your computer.
   

This will get you into the interface you need to be in for changing your management password (for managing this device - the router) and for setting up your wireless network (I suggest WPA2 for the 'best' wireless security you have available to you and setting a decent and different password from the one used for managing the device.)

If you are having trouble, you can call Verizon and tell them you would like help setting up a wireless access point on your modem/router - specifically you want a WPA2 setup.  They should walk you thru.

Questo pass è un codice, spesso da grattare, che trovi nella confezione del gioco ed è utilizzabile solo da un profilo senza limiti di tempo.

Quindi se tu decidessi di rivendere fifa dopo aver inserito il codice nella xbox, chi lo acquisterà, per giocare online, deve ricomprare un nuovo pass. Anche se è un utente gold.

Per questo Iso ti chiede se nella confezione hai trovato un cartoncino (a volte con una striscia argentata da grattare).
