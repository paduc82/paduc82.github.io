There was literally no working fix for the wireless controller paired with a Bluetooth adapter, as some drivers were missing or impossible to update. 1) Use LockHunter to delete xboxgip.sys, xinputhid.sys, xusb22.sys and MTConfig.sys from Windows/System32/Drivers. Reboot with no adapter connected.

2) Connect the wireless controller to another PC and then copy the entire Drivers folder from that healthy, fresh Windows 10 install. Overwrite any and all files from the one you wanna get fixed. This will patch things up.

3) Launch Xbox Accessories and forget about that pesky XINPUT axis configuration :)
