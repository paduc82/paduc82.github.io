The Access Denied error that you're receiving can be resolved by using bootable installation media. Follow these steps:

  1. Follow the steps here to create installation media in a USB flash drive or a DVD in a working computer.
  2. Once you have your DVD or flash drive ready, hard reboot your computer until you get to the Windows Recovery Environment screen. It usually takes three hard reboots.
  3. Click See advanced repair options.
  4. Click Advanced options.
  5. Click Command Prompt.
  6. Then, type net user administrator /active:Yes and press Enter. This will activate the built-in administrator account.
  7. To change your user account back to an administrator account, type net localgroup administrators "Username" /add and press Enter. Substitute Username with your actual account username. Don't forget the quotation marks.
