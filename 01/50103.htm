SUMMARY:

Getting this issue on pc is a bit tricky. High disk usage can be caused by FIVE different things: Virus Infection, Hard Failure & Compatibility Issues, Application Conflicts or Application Usage, Operating System Issue and Lastly Operating System can't be handled by the hardware.

1. While browsing or using an application/s.

2. After PC Start-up but then go back to normal usage.

I suspect you are experiencing a TPM chip pin lockout.  The TPM(Trusted Platform Module) is a security chip that works with various security features of your sp4 to protect from, among other things, brute force password attacks and will lockout your pin for a predetermined period of time.  It will increase this lockout period each time the pin is entered during the lockout period.  I suggest you leave your sp4 on but not used overnight and hopefully it will timeout and be unlocked by the morning.
