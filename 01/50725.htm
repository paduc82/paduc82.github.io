Windows 10 Update version 1511 on Surface 3 - Remove your microSD Card - Microsoft Community

the cyber knight say:

Windows 10 Update version 1511 on Surface 3 - Remove your microSD Card Before!

There is an installer issue with the SD bus that causes the update to freeze at 40% completion.

Remove any SD card from the slot before updating.

If you're stuck at 40% for several minutes, just shut the Surface down. The installer will revert to the previous Windows 10 version, a process that takes few minutes. Then, remove any microSD card and restart the update process. You unfortunately have to download the update again but, this time, things will go as expected.
