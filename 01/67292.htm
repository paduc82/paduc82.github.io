cager say: There are several things to consider here.

You say you upgraded from Home to Pro - in other words you didn't get an XP Pro installation CD and do a completely new installation?

If you had done a new installation, it would have re-formatted your HD, deleting everything on it - including the motherboard drivers and utilities.

Do you have a CD containing the motherboard drivers & utilities? - because without them the pc won't operate correctly.

If you are happy to do a re-format & new installation, then that is the quick & easy way to get rid of the inherited material.
