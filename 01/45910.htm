jaimelobo say:

Patrick,

As to your other question about configuring without a remote - MCE can be configured without IR equipment, if you are using antenna or direct cable connection, since the tuner in your PC would be actually controlling the channels. If you are using a STB, then you need the equipment connected, since without it channels selection doesn't work.

It really has nothing to do with you using the KB/mouse to change channels vs the convenience of a remote, it has to do with MC not having a way to change the channels (the IR blaster sends a signal from MC to the STB to change stations).

There is one possible workaround, some years ago, on the Monsoon forum, there was an "IR Blaster driver", that fooled MC into thinking the IR equipment was connected. You can give that a try if you wish, but again, without the IR equipment, MC has no way to change channels on the STB. Makes it pretty hard to do unattended recordings (which is one of the main points of having a DVR).

Windows 10 has a feature to project your screen into your smart TV. We'd like to know the make and model of your smart TV for further isolation. To be able to mirror the screen on a third-party device, you need to make sure that the source as well as the receiver have Miracast support. Kindly follow the steps below to use Miracast on Windows 10:

  1. Press Windows key + P at the same time to access the Project Menu.
  2. If your computer supports the Miracast display, then you will see an option as Add a wireless display.
  3. Click on Add a Wireless display. It will start searching for the new displays which are available in the range provided. Once you find the required device on the search results, click on the device name.
