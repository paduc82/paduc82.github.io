However, given you are moving from Windows 7, there's probably very little chance of anything *not* working with Windows 8.1 - unless it has been with you from 2008 or before and was possibly end-of-life according to the manufacturer some time back and just happened to work under Windows 7 (even then, odds are in your favor - but not certain.)
