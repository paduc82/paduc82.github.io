halima s say:

Hi Ang3l luv,

• Are you trying to send an email attachment using a cell phone?

Try the below steps if you are trying to send an email attachment using cell phone.

• Open your e-mail program and compose a message. Be sure to include a title or subject for the message and make sure the message is 160 characters or less, the limit allowed by many cell phones.
• Add the attachment to the e-mail just as you would to send a regular message Click on the open text box to enter the e-mail address for your mobile device.
• Enter an e-mail address in the "Send To" box. The address will include your 10-digit phone number and your particular provider's e-mail address. Example for Verizon users: *** Email address is removed for privacy ***
• Click "Send." The message and attachment will be sent to your phone as a text message.
• Open the text on your phone and you will be provided the opportunity to open the attachment as well.

If you are facing problems it doing the above steps I would suggest that you contact the cell phone service provider for more information.
