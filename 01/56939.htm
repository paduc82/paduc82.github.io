ubergadgetfreak say:

There are a few settings you may want to check.


Go into Desktop

Right-click the wallpaper

Select Screen Resolution


Look for the blue text that says "Make text and other items larger or smaller" and click on it.

"Smaller - 100%" is the 'traditional' setting.  Selecting this will reduce text and fix a lot of apps to look correct for the resolution.


Default on a PRO device is "Larger - 150%)" which can cause things to look oddly sized.


Changing this setting will require you to log off then back on.
