Please don't post incorrect statements. 

Although Adobe created the PDF format, at this point, many different applications can open PDF files, including WordPad (built into every version of Windows) and (free) Foxit Reader, to name just two.  See http://extension.nirsoft.net/pdf  Most modern web browser also can open PDF files.

You don't want Adobe's "acrobatic" software.  Adobe's Acrobat is a very expensive application for creating and manipulating PDF files  If you want an Adobe program just want to open PDF files, you can use free Adobe Reader.  See

Understanding the differences between Adobe Acrobat and Adobe Reader DC

You do not need Adobe Flash Player to open PDF files (and You Really Shouldn't Be Running Adobe Flash Player Anymore).
