Things that cause sudden shutdown:

  Overheating - CoreTemp should tell you your temperatures...  Fans working?  Clogged with dust?

  Bad RAM  - Try running a RAM check program or remove one bank of memory at a time.

  Low Battery - Is your charger working?

 

It could also be a driver or startup program.  Since you can boot to Safe Mode, try running Microsoft's configuration utility "msconfig" (Start -> Run -> "msconfig").  Start by turning off all Services and Startup items and rebooting (this should almost be like Safe Mode).  If it boots, start turning services and startup items on a few at a time, reboot, and see what happens.  It's an iterative process but may locate a bad startup item.
