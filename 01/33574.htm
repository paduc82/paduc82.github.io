In Windows 7, the AllUsers folder is not an actual folder.

It is a Symbolic Link that redirects to the C:\ProgramData folder.

ProgramData is not hidden, by default.

C:\>attrib -h ProgramData

The only solution is to hit the on/off button for 10 seconds.  Then restart the machine to repeat the above process ad infinitum.

georgebirch say: The problem was sorted by Crucial.  The firmware Version 0009 of the Crucial Model M4-CT256M4SSD2 was causing the SSD to freeze after a fixed time usually of the order of 30 to 40 minutes, but in my case 60 minutes. This apparently was a known fault  that occurred after around 5000 hours operating time. When the firmware version was changed to 070H the issue was resolved.

HP laptops have driveguard which ‘braces’ the hard drive when it detects the device is potentially falling to prevent damage.
HP 3D DriveGuard protects your hard drive against rough handling, excessive vibration, and other everyday wear and tear; so when an accident happens, you can go on working.
