TURN NUM LOCK ON AT STARTUP - Microsoft Community

pavithmadusara say:

TURN NUM LOCK ON AT STARTUP

I use dell inspiron 15 3000 laptop. On this numlock key bulb not available. I want it tun on to type my passwor at start up. How set it turn on automatically on startup?

a. user say:

Hi,

Thank you for posting the query on Microsoft Community.

I appreciate your interest on Windows 10. 

I understand your concern and will help you with the issue.

I suggest you to follow the steps below:

1. press Windows key + X  to open Control Panel.

2. From Control Panel ,type Power Plan. In the search box.

3. In power plan, click on Choose what the power buttons do on the left side panel.

4. select Change settings that are currently unavailable

5.Navigate down to Turn on fast startup and uncheck it.

After completing all the steps, reboot your system. You can find that NumLock is enabled on startup.

Alternatively, If you want to do it the manual way, follow these steps:

1. Click on the Start Button and type in regedit and hit Enter.

2. Navigate through HKEY_USERS, .DEFAULT, Control Panel and then Keyboard.

3. Right click on InitialKeyboardIndicators and select Modify.

4. Set the value to 2147483650 and click OK.

5. Reboot and number lock should now be enabled.
