cmdrkeene say:

Windows performs many tasks at night, including backup jobs, degragmenting hard drives, and more.  These are typically the root cause of the wakeups.

You can begin troubleshooting by opening an elevated command prompt* and typing powercfg /lastwake to see what woke up the computer last.

 

 

*To open an elevated command prompt, type cmd into your start Menu, then press Ctrl-Shift-Enter.
