misses joust say:

This is where some regional mismatch issues come in, in many different ways.

First off, I see you have a Gold membership. It's safe to guess that your account's region is set to a different location since Morocco is not Live-supported. This can cause regional mismatch problems when it comes to buying and playing games, like Magic 2014. The game may be supported and work flawlessly in your account's native region, but not in your physical region.

This varies by game, too. Different games will have different regional restrictions, which can explain why you're able to play some games without issue, and others toss up errors, and some games you can't buy at all.

Multinational retailers are just that - multinational. They sell in a variety of countries that are both Live-supported and not Live-supported, so those products are available to those who can use them. Lots of games can be played offline without Xbox Live, so oftentimes Xbox 360 games can still be found and played in regions that aren't supported by Xbox Live.

We always recommend having your account's region match your physical location in order to avoid these mismatch issues. This is reinforced in section 1.10 of the Terms of Use: "You may be unable to use the Services outside the country associated with your account ("Territory")."
