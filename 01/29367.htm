a. user say:

Hi Patricia,

I would inform you that when a AC adapter is connected to a laptop, the battery gets charged till 100% and the connection between the AC adapter and battery is cut. After the battery is completely charged, laptop only uses AC supply.

The other option is to remove the battery and plug the AC Adapter. Now the laptop only uses AC supply.

However, if you want to disable battery charging while the AC adapter is connected, you have a option in Battery Meter (in Control Panel or click on battery icon on taskbar). Once you enable that option battery will stop charging even if the AC adapter is connected. Once you reboot the system, battery will automatically start charging normally.
