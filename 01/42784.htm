jimwynne say: Cloning and imaging are not the same thing.  A clone is a complete copy of a hard drive made to a second hard drive, while imaging creates a large compressed copy of the drive or partition that can be retained for future use.  There are advantages to both methods.  A cloned drive can be swapped immediately, while an image must be restored, which takes time.   An image, on the other hand, can be retained and used again if necessary.
