If Start Menu stops responding on your PC, then it might be due to one of the below reason.

* System might have loading still. (You confirm this by waiting on desktop screen without triggering an action for 2-4 Minutes)

* Some drivers or applications creating conflicts.

* User Profile got corrupted or some system files might be missing or infected.

  1. As mentioned above when you reach the sign-in screen, hold the Shift key and select the Power button, and then select Restart.

Resetting PC with Keep my files option will do the following,

-Reinstalls Windows 10 and keeps your personal files.

-Removes apps and drivers you installed.

-Removes changes you made to settings.

-Removes any apps your PC manufacturer installed. (If your PC came with Windows 10, apps from your PC manufacturer will be reinstalled.

  The large November Update (alias v1511, Build 10586) was basically a full upgrade as was the original RTM release in July.
