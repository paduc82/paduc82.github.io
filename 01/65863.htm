does ram have to be added in equal pairs?

I had 2 sticks of 2gb ram and then added a 4gb stick.  It recognized the only 4gb and actually ran slower. Windows home premium 64 bit OS.

gts-nj say:    Generally, it is not necessary to install RAM in pairs but most computers today support dual channel memory and there is a performance benefit to matched pairs.  Generally though, more RAM even in not dual channel will out perform less RAM.

  In your case, I suspect that you have installed a combination that the motherboard doesn't support.  Check the specs for your computer re. exactly what the RAM requirements are.  You can also look up your machine at crucial.com which will have some information.
