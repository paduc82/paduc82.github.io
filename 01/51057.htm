tryx3 say:

The Trusteer Rapport support site includes the following advice for enabling screen capture on "partner & sensitive websites".  This procedure allows all other Rapport functions to remain active.  I've used the procedure once and it worked correctly but I then reverted to normal settings [blocking screen captures] because I became more concerned about malware.

- - - - - - -

Extract from Trusteer Rapport website - Enable screen captures

Trusteer Rapport inhibits capturing screenshots of its protected sites.  This may be overridden in Rapport Advanced policy settings.  When you browse to a protected website Rapport blocks screen capturing attempts to prevent malware from capturing sensitive information that is presented by your browser. A screen capturing program would show black screens when your browser presents a protected website.

For this reason Remote Desktop software such as LogMeIn, WebEx, VNC, Teamviewer and others will be blocked if Rapport is installed on the Target computer and a protected website is accessed.

If you are trying to capture the screen of a protected website you should first turn off this specific protection mechanism by following these steps:

1   Open Rapport Console:

  •           Click the Rapport icon, located next to the browser's address bar, and then click "Open Console", or
  •           Click Start -> All Programs -> Trusteer Rapport -> Rapport Console

2   Change the policy by following these steps:

  •           At the bottom of the console there is a green right arrow - click it
  •           Click "Edit Policy"
  •           Enter the letters as they are shown in the image and then click OK.
  •           Find "Block Screen Capturing " (1st row)
  •           Next to "Block Screen Capturing", Click the drop down menu and choose "Never"
  •           Click "Save"

3 Reboot your computer

Hi Lionel,

I would suggest you to try Clean print and check if it helps.

Clean Print.

Follow the below steps to perform Clean Print.

  1. Press Windows + R keys on the keyboard.
  2. Type services.msc into the textbox and click on Enter.
  3. Find the Print Spooler service and click on Stop from the left pane.
  4. Uninstall the printer software from the Control Panel under Programs.
  5. Press Windows + R keys on the keyboard.
  6. Type regedit into the textbox and click on Enter.
  7. Follow the path HK_CURRENT_CONFIG > system > CurrentControlSet > Control > Print > Printers and delete your specific printer.

 

Disclaimer:serious problems might occur if you modify the registry incorrectly. Therefore, make sure that you follow these steps carefully. For added protection, back up the registry before you modify it. Then, you can restore the registry if a problem occurs. For more information about how to back up and restore the registry, click on the below:
http://support.microsoft.com/kb/322756/ 
 

8. Find the folder Spool/Spooler under C:/Windows/ and delete all the contains of it.

9. Restart the Print Spooler from services.msc which you had stopped in step c.

10. Finally, Re-install the printer software and drivers.

 

Disclaimer: Using Third Party Software, including hardware drivers can cause serious problems that may prevent your computer from booting properly. kate edson say:

'Add Device' is under the Hardware and Sound category.

 

Connect your Wedge Touch Mouse to your computer
  1. On the underside of your Wedge Touch Mouse, press the battery door release button, insert one AA alkaline battery (positive side first), and slide the door shut.
    The BlueTrack Technology light on the underside of the device turns on and flashes.
  2. Press and hold the Power/Connect button on the underside of the device for 3 to 5 seconds.
    A small light on the back of the device blinks green and red alternately. This indicates that the device is discoverable to other Bluetooth devices within 6 minutes.
  3. To connect the device to your computer, open Search, type Control Panel, tap Control Panel from the search results, and then tap Add a device (in the Hardware and Sound section).
  4. When the device is listed, select it, and follow the instructions to complete the connection.

 

You can also open the Settings charm, tap Change PC Settings, select Devices, then Add a device.

 

Hope this helps,

 

Kate
