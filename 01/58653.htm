b) Restart the computer, keep pressing the Boot menu option key to enter the boot menu and change the boot option to CD\DVD rom

d) Click on Troubleshooting, advanced option and then click Automatic repair.

b) Click on Troubleshooting, then Advanced options and click on Command Prompt.

c) In the command prompt type the following command: sfc/scannow and press ENTER.
