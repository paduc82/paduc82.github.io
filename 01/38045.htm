Its equivalent to a retail license and permits 1 installation, its also an upgrade license, which means, you need a base qualifying license such as XP or Vista to use it. If you are replacing your motherboard, the previous qualifying license that came with that motherboard will be invalidated, this means, the upgrade version will not longer have a valid license to use.
