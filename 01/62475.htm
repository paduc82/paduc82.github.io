On the bottom right of your screen click the arrow pointing up, then click customize, on the top of the window that should appear click the "All Control Panel Items" then click Administrative Tools, Find and click "Windows Firewall with Advanced Security" (For me its near the bottom of list.) on the left click on Inbound Rules and find the app you want to allow then RIGHT click it, click properties, and change from Block The connection to Allow The Connection.
