You just need to make a split before and after the unwanted frames in order to break the video into parts.

 

To make a split, position the playback marker at the desired split point.  The quickest way to make a split is to press M on the keyboard, or you can go to the Video Tools | Edit tab and click on the Split icon.  Repeat the process for the end of the unwanted frames.
