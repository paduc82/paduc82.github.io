Call of Duty: Black Ops 2 is a broken game... - Microsoft Community

smbfootball53 say:

Call of Duty: Black Ops 2 is a broken game...

That's not to say that it's a bad game.  The innovations made to the Create-A-Class system, as well as the general gameplay mechanics, are spot-on as always.  However, none of this matters when the spawn system is so obviously broken and the lag compensation measures taken by the developer are ultimately counterproductive.

First, the spawning system.  I have played more than my fair share of Call of Duty, and have been an avid fan of the franchise since the original CoD was released in '03.  That being said, I have NEVER experienced spawns as terrible as those in BO2.  Players are consistently spawned in enemy-occupied areas of the map, or directly in front of one or more enemy players.  This problem becomes more apparent when playing objective-based gametypes (particularly CTF or Domination).

Second, lag compensation, or lag comp.  From what I understand, this software recognizes the ping of every player in the room, and configures every player's connection to match that of the player with the slowest, or worst, ping.  The intended outcome is to eliminate host advantage and smooth the experience over for everyone involved. While it may succeed in the former goal, the lag comp software effectively ruins the experience for almost every player in the room.  Players who are accustomed to playing with a good/decent connection now must adapt to subpar/bad conditions.  The end result in gameplay is the need to shoot at where a player was or is going to be in half a second or so.  In other words, to hit your target, you need to be slightly off-target.

I wouldn't have the first clue how to resolve the spawning issues, but the lag comp issue can easily be resolved with dedicated servers.  Call of Duty is one of very few big-name titles that does not utilize dedicated servers.  Other shooters (Halo 4, Battlefield 3, Gears of War 3) use dedicated servers to provide a stronger and more consistent connection for all players, and have been very successful.  Call of Duty has always been a great and innovative franchise, but if these issues aren't fixed, the inventiveness of future installments will be understandably overlooked and overshadowed by glaring and unjustifiable programming issues such as these.

john rubdy say:

Hi,

 

The error message clearly indicates Windows needs to format the drive that the Windows Recovery environment is currently running on. Since you have formatted the OS partition using Diskpart the recovery drive will include only the recovery tools and a bootable image, but not a recovery image.

 

You need to use the Windows installation disc or a system repair disc, format the drive and then recover using System Image Recovery.

For additional reference you can check this Windows 7 article which is also applicable for Windows 8 image recovery.

 

http://windows.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/restore-computer-from-system-image-backup#1TC=windows-7

 

Warning:  When you restore your computer from a system image, it's a complete restoration. You can't choose individual items to restore, and all of your programs, system settings, and files are replaced with those on the system image.

Hope this helps. If you need further assistance with Windows, let us know and will be glad to help.

- Scroll down and look for Forza horizon 4 from the list and tap on Advanced options.

Method 2: Allow firewall exception.
- Hold down ‘Windows+ R’ keys together on your Keyboard.
- Type in Control and click on Enter.
- Tap on ‘System and Security’. Then, tap on ‘Windows firewall’.
- Press ‘Allow an app or feature through Windows Firewall’ on the left pane.
- You will see a list of several applications, to make changes tap on ‘Change settings.’
- Now, you can select apps on your PC that you want to enable for communication over the Internet without being stopped by Firewall.
- Click on the Private and Public checkbox to allow this communication.
- Click OK and close the Windows Firewall window.
