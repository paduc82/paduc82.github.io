greg carmack - mvp 2010-2020 say:

You wouldn't need Volume Licensing for PC's that have embedded Product Keys.  You'd either use the OS that comes preinstalled or generalize the OS using Sysprep to mass deploy as an image to client PC's that would then self-activate with the embedded key. 

Generalizing, SysPrep and Volume Licensing are not really consumer topics but are all subjects regularly handled at Technet forums where they can get you started or answer those questions.

Be aware that a keyfinder program like Magical Jellybean will not read an embedded OEM BIOS Product Key which requires a specialized tool like ProduKey.  But this key is only used for reference since an embedded Product key should always activate itself. 

 

In addition some factory installs are SLP activated with a mass activation key at factory.  This key will be different than the Product Key on the COA sticker used only for reinstalling the OS, and cannot be used for reinstall.

Go to the "Insert" tab.
In the "Header and Footer" section, click on "Page Number". 
From the drop-down list, choose where you would like the numbers to be placed.

Click on your choice and then choose the style from the expanded list. 
Your pages will now be automatically numbered.
