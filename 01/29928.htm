stevenphx say:

Here's what I did to solve this frustrating problem. I have two disks; an SSD (System drive) and a secondary data drive (D:).

There are two problems with trying to create an image when you have 2 disks.

First, the backup was failing with several error codes, and I learned the reason was my secondary drive had initially had Windows installation on it, and Backup was not happy with that. Even after deleting the system partition on the secondary drive, Backup was still insisting on including it in the backup; and failing.

The second problem is that you can't choose which drive you want to back up. If you have 2, it wants to back up both. The solution: go into Disk Management, and remove the drive letter for the secondary drive. Backup will then only back up the System drive (C:). Restore the drive letter after the backup is done. 

(I backed up the secondary drive separately; just needed to copy the files to the external).
