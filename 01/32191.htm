Hi Master_T,

 

For your information, you cannot sync the files present on your CD/DVD directly to your portable media player. You need to rip the music files so that the tracks are created as files on your computer before you sync them to a portable music player.

You’ve to store the songs from a removable storage device (CD/DVD/USB) on to the hard drive so that you can sync the files to a portable music player.

1. Back up everything you can

6. do not hit repair PC at any time. System restore is not the one you want. if you can find the Image recovery option then you will be able to reinstall windows 7
