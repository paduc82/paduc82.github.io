Is it possible to BCC mail while leaving the TO field empty?

Yes, but it's not really a good idea. Some mail servers won't deliver messages with an empty To: line.

When sending to a distribution list, it's polite to put the addressees in the BCC field so that none of the recipients will see who else has received the message. A useful trick to ensure that the message makes it to its destinations is to put the To... box is left empty, the message will probably show as being sent to Undisclosed recipients, which isn't very helpful to them or to you when you need to find a specific message in your Sent items folder. 

The error code appears to be complaining about the "To" field being empty.

If you ever need help dealing with an error, please post the entire error message. It may actually mean something quite different from what it appears to say.
