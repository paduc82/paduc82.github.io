The issue that you're experiencing has something to do with the Recycle Bin. Since the suggested steps didn't resolve it, and you're still getting the error message on your computer, what you can do next is reset the Recycle Bin. Here's what to do:

  1. Run the Command Prompt as Administrator. To do that, press the Windows key + X on your keyboard, then click Command Prompt (Admin).

  3. Once you already entered the command, press Enter.
