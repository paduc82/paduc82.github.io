How do I reinstall Microsoft Hello?

After a recent software update, the fingerprint sign in for Microsoft Hello has gone missing.  How do I re-install this?

anne ros say:

Hi Rico,

This might be caused by software conflicts. If it happened after the update, try these methods and see if it will work for you:

Method 1: Reset Fingerprint options

  1. Open Settings.
  2. Select Accounts and click Sign-in Options.
  3. Look for Fingerprint options and click the Remove button.
  4. Once done, click Get started and follow the on-screen instructions on how to reset the Fingerprint option.

Method 2: Update drivers

  1. On the search bar, type Device Manager and hit Enter.
  2. Look for the drivers you want to update, in your case look for  Hello, webcam, and fingerprint drivers, then click it to expand.
  3. Right-click or double click on the audio driver's name then select Properties.
  4. Under Driver tab, click the Update Driver button to roll the driver back.
  5. Let it search automatically for updated driver software and wait for Windows to update it.

Keep us in the loop with the results.
