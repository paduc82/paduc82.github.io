james vinyard say:

If all fails and you cannot find the Calendar app anywhere on the tablet, you can open Internet Explorer and go to http://www.onedrive.com and click on the down arrow on the Onedrive title.  It will display the online versions of the Metro UI apps that sync with the tablet and Calendar will be there.  It should be populated with all of your entries since it syncs automatically with the Metro UI app on the START screen.

That will make sure you still have the data.

Now, on the START screen, click on the "Search" icon and type in "Calendar" and see if you can find it in the search list and launch it.

Every now and then, Metro UI apps vanish from Windows 8.  It is unknown why it does that and the only real fix is to double check the Store and open the Charms Bar menu and go to Settings and then App Updates and force it to check updates.  Calendar is a pre-installed Metro UI app and should still be in the system.

If all fails, you may have to perform a RESET.  That would be the 'last ditch' solution.

For now, though, your information should be on the OneDrive cloud storage and syncs with your Microsoft Account.
