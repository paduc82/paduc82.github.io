The issue is not connecting your computers to 2 connections, but making your connections (whether wired or wireless) able to communicate on the same subnet.

The proper configuration for what you want to do is to configure the wireless router as a WAP or replace it with a WAP.  Currently you have a double NAT situation.

- Turn off DHCP in the W Router.
