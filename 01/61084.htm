The Spatial Sound feature in Windows 10 is disabled by default. To enable it, follow these steps:

  1. In the notification area, right-click on the sound icon.
  2. In the context menu, click on Playback devices.
  3. Select your playback device and then click on Properties.
  4. Click on the Spatial sound tab.
  5. Select the Spatial sound format you want to apply.

Based on the details provided on this thread, I recommend that you update your drivers and see if there are any changes. To update your driver, follow these steps: 

  1. On the search bar, type Device Manager. 

  2. Expand the selected driver that you want to update. 

  3. Right-click and choose Update Driver. 

  4. Select Search automatically for updated driver software. 

  5. If Windows cannot find any driver update, download and install the driver from your manufacturer's website. 

For more information regarding how to update your drivers, kindly refer to the article entitled "Update drivers in Windows 10". 

 If your drivers are already updated, access Device Manager > under Action, select scan for hardware changes. 

If the issue persists, check if the issue occur in safe mode. To access safe mode, there are two ways to start your PC in safe mode, first is from the Settings and second is from the sign-in screen. To access safe mode, follow these steps: 

From Settings: 

  1. Go to Settings. 

  2. Choose Update & Security. 

  3. Select Recovery and click on Restart Now. 

  4. In Choose an option screen, select Trouble shoot > Advanced options > Startup Settings > Restart. 

  5. Select 4 or F4 to start your PC in Safe mode and 5 or F5 for safe mode with networking. 

From the sign-in screen: 

  1. Hold the Shift key + Power > Restart. 

  2. In Choose an option screen, select Troubleshoot > Advanced options > Startup Settings > Restart. 

  3. Select 4 or F4 to start your PC in Safe mode and 5 or F5 for safe mode with networking. 

For more information regarding how to access safe mode, refer to the article entitled "Start your PC in safe mode in Windows 10".
