5.    Select Repair all Windows Live programs.

  http://windows.microsoft.com/is-IS/windows-live/essentials-programs-didnt-install-ui

http://windows.microsoft.com/en-US/windows-live/mail-import-backup-restore

    1. Go to Computer.
    2. Click on Local Disk (C:) and then go to Program Files.

http://windows.microsoft.com/en-US/windows-live/essentials-other-programs
