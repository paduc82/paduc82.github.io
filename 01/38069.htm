Another possibility is that Movie Maker is just having troubles previewing the webinar clip.  Webinar clips use a different compression standard since they don't have a lot of changing information.  Also, from your screenshot, it appears that the clip is in neither a 16:9 or 4:3 aspect ratio, so perhaps when Movie Maker resizes it for the preview, it causes the content to look blurry.  This is extremely noticeable when you have text slides where there is a lot of fine detail.
