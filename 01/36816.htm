muralidhar_g say:

Hi Ravinder,

Thank you for writing to Microsoft Community Forums.

I would suggest you to follow the steps mentioned below to create a You Tube Video with an Image and Audio File.

Method 1

Using Windows Movie Maker 10

  1. Download the Windows Movie Maker 10 from Microsoft Store.

    Movie Maker 10 is the app to help you make movies from your photos, video clips, and music. It provides basic features such as video trimming, joining, adding background music and text caption, to more advance like image filter, transition effects.

  2. Install Movie Maker 10.

  3. Start Windows Movie Maker.

  4. Click on Add videos and photos. You'll see this in the Add section of the Home tab.

  5. Browse for the picture you want to use. Locate the picture on your computer that you want to use for the YouTube video. Select it and click Open.

  6. Click the Add music button. Click the musical note part of the button to open the file browser.

  7. Browse for the audio file you want to use. Select the audio file and click the Open button.

  8. Click the Options tab. You'll see this under Music Tools at the top of the window.

  9. Select the End point value and press Ctrl+C. This is the length of the audio file in seconds. You'll be using this value to change the duration of the image.

  10. Click the Edit tab. You'll see this under Video Tools at the top of the window.

  11. Click the Duration field and press Ctrl+V. This will paste the copied song length into the Duration field. You'll need to remove the "s" from the end of the.

  12. Click the Play button to preview the video. You should see your selected image while the audio file plays from start to finish in the background.

  13. Click the File tab.

  14. Highlight Save movie and then click YouTube. You'll need to scroll down the list to find it.

  15. Give the file a name and click Save.

  16. Wait while Movie Maker renders the video. Movie Maker will create your video file, which may take several minutes to complete.

  17. Upload the video to YouTube. Once the video is finished being saved, you can log into your YouTube account and upload the video.

Method 2

Steps to Upload the Video on You Tube

  1. Ensure you’re signed into your You Tube account.

  2. To the left of your username, you'll find a link called "Upload." Click on that.

  3. The page will load with two options. Upload Video and Record from webcam. Choose the preferred option.

  4. A new window will pop up when you click on Upload video button to select a file from your hard drive. Select the video you want to upload and click the "Choose" button.

  5. Once the Video has finished uploading, it will take some time processing on YouTube’s servers before it is ready for viewing online.

Hope it helps.
