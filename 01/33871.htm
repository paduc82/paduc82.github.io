Hi Maugan,

Though you've already connected the wireless card and it's antenna on your gaming PC, there's a possibility that it's still not properly installed that's why it's not showing up on your Network and Internet settings. To verify this, please check the Network adapters category on your Device Manager. Follow these steps:

  1. Right-click the Windows key and select Device Manager.
  2. Expand the Network adapters category and see if your wireless card is there.
  3. If yes, then right-click it and click Properties.
  4. Take note of the Device status and please include the details on your response.

In case your wireless card is not listed under the network adapters category, then please check if an Other devices category appears on your Device Manager. If you have, then expand it and see if it displays your wireless card. If it does, then please right-click the wireless card and select Update Driver Software. Make sure to locate the appropriate driver that you've downloaded on your USB flash drive.
