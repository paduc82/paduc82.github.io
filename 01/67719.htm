From button push to login screen:
45,2/(R)45,3/(R)38,8/(R)38,1/(R)38,1/37,7/(R)37,5/(R)35,7

From button push to icons displayed on screen:
1:59,6/(R)1:51,2/(R)2:36,8/(R)1:43,8/(R)1:31,6/2:27,8/(R)2:25,9/(R)2:29,7
A similar story here, arguably sometimes it helps but cannot be relied on.

From button push to HDD LED going off:
7:41,7/(R)over 15min/((R)over 15min/(R)14:55,7/(R)6:48,9/8:10,9/(R)over 15min/(R)over 15min
Didnt expect this, but on first look massive slowdown with ReadyBoost. But when inspecting this I found the laptop to be responsive without any issues. Possibly the SuperFetch was doing some ReadyBoost related caching?

Chrome from icon doubleclick to taskbar idling:
20/(R)5,8/(R)4,6/(R)3,7/(R)3,8/21,6/(R)3,9/(R)3,5

(R)17,6/(R)15,4/18,8/(R)16,4/(R)15,5
