    The "Last Activity" is the time when you have last had any interaction with this user on the particular device, and therefore it will be different on different devices.

     

    The "Last Activity" can e.g. be triggered by just typing anything in the text box of the contact, even without sending the message. This has nothing to do with contact's connection status or what this contact is doing. Only your actions count.

     

    In this example, the contact was Offline for several hours, but few minutes ago I have typed and then cancelled a message to this contact. The "Last Activity" time is set to this time when I did this action.
