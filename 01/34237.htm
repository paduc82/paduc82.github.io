To make and receive phone calls using Skype for Business, we need dedicated plans and licenses. Generally, we can achieve it if we have one of the two conditions below:

If the requirements above are met, you have existing phone numbers and want to use them other than ones which are assigned from Office 365, you can refer to this article: Transfer phone numbers over to Skype for Business Online.

How can I change Win10 "Battery % remaining" to be "Battery time remaining"?

falco4345 say:

after adding this key:

[HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SYSTEM\CurrentControlSet\Control\Power]
"EnergyEstimationDisabled"=dword:00000000

and deleting the key (if existing)

"UserBatteryDischargeEstimator"=dword:ffffffff

the missing remaining time estimation is metioned additionally to the percentage!
