How do I rotate only half of a movie in movie maker? - Microsoft Community

jacquieblue say:

How do I rotate only half of a movie in movie maker?

I have not selected (select all) but the entire video rotates anyway.
considerate_guy say:

Any effect applies to the complete clip. This includes rotation, brightness, black & white, etc.

If you want to apply an effect to only *part* of a clip, you need to split it.

Double-click on the clip in question. That will open up the Video Tools | Edit menu. Now put your playback marker at the point where you want to change the rotation. Click the Split button. Go to the end of that section and make another Split. The result should be that you have *3* clips on your storyboard/project area.

Select the middle clip and apply the rotation only to that part.

As a shortcut to using the Split button, you can press M on the keyboard. That's usually how I make my splits and it is much quicker. Just play the clip until you want to split it, press M. Repeat as needed.
