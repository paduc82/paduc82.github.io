steeledwand7664 say:

The thing that determines if an account is a child account is the Date of Birth (DOB) that was put onto an account when it was ORIGINALLY setup. 

If that DOB is still under 18 by law it has to have an adult account attached to it in the family.

You can actually have multiple adult accounts in a family so just because there is already an adult account in your family is not what is holding you up it is the DOB when the account was originally setup.

Changing the DOB on the account will not affect the DOB for the Xbox profile once an account is setup the DOB thats put in is the DOB that the Xbox profile sees and it is set in stone there is no way to change that.
