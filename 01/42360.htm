a. user say:

It depends on whether it is an OEM or Retail Licence for Windows.

An OEM Licence for Windows is tied to the original computer it was installed on, for the life of that computer.

When that computer dies, the Licence dies with it.

If Retail Licence, you may transfer it to another computer.

BUT, it can only be installed on one(1) computer at a time.

Even if it Retail and you move the Licence, you will have to do a Phone Activation, as you will probably get a message saying "Product Key already in use", which it is by the older computer, where it is now installed..

And you will have to format the old Drive.
