There are a few fixes out there that programmers have come up with, but a lot of them won't work for home users, as the permissions needed to implement them are restricted from the home (non-professional) version of Windows 10. Even if you run it as an admin.

    Keep using your wireless connection until Microsoft rolls out a fix, even though it's slower than a wired connection. If you want to use your wired connection for a little while (gaming or a large download or something) you can roll back your system to a restore point before your computer added the newest Windows 10 update -- try an automatic save point sometime at the beginning of February 2016 -- but just be aware that Windows will download the update and apply it again within a few hours.
