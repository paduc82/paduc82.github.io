¡firedog say:
These bounce messages have nothing to do with receipts. RCPT is cryptic SMTP shorthand for Recipient, and the command RCPT TO precedes an email address that the message is addressed to. Error 550 is pretty self-evident: the address given doesn't exist at that domain. Error 542 is more unusual; it means Bad connection, but that could hide all sorts of different underlying causes. Do you get the same bounce message consistently with repeated attempts? In any case, you should contact the addressees by other means to alert them to the fact that you can't get through.
