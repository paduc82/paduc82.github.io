I found for my issue and hope it could help anybody:

The reason why I got this issue was the fact I tried to forward the "WindowsApps" folder from "C:\Program Files\" to another disk (because I don't have a lot of spaces on C: drive), and I did something wrong during manual deletion of the old application.

  • HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\Appx\AppxAllUserStore\Applications\<app>
  • HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\Appx\AppxAllUserStore\DownlevelInstalled\<session ID>\<app>
  • HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\Appx\AppxAllUserStore\<session ID>\<app>

After that, I reinstalled the app from Windows Store. Then, the launching was still broken. And I tried to repair via Windows Store and it worked.
