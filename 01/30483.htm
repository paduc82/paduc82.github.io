mark bu say:

Hello Mogojohn,

These are two separate accounts. One is your Microsoft Account that uses your Gmail address and is required to open the Mail app. The other is your Gmail account that has been setup in the Mail app to access your email. Both accounts are listed in the Mail app and use the same login ID (the Gmail email address), and may be setup with the same password. For added security it’s recommended that you use two separate passwords.

 

If both accounts are listed in the Mail app folder list, you can remove one from that view by going to Charms -> Settings -> Accounts -> <account> (the account you do not want to not be listed) and uncheck the box to sync mail for that account.

 

 

Note that removing the Microsoft account will remove all of your accounts from Mail, Calendar, Messaging, and People.
