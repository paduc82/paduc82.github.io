hairyfool say:

The switching between headphones and directly attached amplified speakers on many motherboards is an entirely mechanical process, at best it can sense that a plug has been inserted and ask you to confirm that a device has been plugged in and what it is.

 

In your case you default output device if is the SPDIF optical/co-axial digital output, not the green speaker output on the back panel. When the headphones are plugged in the switch in the socket is detecting  the connection and the software is turning off SPDIF and enabling the speaker output which is redirected to the dedicated headphone socket.

 

So the question is do you have a digital amplifier such as a home theatre system attached or analogue amplified speakers?
