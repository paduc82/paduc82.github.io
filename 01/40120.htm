Receiving Skype calls when not logged in

Hi,

I have two Skype accounts, one I use for my business and one private. On my iPhone I usually use only my private account, but sometimes I need to us my business account. Anyway, when logged into my private account, I still receive calls from my business account. Why does this happen and how to avoid this?

Regards

C

jomel_g say:

A call from an account that is not signed in will not go through unless "Call forwarding" is active for that account. When Call Forwarding is active on your account, you will receive the call from your Skype account or Skype Number (if you have) to the phone number you set up on your Call Forwarding page.

If you're not sure if Call Forwarding feature is enabled on your account, you can follow the steps on this support article: How do I use Skype call forwarding?
