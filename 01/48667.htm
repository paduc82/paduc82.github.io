Resolution on a video in Windows movie maker - Microsoft Community

deborahmcphail say:

Resolution on a video in Windows movie maker

I have been trying to save a movie but, it keeps telling me:  not enough memory to lower the resolution.  How do you lower the resolution?  I have looked, looked and looked?

considerate_guy say:

Most likely this is just an erroneous message telling you that Movie Maker is unable to save your movie at all. You can try saving in a lower resolution, but that may not help.

To do that, when you choose File > Save movie look through the list of output options. One of those is For email which is probably the lowest resolution you can output. Notice if you hover over an option it will show you the dimensions (e.g. 426 x 240 for email).

But as I said, that may not even help you out. If you are still having problem saving, I would go back and check your source files. For example, if the video files aren't 100% compatible with Movie Maker, you can convert them to .wmv format instead. And if you are having problems with the audio files, convert them to .wav or .wma.
