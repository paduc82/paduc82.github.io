jcfarnham1961 say:

Recently resolved an BT issue with my SP4, so documenting here in case it can help others.  Symptoms: Could not reconnect SP Pen, and would not pair with other devices.  On further inspection, found that the BT Toggle on the Settings/Devices, BT, Printers, Mouse window was missing, and BT not listed in Device Manager.  Problem due to UEFI settings.  Steps: At Start menu, type 'Recovery' and look for 'Recovery Options' (its a Settings window).  Recover Options screen has two primary selections (Reset this PC, and Advanced startup).  Select Advanced Startup and click Restart.  When PC restarts, at Choose an option screen, select Troubleshoot. At Troubleshoot screen, select Advanced options. At Advanced options, select UEFI Firmware Settings.  At EUFI Firmware Settings screen, click Restart. PC restarts with Surface UEFI screen displayed.  Select Devices option, you should see a list of devices, and 2nd from bottom is Bluetooth.  Mine was set to off and toggling on and rebooting solved my problem.

I did not deliberately turn off BT UEFI setting.  My suspicion (all speculation) is that I turned BT off in normal settings window (forlorn effort to save battery), and a subsequent Update turned the BT UEFI setting off.
