markusabel say:

So....

Since nobody has given an answer yet (the link for xbox troubleshooting doesnt even mention in-game settings), let me try to explain whats happening. Windows Sonic as well as Dolby Atmos for Headphones downmix a surround signal to stereo in a way that makes it seem as if you're listening to surround sound. If you set your Game audio to Headphones the Game will not output 7.1 Surround  sound but either pure Stereo or already have done the work that sonic is supposed to do. So, if your game has built in surround sound (or binaural sound) for headphones turn off sonic and use that or use the speaker settings and turn on sonic. Dont turn on both or sonic will try to make a surround sound out of a binaural stereo signal. Trust me, that's not a good listening experience.
