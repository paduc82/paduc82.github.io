There is a cleartype tunner in Windows 7 that may improve the display quality on LCD.

Aero uses Direct3D which draws faster on your high-end graphics card than the GDI 2D engine used by the basic theme. Some fonts are designed for CRT/GDI and not for LCD/Direct3D drawing, causing blur images when used with LCD/Direct3D.  

Also some applications are not Hi-DPI aware. Your resolution looks like a Hi-DPI one, so you may want to check the program's "Disable display scaling on high DPI settings" on shortcut's compatibility tab too.
