aakkam22 say:

If you use the DVD, then your only method is to upgrade starting from the PC.

This means that you'll have to pop in the DVD you created, and perform an upgrade from within your currently running Windows. Once you get to Windows 10, the product key will be considered a Windows 10 product key and you can do as many clean installations as you'd like. It makes no difference that you upgraded to 8.1 from the Windows Store.

Any other method will invalidate your activation status.
