arminatorx say:

Is your console enrolled in the Xbox Insider Preview program? If so, they are investigating an issue where some consoles completely turn off instead of going to instant on mode (causing you to see the green screen during startup). 

Also, during that green screen, the console does some sort of self check. It's comparable to the boot process of your PC after the PC was shut down unexpectedly (for example because of a power outage). If the PC wasn't shut down properly, the PC sometimes does an integrity check of the C: drive with the operating system, which can take some time. 

The console does the same, and if the console does that check during startup, the green screen can stay for up to 10 minutes and might cause a restart to finish the recovery process. 

However, that should only happen once in a long while, and not every time you start the console. 

If the console does have issues starting a lot, you might try to resolve that by resetting the console to factory defaults.
