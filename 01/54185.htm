If the Power Options settings have been configured to reduce brightness when the laptop is plugged in, you will experience this issue.

To rectify the problem, please be guided by the steps below:

  1. From the desktop screen, right-click the Start button.
  2. Click Power Options from the menu.
  3. From the right pane of the Power Options window, click the Change plan settings link representing the power plan that is currently selected. Normally the Balanced (recommended) power plan is selected by default.
  4. Once the next window opens up, move the Adjust plan brightness slider under the Plugged in column to the right to increase the brightness. If you are unsure about which settings would be best for your laptop, click the Restore default settings for this plan link.
  5. Click Save changes. The Save changes button would remain disabled if you have restored the power plan settings to default.
