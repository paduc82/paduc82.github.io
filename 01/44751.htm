kr_metal say:

Sharing Themes Online

How can you share your windows 7 themes online?

_null____ say: kr_metal,

To share your Windows 7 themes online, follow these steps:

To save your themes for sharing:
1. Right click on the desktop and then click on Personalize.
2. Select a Windows 7 theme to share, then right click on it and choose "Save theme for sharing".
3. Choose a location to save the theme to, type in the file name if it has not already been populated, and then click on Save.

To share your themes via Windows Live SkyDrive:
1. Open Internet Explorer by clicking on Start, All Programs, and then click Internet Explorer.
2. In the address bar, type http://skydrive.live.com and then press Enter. 
3. You will need to sign in with a Windows Live ID to continue.
4. Once signed in, click on Add Files.
5. To share with the public, click on the Public folder.
6. Click on "Select files from your computer". Then, locate the theme file, and then click on "Open".
7. Click on the Upload button to begin the upload process.
8. Once the file has been uploaded you will see the file on the page, click on it and then you will see "Web address", which you can copy and paste to others.

To share your themes via e-mail:
1. Open your e-mail program (Windows Live Mail, Microsoft Outlook, etc).
2. Create a new e-mail message and type the address of the receipient in the "To" field.
3. Attach the theme file to the e-mail message.
4. Type a quick message to your friend letting them know that this is a theme that you would like to share with them.
5. Click on Send.

To share your themes via Windows Live Messenger:
1. Open a conversation with a contact.
2. Click on Files and then click on "Send a file or photo..."
3. Locate the theme file and click on Open.
4. Wait for your contact to accept the file transfer.

For more information on creating, customizing, and sharing themes please see http://windows.microsoft.com/en-US/windows7/Using-themes.

Hope that helps, have a great day! :)
