Hello,

unfortunately, this scenario won't be possible.

You can (on Skype):

  • have Skype Number to accept calls on your skype when people call that number from their phones (or you can also forward these calls to your russian number, but you will need Skype Credit or subscription to russian numbers)
  • make calls and send SMS from Skype with Skype Credit (and for calling with appropriate subscription)
