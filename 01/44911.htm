7-7-2011 Update:

RESOLVED!

Updating this - Working with our System Admins - we figured out the there is "was" a policy being applied that redirected the user's "My Documents" folder to a mapping that looked like this: \\server\share\users$\%username% (note the $ ) -- Turns out Windows 7 doesn't like that $ as a symbol in a path statement whereas XP had no issue with it. They changed the policy to redirect to a "mapped" drive which points to \\server\share\users$\%username%   mapping like this: Z:\My Documents. Once the policy was changed, logging off and back on MSIs install just fine.

Situation resolved.
