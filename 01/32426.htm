Safe mode Win 10 - Microsoft Community

markusschild say:

Safe mode Win 10

How do I start up Windows 10 in safe mode?

vijay a. verma say:
There are two versions of safe mode: Safe Mode and Safe Mode with Networking. They're quite similar, but Safe Mode with Networking includes the network drivers and services you'll need to access the Internet and other computers on your network.

Option 1: From Settings
Windows Key+X > Settings
Update & Security > Recovery
Under Advanced startup, select Restart now
After your PC restarts to the Choose an option screen, select Troubleshoot > Advanced options > Startup Settings > Restart
After your PC restarts, you'll see a list of options. Select 4 or F4 to start your PC in Safe Mode. Or if you'll need to use the Internet, select 5 or F5 for Safe Mode with Networking

Option 2: From Sign-in Screen
Restart your Computer
When you get to the sign-in screen, hold the Shift key down while you select Power icon > Restart
After your PC restarts to the Choose an option screen, select Troubleshoot > Advanced options > Startup Settings > Restart
After your PC restarts, you'll see a list of options. Select 4 or F4 to start your PC in Safe Mode. Or if you'll need to use the Internet, select 5 or F5 for Safe Mode with Networking

Option 3: Power ON and OFF
Power ON your computer and as Windows / manufacturer's Logo appears, Power OFF - Repeat 3 times
Now your computer will go into Advanced Recovery Environment
Advanced Options > Troubleshoot > Advanced Options > Startup Settings > Restart
Press 4 or F4 to enable Safe Mode (5 or F5 to enable Safe Mode with Networking)
Log in once Windows starts in safe mode

Option 3: Use the System Configuration tool (msconfig.exe)
Windows Key+Recovery
Type msconfig.exe > Enter
Under Boot tab > Select Safe boot and select minimal > OK > Confirm the Restart when it asks
Note - You don't get Safe mode with Networking in this case
Now your computer will go into Advanced Recovery Environment
Advanced Options > Troubleshoot > Advanced Options
Click Startup Settings > Restart
Press (4) to enable Safe Mode
Log in once Windows starts in safe mode

Start your PC, just as Windows attempts to load (spinning dots), press and hold Power Button for 5 - 10 seconds to perform a Hard Shut Down
