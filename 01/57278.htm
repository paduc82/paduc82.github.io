There are two types of backup:

A file and folder backup is a copy of your important files and folders: documents, music, pictures, videos, bookmarks, email and anything else that's important to you. This can be as simple as copying from your hard drive to another place (an external drive or 'the cloud.') However, backup software makes it so convenient.

A disk image backup is an exact copy of your entire disk, compressed into a single file. This is how you backup Windows and all your software. When you restore a disk image, your computer goes back in time to exactly the way it was when you made the backup. And it only takes minutes to make or restore an image backup.
