Hi,

I would suggest you to try adjusting the resolution to 1280x768 and check if it helps.

Follow these steps:

1.     Open Screen Resolution by clicking theStart button, clicking Control Panel, and then, underAppearance and Personalization, clicking Adjust screen resolution.

2.     Click the drop-down list next toResolution, move the slider to the resolution (1280x768), and then clickApply.

3.     ClickKeep to use the new resolution, or click Revert to go back to the previous resolution.
