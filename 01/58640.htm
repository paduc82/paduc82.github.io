Can't skip ahead when watching some recorded TV programs (Media Center)

Hi there,

I have Windows 7 Professional installed with all updates and am using Media Center to record TV from two capture cards (a Hauppauge PVR150 and a Hauppauge PVR250, if that matters).  I am finding that certain recorded TV programs will not allow me to use the skip ahead button (30 second skip) to skip over commercials.  I am starting to track it and see if it only happens with certain channels or programs but am wondering why this would happen.  Is this a bug, or a new "feature" in Windows 7 Media Center that can be enabled by "content providers"?  Has anyone else run into this?

ETA:  When I attempt to skip ahead, the timeline on the bottom of the screen is as follows:  current position is shown as being at the end of the recording, total length of the recording on the right side is shown as 0:00, and current "time position" is shown correctly.  Pressing fast forward instead of skip, MCE does not respond.  Have tried restarting Media Center and restarting the computer, neither seems to make a difference, so it seems like it's a problem with the recording.

Milo
milobloom-ab say: Hi all.. I seem to have this issue resolved.  Disk checks came up with no problems, video drivers made no difference.  The resolution in the end was to replace one of my tuner cards - I replaced the Hauppauge PVR250 with a Hauppauge HVR1800.  The issue with not being able to skip ahead in certain recordings has now gone away.  (In doing a bit of reading, the PVR250 is certified for Windows 7, but not certified as compatible with Windows 7 Media Center.)  Thanks for all your suggestions.
