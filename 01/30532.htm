Hi,

The total download size for the v.1703 installation will be around 3.5GB of data. As far as being safe and seamless? Being that you have been notified the devices hardware configuration has been deemed compatible. As far as loaded software and device drivers? Best practice before upgrading is to do a system disc cleanup, uninstall any programs or apps that you do not wish to carry forward (if any), disconnect all peripherals but the keyboard and mouse (if you're using wireless substitute wired), and disconnect or disable in BIOS any secondary internal hard drives, then restart Windows, let it rest for 15 minutes or so and then begin the update/upgrade.

Depending on your download speeds this will take about an hour and a half, or perhaps longer. There will be several restarts and perhaps some periods of a black screen during the process. (If things hang for a long time, say an hour, at 99% or close to it, disconnect completely from the internet and then manually update after it has completed.) After it has completed and you have signed in, let it rest for a half hour then restart one more time then check for updates again.

Note: these are my recommendations and mine alone, take them as you will.
