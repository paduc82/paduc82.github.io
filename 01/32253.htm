manuelcapiro say:
Hello SYCLAR,

Disabling Automatic Connections in Windows 10
Go to the Action Center icon and choose All Settings.
Select Network & Internet.
Click Wi-Fi.
Select Change Adapter Options on the right panel under Related Settings to open the Network Connections dialog.
Double-click on the relevant Wi-Fi connection to open the Wi-Fi Status dialog.
Click the Wireless Properties button below the General tab to open the Wireless Network Properties dialog.
Uncheck the entry Connect Automatically When This Network is in Range under the Connection tab.

Hope it helps, have a good day

I have found a solution that works.

From an administrator command prompt enter this:

netsh wlan add filter permission=block ssid="BTWifi-with-FON" networktype=infrastructure

replacing "BTWifi-with-FON"  with whatever network you don't want to connect to.
