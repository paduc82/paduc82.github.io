You need to start your own thread. Although in this case it is not marked answered it is fairly old and each thread can only be deemed answered once so multiple issues cannot be managed.

OneNote is a program in its own right and a part of the Office suite. It does cause confusion because it has its own printer driver in order to upload any document into a OneNote Project. If someone is Spoofing emails that appear to be from you then they probably never
had access to your email account. Instead they either have a copy of your contacts
