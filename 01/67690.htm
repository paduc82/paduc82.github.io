Open Explorer to the folder and tap Ctrl+A to select all. Hold down one of the Shift keys and right-click your selection then choose Copy as Path. Open Excel to a blank worksheet and paste.

If you need to remove the drive and folder path from the file names, use Ctrl+H (e.g. Find & Replace).
