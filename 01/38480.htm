considerate_guy say:

Unfortunately not in the latest version of WLMM, at least not directly.  The only way to accomplish this in WLMM would be to find a way to extract the audio from your video and use it as your "added music" track.  You would take the first 5 seconds of this, use it as the "music" with the 5 second photo.  You'd also then have to manually trim out 5 seconds from the original video so it picks up where the "music" stops.

In the original Movie Maker (e.g. WMM 2.x or WMM 6.0) you could directly drag a video down to the added audio/music track and then mute the original. You then replace 5 seconds of video with a picture (or perhaps the end of another clip).  WLMM will not let you use video files as your added audio/music however thus the extra step to convert to an audio file.
