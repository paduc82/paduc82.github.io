  2. Select Device Manager.
  3. Click the drop-down arrow for the audio driver you want to restore.
  4. Right-click on the audio driver and select Properties.
  5. Under the Driver tab, select Roll Back Driver.
  6. Select OK.

Once you're done, restart your device and check if your previous driver will reappear.
