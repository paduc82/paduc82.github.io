This is a pretty old thread, but here is a work around:
 

1. Open LifeCam software.

2. Choose the Settings tab on the right of the viewing pane (Gear wheel icon)

2. If the Settings tab area is not showing, tap/click the arrow on the right-hand border of the window viewing pane.

3. Turn off "True-color"

4. Choose "Properties, then the "Camera Control" tab.

5. Uncheck "Auto focus" next to the "Focus" slider.

6. Adjust focus to a value that works for you. And "Apply" then "OK".

7. OPTIONAL: Turn "True-color" back on (optional) (if you turn on "True-color", autofocus will reactivate, but you should be OK for that session (the focus will not go in and out). If you close and restart the program, you'll need to turn off auto focus again.

 

Cheers,

PaulC
