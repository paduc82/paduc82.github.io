"Does the absence of AMD Catalyst  Control Center influences the Gameplay in any way?"

Not necessarily.  The main issues of not having CCC available is not being able to alter settings

outside the games graphics options yourself, but most 'modern' games don't really need the

options in CCC anyway. 

Older games do often benefit from being able to set Anisotropic Filtering and Anti-Aliasing in

Control Panel though.

- You also cannot access various video setting, such as colour options.

- The AMD CAP files are supposed to provide automated optimal settings for games as well.

I believe they can be downloaded separately from the drivers, but as CAP is a relatively recent

'feature' there may not be any optimisations for older games.
