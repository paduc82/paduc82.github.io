Out of date Printer and Windows 10 - Microsoft Community

circe138 say:

Out of date Printer and Windows 10

I have a Konica Minolta qms 2300dl (about 12 yrs old) - which I have managed to keep going through all windows updates. With win 10 I thought I would finally be defeated. But, I have a work around that others with old printers might find useful.

1) Search and download a printer driver that works on Vista or more recent versions of windows.  (you may have to use a different driver to your original...I am using a 2430 dl driver).

2) Connect printer to a router and check manual for network set-up

3) Go to devices and printers, click on add printer and ask win 10 to discover your printer

4) Interrupt search and click "the printer that I wanted wasn't listed"

5) Click on "add a local printer or network printer"

6) Click on "create a new port"

7) Select from "type of new port" LPR

8) On pop up menu add the address of your router - often 192.168.1.1

9) In printer queue box type "print" in lower case without quotation marks

It should be straightforward after that.

Obviously these problems are a mix of hardware issues, driver issues, pc/laptop issues and win 10!  I am not a pc/windows expert - but somehow this worked. Hope it helps.
