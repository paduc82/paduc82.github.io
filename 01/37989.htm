aileen alf say:

Hi CapeLee,

If you were using some older Windows version and have installed Windows Fall Creators Update, your system will automatically use the sign-in information to make the device ready for the new update. This is the reason why you see the login screen once more even after entering the login credentials.

To disable this feature, go to Settings > Accounts > Sign-in options. On your right-hand side, you should find a Privacy category. By default, the Use my sign-in info to automatically finish settings up my device after an update or restart option is enabled. You should toggle the button to the Off position disable this functionality.
