rain_a say:

Hi Amandastorer,

Greetings from Skype!

Based on the issue you have posted, it seems that you are logged in to a different Skype account. Just a heads up, prior to Skype being part of Microsoft services, customers can register a customized Skype username and sign in using the email address they've associated the account with. But now that Skype has been integrated as a Microsoft service, when a customer logs in to Skype using the email address (which is not yet associated to any old Skype account) instead of the Skype ID, this automatically creates a new Skype Account.

So if you want to sign in to Skype using your old account, make sure to log out the current account that is signed in to Skype and manually sign back in using your old Skype user name.

To sign out on Skype:

1. Click/Tap your avatar at the upper left part of the Skype page.

2. Scroll down and look for Sign out and forgot this account/user name.

Let me know if this works.
