hairyfool say:

They are automatically installed as part of the download. All programs provided via Windows Store are App format and will be in the Modern UI start screen. Since the introduction of 8.1 they are no longer added to the user menu but will be in the "All Apps" Menu.


In the Start Menu move your mouse to just below the lowest left tile and an circled arrow will show. Click on that to show all installed Apps.


Your Download will have a "New" flag on it. Right click and Pin to start menu to promote it to the Start Menu.
