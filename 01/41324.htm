xbf amber m say:

Hi Richard!

Thanks for being persistent and letting us know that you are still having an issue. There is a way to view the list of games you own (but haven't necessarily played) based on Achievements.

  1. Sign in at xbox.com.
  2. Select your profile icon in the upper-right corner, and then select Profile.
  3. Tap or select Achievements.
  4. From the Gamerscore Leaderboard, select your GamerTag.
  5. Tap or select Achievements again.
  6. From the list that appears, you may select to see All, PC, Windows Phone, Xbox One or Xbox 360.

The list will contain games you have played, as well as those you haven't as yet had the chance to enjoy.

Hope this is helpful! And, of course, we would love to hear if this has been helpful!
