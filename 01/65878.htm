3GB is the amount of RAM you have; it has nothing to do with its speed.

The speed the RAM runs at is set by the motherboard. When you buy RAM, each stick comes with a particular speed, but that is actually *not* its speed; it's the speed it's certified to run at  without a problem. If you install RAM with a speed lower than what the motherboard will run it at, you're looking for trouble.
