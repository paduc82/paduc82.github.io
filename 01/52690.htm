I believe your issue has been solved lately, but for others who might have the same problem and stumble upon this page:

Avast and AVG Antivirus (/Internet Security) seem to conflict with Solid Edge on Windows 10 1903. Removing these programs seem to instantly fix the problem. Also, if you are running a different AV than AVG or Avast, and removal of that software fixed your issue, then please let your Solid Edge reseller know. They will pass it on to Siemens. It helps troubleshooting. And: check regularly for the latest Solid Edge Maintenance Pack.
