pettyofficer735 say: Xbox 360 Content License Restoration What is the license transfer tool?

The license transfer tool on Xbox.com lets you consolidate all of your downloaded content licenses to one console. For example, if you downloaded items from Xbox LIVE¬Æ Marketplace on one console, then purchased another console later on and downloaded more content to the new one, your content would be licensed to two consoles. This tool lets you download your older content licenses to your new console.

Why did Xbox create this tool?

We are always looking at ways to improve the Xbox 360¬Æ and Xbox LIVE experience. With the success of Xbox LIVE Marketplace, the need for a tool to consolidate content licenses became apparent.  

My console was recently repaired; do I need to use the tool?

In most cases, no, you do not need to use the tool. Licenses are transferred automatically during the repair process but you may need to download them again. In a small number of cases, the transfer may not complete correctly during repair and you might need to use the transfer tool to move your licenses. If, after redownloading the content, you are still having an issue, then using the transfer tool is the right step.

How often can I use the tool?

The tool can only be used once every 121 days.

Why can I use the tool only once per 121 days?

The tool is intended to allow consumers to consolidate their licenses they purchased on multiple consoles to a primary console. It is not intended to continually move content between multiple consoles.

Why can't I move licenses to multiple consoles?

Most customers have a single console. However, they may have downloaded items to multiple consoles over time. We want to provide a way for these customers to consolidate their licenses on their current console. You can still use your content on other consoles while signed in to Xbox LIVE.

What licenses are NOT transferred by the tool?

Due to licensing restrictions, movie rental licenses are not transferred. Make sure you watch your movies before using this tool.

I've finished the process on xbox.com, now what do I need to do?

You need to download the licenses to your new console.

  • Attach a hard drive or memory unit.
  • Turn on the console.
  • Sign in to Xbox LIVE with the gamer profile that originally downloaded the content.
  • Press the silver Guide button on your controller to open the Xbox Guide.
  • Go to Settings and select Account Management.
  • Then select Download History.
  • Repeat step 5 for each item in your download history.
What happens to my content on the original console?

The content is no longer licensed to the old console. You can delete it, or you can use it while signed in to Xbox LIVE.
