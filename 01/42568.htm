Change a slide in a video - Microsoft Community

miliza say:

Change a slide in a video

I have a video that was made from powerpoint slides on which they superimposed an audio file for narration. I have Windows Live movie maker as an editor. Is there a way I can replace one of the slides with a new one and keep the audio and the rest of the film intact?
considerate_guy say:

This would be hard to accomplish in Windows LIve Movie Maker.  Technically it could be done, but would require using another program to extract the audio (for example, you could extract the audio track using Freemake Video Converter and having it convert to .mp3 format).  If you did that, you could then put the original clip into WLMM, mute its volume (Video Tools tab, volume set to the far left).  Add the extracted audio (.mp3) as the "music".  Now split out the picture that you want to replace, delete it and put a corresponding picture in its place. Be sure you keep the duration the same as the one you removed so the sound is still in synch.

Another option is to use the prior version of Movie Maker (Windows Movie Maker 6.0).  If you were to install that version, you could then do it all inside of Windows Movie Maker.  You would put a copy of your video on the timeline, then put a second copy onto the "music/audio" track.  When you do this, WMM knows you just want the audio.  Now mute the original audio.  Again, split out the slide you don't want and put another in its place.

a. user say:

Hi,

Another way to do this is by splitting the video using Windows Live Movie Maker 2011. Here are the detailed steps:

1. Make a copy of your original video. We will use this copy to convert the video to mp3 format. You can use the any third-party software to do this. Try to search Bing using this tag, "how to convert wmv to mp3" and it will yield some good results.

2. Once you've got a copy of audio from your original video, launch Windows Live Movie Maker.

3. Add your original video using Add videos and photos option.

4. Click Edit under Video Tools.

5. Drag the slider in the preview board to split the video into three parts:

       a) First part: Beginning of the video to the start point of the part you've planned to remove. Click Split or press M.

       b) Second part: Start point to the end point of the part you've planned to remove. Click Split or press M.

        c) Third part: End point of the part you've planned to remove to the end of the video. Click Split or press M.

6. Delete the second part and add the new slide using Add videos or photos. You can drag the new slide in between the videos.

7. In the same tab (Edit tab), click Video volume and mute the sound.

8. Switch to Home tab then click Add music to add the audio you made using step 1. Press Spacebar to test your project.

9. Click File (blue button in the upper left)

10. Select Save movie.

11. Choose Recommended for this project or choose your preferred settings.

12. Save the project.

Please inform us if you have further concerns or questions.
