Registry Cleaners

I and many experts in these forums will tell you that registry cleaners generally do more harm than good. They often claim to fix registry "errors" which simply don't matter. In trying to fix these unimportant "errors" they can often wreak havoc on the PC causing it to either not work properly or at all.
