Hi Chris and Dan,

 

As you guys have mentioned, there is no option to disconnect a wired Ethernet connection from the task bar or from the charms bar in Windows 8. The only work around is to disconnect the Ethernet connection from the network and sharing center. I understand that this could be quite annoying to do every time, so you can create a shortcut in your desktop for easy access. Refer to these steps:

 

a)      Right click on the network connection icon in the task bar.

b)      Click on ‚ÄúOpen Network and sharing center‚Äù.

c)       Click on ‚ÄúChange adapter settings‚Äù in the left pane.

d)      In the network connection window, right click on the Ethernet connection icon and select ‚Äúcreate a shortcut‚Äù to create a shortcut in the desktop.

e)      Now, you can double click on the desktop shortcut and click on the disable button to disable the Ethernet connection.

f)       When you need to connect it back, just double click on the desktop shortcut and it will get connected automatically.
