    Xbox one starts on its own

    My xbox sometimes start all of a sudden on its own ? How can that happen ?  Why is it happing ?  


    I don't have standby activated. 

    internet expert say:

    Is your Xbox connected to a surge protector or a power strip? If so, that is usually the most common reason for the console randomly turning on. Connect the Xbox power directly to the wall outlet. 


    The Xbox One has its own surge protection built into the power supply, and connecting it behind another power strip or surge protector can cause electrical "hiccups" that cause it to start on its own.
