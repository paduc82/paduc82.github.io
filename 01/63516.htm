To anybody having this issue, the fix to my specific problem was in BIOS. THE C6 State option needs to be disabled. Now this worked for me, it may not work for others. I believe my issue to be power related, as I'm using a 430W with a 6-core power hungry processor, along with an AMD R7 240 2GB and 5 fans.
