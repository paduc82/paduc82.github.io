keith a. miller say:

I think "this is by design" as they say.  A file won't appear in the Recent list if its ProgID in the Registry has the EditFlag FTA_NoRecentDocs set.

https://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/windows/desktop/bb762506(v=vs.85).aspx

So, if you use Media Player, then in the registry, .mp3 points to WMP11.AssocFile.MP3

which has EditFlags in binary format as 00 00 31 00.  to clear NoRecentDocs, you would change it to 00 00 21 00.
