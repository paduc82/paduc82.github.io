Windows Media Center scans only the first tuner of a kind (DVB-T) and incorrectly uses that tuner's tuning details on any other tuner of the same type. Have a virtual DVB-T tuner (as used by several DVB-C manufacturers with WMC) (or multiple antennas pointed at different stations) and hilarity ensues.
