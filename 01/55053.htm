    techfreak_ say:

    All subscriptions attempt to bill 3 days in advance of the end of the subscription. It renews based off when you purchased it, not at the beginning the month. Purchasing multiples of the same subscription will not provide you with additional minutes, only bill you immediately for a future month. 

    I do not know exactly when you purchased your subscription, this is only an example:

    Example:  If you purchased your subscription on Aug 5th at 10:47, your subscription renewal would attempt to bill you on Sep 2nd and release your new minutes on Sep 5th at 10:47. This is how it would work every month. Of course if there are billing issues, it would periodically attempt to bill you from Sept 2nd to the 5th. If you attempt to check your exhausted minute balance any time before Sep 5th at 10:47, it would still be zero and you would not be able to place calls. After that time, you would have the full 800 minutes to cover the Sep 5th to Oct 5th time frame.
