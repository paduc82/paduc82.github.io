hyrulebalverine say:

When you purchase digital content it becomes licensed to your profile, which is why you can play it on any console when you are signed into Live. It's also how you are able to download it from your purchase history on any console.

There is a second license that comes when you make a purchase; this one is for your console. This second license is what allows other users on your console (or the console the purchase was made from) use that content whether or not they are connected to Live. It is this license that gets transferred when you purchase a new console and perform a license transfer.

You can read more about these licenses and how they work here: support.xbox.com/.../download-content

But your brother should purchase the content for use on his own console. That way he can use it wherever he wants.
