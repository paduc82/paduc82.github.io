Microsoft has *never* sent a virus, neither along with an update nor
by itself.
 
One of three things happened to you:
 
1. what you thought was a virus was actually not a virus
 
2. what you thought came from Microsoft actually came from somewhere
else
 
3. you did get a virus and you did get a Microsoft update, but they
had nothing to do with each other and arrived at your computer
separately.
 
One additional question (and point) for you: you say Microsoft "sent
me a update." Was this an e-mail, or did it come as part of the normal
update procedure? If it came as an e-mail, it definitely didn't come
from Microsoft. Microsoft never sends updates (or anything else) to
you by e-mail. Almost certainly, something like that was a malware
infection that came from somebody other than Microsoft.
