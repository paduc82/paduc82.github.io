     "--------------"

     "Properties"

anil kumar b say:

Larry,

I would suggest you to remove and reinstall all USB controllers.

 

  1. Press Windows key + X, click Device Manager option.
  2. Expand Universal Serial Bus controllers.
  3. Right-click every device under the Universal Serial Bus controllers node, and then click Uninstall to remove them one at a time.
  4. Restart the computer to reinstall the USB controllers automatically.
  5. Plug in the USB devices and check the status of the issue.

 

You may also visit computer manufacturer website and install latest chipset drivers. Keep us posted.
