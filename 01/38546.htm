steve winograd say: For each address, create a folder to hold messages and a message rule to automatically move messages into that folder.

To create a folder, click Local Folders , then click File > New > Folder and assign a folder name. 

To create a message rule:
  1. Click Tools > Message Rules > Mail . 
  2. Click New .
  3. Select the Condition Where the To or CC line contains people .
  4. Select the Actions Move it to the specified folder and Stop processing more rules .
  5. Click contains people , fill in the desired address, click Add , and click OK .
  6. Click specified , click the desired folder for that address, and click OK .
  7. If desired, give the rule a name.
  8. Click OK twice.
