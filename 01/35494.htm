aredhel say:

I found the solution to this problem. I have 3 different mail accounts, one of which is the Windows ID, but it's not Outlook.com account. In Windows 8 all 3 accounts worked fine, as I already stated above. When moving to Windows 8.1, these accounts are carried over. Same as when you reinstall the Mail app.

 

But that's not where it stops. In addition an account is created from your Windows ID and named after the server part from that e-mail. Say, your Windows ID is *** Email address is removed for privacy ***, then Mail will create an account "Bar". If, at the same time, you have an actual "Bar" account then this automatically generated account will seem innocuous to you. Instead, you'll focus on the "Outlook" account which was carried over from Windows 8.0.

 

After some tinkering you decide to remove it altogether and re-add it. You'll fail, because the account already exists - as "Bar", but you don't know that at that point.

 

So the fix is to look for an account with a name similar to your Windows ID, rename that to "Outlook" (or whatever you like), enable sync for e-mail and you're done.
