To add to what Lorien – One says, if you are not able to format the disk, I suggest that you delete the from the disk management.

Open Computer Management by clicking the Start button , clicking Control Panel, clicking System and Maintenance, clicking Administrative Tools, and then double-clicking Computer Management.‌  If you are prompted for an administrator password or confirmation, type the password or provide confirmation.

1. From the Disk Management Right-click the partition (Memory Stick) or drive that you want to change, and then click Change Drive Letter and Paths.

2.  To change a drive letter, click Change, click Assign the following drive letter, click the letter that you want to use, and then click OK.
