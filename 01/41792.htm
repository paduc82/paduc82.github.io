Sounds like a corrupted system file somewhere. To try and correct it do the following.

  1. Hit Windows key + X to open the power users menu and click "Command Prompt (Admin)".
  2. Copy and then right click and paste the following command and hit Enter.

DISM.exe/Online /Cleanup-image /Restorehealth

You won't see much while the tool is running and it's important to maintain your Internet connection since it downloads original system files from Windows Update to replace any corrupt ones found. Also it takes about 30 mins or so to run.

At the end though, you should see the message that the component store was repaired.

Reboot and then see if it fixes the problem. If it persists, open Command Prompt (Admin) again and run the following command:

sfc /scannow

This command can also take around 30 mins to complete after which the results will be displayed.

a. user say:

Error 807b0193 occurs when you try to download your Xbox Live profile

You see the following error when you try to download your Xbox Live profile from the Xbox Live service.

Error 807b0193

This may mean that the Xbox Live service is temporarily down or there's a problem with your Xbox Live profile.

Solutions

To resolve this problem, try the following solutions. If the first solution doesn't work, go to the second solution. 

Solution 1: Confirm your connection to Xbox Live 
Using the links at Xbox Live: Networking to confirm that you're not experiencing connectivity problems and that the Xbox Live service isn't temporarily down. 

Solution 2: Wait a few hours and try again 
If the Xbox Live service is temporarily down, wait a few hours and then try to download your profile again. 

To download your Xbox Live profile, see Download, move, or delete your Xbox Live profile. 
If you keep experiencing the problem, contact Xbox Support.
