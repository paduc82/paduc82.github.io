Update on the issue:

After days of looking for solutions online, I decided to attempt to reinstall the new Creators' Update. The update went very smoothly but, when looking at task manager, the CPU was pinned at 100% for the whole time I was using my PC. This was due to many instances of "svchost.exe" being open.

This led me to believe that the problem for my slowdown was caused by something wrong with one of the services running on my computer.

I opened the services list and found that one process was still in the "starting" state after my computer had already been on for half an hour. I disabled the service and set the start-up setting to manual instead of automatic. This resolved my problem of having 100% CPU usage.

The service that caused this was called "Internet Connection Sharing (ICS)" so if you are having the same issue as I was having then look at your services list and check that it isn't stuck in a "starting" state.
