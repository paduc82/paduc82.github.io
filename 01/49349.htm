    ramesh srinivasan say:

    Hi Cathy,

    Items in the Quick access view can't be sorted as you like. It's a special folder designed that way. The "recent files" section, files that are most recently accessed are shown at the top. For Frequent folders listing, its sorted as:

    Pinned Special folders

    Pinned folders

    Recent folders

    Two things that can be changed:

    1. Mix up Frequent folders and Recent files listing, instead of them being shown in separate groups.

    2. Sort "frequent folders" items by name regardless of type (whether it's a special folder, pinned normal folder or a recent folder.)

    Unfortunately, both of this involve editing the registry.

    For custom sort, AFAIK, that can't be done for Quick access.. and even if it were possible it might involve editing the registry and changing the shell folder bitmask values.

    Two other workarounds would be:

    1. Pin "Recent folders" in Quick Access.

    2. Pin "Recent folders" as a separate namespace item, appearing below Quick access section.

    REF: How to Pin Recent folders or Recent Places to the Navigation Pane in Windows 10 File Explorer

    Benefit: Items in this view can be custom sorted.
