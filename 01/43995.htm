"For the  there are two choices you can install"


AC97 is an older more basic version. HD (Azalia) is the 'improved' multi-channel version
capable of playing higher kHz and bit rate audio. So, in theory the HD is better,

[EDIT: and unless yourmachine is rather old   HD is the go in your case (I didn't check your link

at first) ]

Unfortunately, if you play games you sometimes have to limit the kHz to 48kHz (the limit of AC97 if using quad or 5:1 . And 96kHz being the limit if using stereo) anyway, as some games (mostly older) crash

when using higher settings.

Note that it is best to download the most recent version Realtek drivers  from the mfg of your PC,

or motherboard mfg (also suggested by Realtek ) because the PC and motherboard mfg's make

changes to suit their product, but in practice the drivers don't get updated very often.

Some motherboard related drivers and BIOS may get one or even two updates, but

these days the graphics drivers are generally the only one's that still get frequent updates.

"Does it matter which one you use for the OS"

Yes, drivers are generally specific to OS and in particular if 32 or 64bit.
