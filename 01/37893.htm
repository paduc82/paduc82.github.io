    liza.z say:

    Hi,

    Thank you for visiting Microsoft Community.


    1. Click "Start."
    2. Click "Computer."
    3. Right-click the external hard drive and Click "Properties."
    4. Click the "Sharing" tab. Click the "Advanced Sharing" button in this window.
    5. Uncheck "Share this Folder."
    6. Click "OK."
    7. Click "OK" in the original "Drive Properties" Window.
