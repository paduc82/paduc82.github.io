onyxishere:  i have never seen a usb drive get "changed" by connecting it to a pc, but i have seen that with internal hard dives.  when you connect a usb drive formatted for xbox 360 storage to a pc and open the folder you will see a folder called Xbox360, and inside is all the data from the 360 filesystem stored as chunks labelled Data0000, Data0001, etc.  i back up those files all the time in case my flash drive dies, or i need to use it for something else.  i have successfully "restored the backup" simply by copying the files back as they were, without again formatting the usb drive with the 360.  also she said she was able to copy the files back to the console, but i have another guess as to what may have happened.
