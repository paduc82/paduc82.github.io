Hello

Signing in to Skype using your Microsoft account will generate a new account, which means whatever subscription or credit you have on the other account will not reflect in there.

When signing in to Skype, make sure you're using the correct account. If you usually sign in using a specific username and password, then it should be the same information you will provide when logging in.

Note that Skype is under the umbrella of Microsoft, thus, the option to log in using a Microsoft account is now possible, but that does not mean that when being asked to sign in to Skype, you will provide the Microsoft account, you still need to use your original signing in details.

And since you've used a different sign in credential, it might be the reason why your credit and contacts disappear. To make sure you're accessing the correct account, carefully follow the steps on this link: Why are my Skype contacts or credit missing?
