You can use installation or recovery media to get to startup settings, even if your PC won't start:

  1. To create installation media, go to the Microsoft software download website from a working PC and follow the instructions.

  2. Insert the USB drive or DVD and restart your PC. If you’re using recovery media, skip to Step 4.
