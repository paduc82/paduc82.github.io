5.    In the properties window, under Driver tab, click on Update Driver button.

6.    After the installing the updates restart the computer.

Steps for Startup Repair from the Windows Recovery Environment (WinRE) ==============================

 

1. Insert the Windows installation disc into the disc drive, and then start the computer. 

2. Press a key when the message indicating "Press any key to boot from CD or DVD …" appears.

3. Select a language, a time and currency, and a keyboard or input method, and then click Next. 

4. Click Repair your computer. 

5. In the System Recovery Options dialog box, choose the drive of your Windows installation and click Next. 

6. In the System Recovery Options dialog box, click Startup Repair.
