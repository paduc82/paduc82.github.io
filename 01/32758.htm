Hey,

I know this thread is super old, but I've found a solution to that kind of problem.

I'm a trainee in an engineer school in the computer ressources department, and when configuring a classroom full of PCs (brandname : Dell) for Wake on Lan, I thought it was a good idea to activate the watchdog timer so we could monitor each PC's activity and stuff.
Thing is that after that, I've received a ticket saying that the PCs in this classroom kept on rebooting on themselves even when working on them, which was indeed problematic.

At first I thought it was the wake on lan that kept doing this, so I deactivated it in the BIOS, but the problem was still there, the machines kept rebooting on and on, after several minutes.

>The solution was in fact deactivating the watchdog timer, to do so I had to get in the BIOS settings when the PCs would restart, then get to the Maintenance tab and the Watchdog Timer tab, and disable the Watchdog Timer by unticking the case.

Now the issue is solved and Wake on Lan still works like a charm.

I hope this will help anybody having that kind of issue and if so, I'm glad it did !
