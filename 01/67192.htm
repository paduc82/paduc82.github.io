jeff d - windows camera team say:

The 0xA00F4246 error means the Camera app can't initialize your camera driver. What this means is that the Camera app isn't communicating with your camera hardware successfully. It might be a bug in the Camera app, it might be a bug in the camera driver, or it may be that your camera driver is not installed correctly.

I wish I had a clear solution for you, but I don't yet. In the meantime, here are some things to try:

  • Try an external USB camera.
  • Try reinstalling your camera driver.

We are investigating this issue for various cameras, and if I learn anything more I will report back to you.

grahamcpt say:

I had this problem with the camera and similar problems with other program files after upgrading from Windows 7. I discovered that the original files had been stored under the original user naming from Windows 7. On updating I had opened with my Microsoft account as administrator. The solution I found was to open the Properties of each file group (Documents etc) and allow these files to be shared with the "new" administrator (Microsoft account). Everything now seems to work fine ..... I hope. 

Hope this helps
