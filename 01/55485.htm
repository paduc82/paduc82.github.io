                               net stop cryptSvc

    

                               net stop bits

    

                               net stop msiserver

                              ren C:\Windows\SoftwareDistribution SoftwareDistribution.old

                            net start wuauserv

    

                            net start cryptSvc

    

                            net start bits

    

                            net start msiserver
