Method 1:

Step 1: Disable IP/V6

Follow the steps to disable IP/V6

1.    Go to Start > Control Panel > View Network Status and tasks (under Network and Internet)
2.    Chose Manage Network Connections on the left side menu
3.    Right click Local Area Connection and choose Properties
4.    In the pop-up box, uncheck the following:
              o Internet Protocol Version 6 (TCP/IPv6)
5.    Click OK and then restart your computer.

Step 2: Enable IP/V6:

1.    Follow steps 1 to 3 from above.
             o In the pop-up box, put a checkmark on Internet Protocol Version 6 (TCP/IPv6).
2.    Click OK and then restart your computer.
