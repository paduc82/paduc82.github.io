1. First things first turn on your 360 and go to the network tab and find your 360's IP address. It should be at the very top.

4. Find the port forwarding section of your router. It should be in the routers Security or Firewall tab.

 

5. Open these ports and assign them to your 360's IP address

TCP 53

UDP 53

TCP 80

UDP 80

TCP 88

UDP 88

TCP 1863

UDP 1863

TCP 3074

UDP 3074

After that your NAT should be open.
