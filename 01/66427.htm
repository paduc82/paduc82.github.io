Login Information

  • Gateway - 192.168.0.1
  • Username - admin
  • Password - admin

DMZ

  1. Open a web browser.
  2. Enter "192.168.0.1" in the address bar.
  3. If prompted for a username/password, type in the username and password.
  4. Click on the "log in" button.
  5. Click on the "Application & Gaming" category located on the top of the page.
  6. Click on the "DMZ" subcategory just below the main category.
  7. Select "Enabled"
  8. Type in the IP address for the device desired.
  9. Click on "Save settings" on the bottom of the page.

 

 Disable DMZ

  1. Open a web browser.
  2. Enter "192.168.0.1" in the address bar.
  3. If prompted for a username/password, type in the username and password.
  4. Click on the "log in" button.
  5. Click on the "Application & Gaming" category located on the top of the page.
  6. Click on the "DMZ" subcategory just below the main category.
  7. Select "Disable"
  8. Click on "Save settings" on the bottom of the page.

  1. Unplug external devices connected to the computer expect keyboard and mouse.

  5. Select Startup setting from advance option.

  7.  In new windows press F5 to login with safe mode with networking.

This option takes your PC back to an earlier point in time, called a system restore point. Restore points are generated when you install a new app, driver, or Windows update, and when you create a restore point manually. Restoring won‚Äôt affect your personal files, but it will remove apps, drivers, and updates installed after the restore point was made.
