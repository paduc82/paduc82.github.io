louie del say:

Hi,

Windows allows users to use either a Microsoft account or a local account to be used to log onto a their computers. If you're currently signed in to your device using a Microsoft account, here are the steps that you can follow to switch it to a local account:

  1. In the search box, type Settings and click on the result. Click on Accounts.
  2. Click Your info, on the left side, click on Sign in with a local account instead.

  3. Typed in the password of the Microsoft account that's currently signed in, and then click on Next.

  4. Typed in a user name, password and password hint that you prefer for your local account. Click on Next once you're done.

    Note: User name that's already associated to an existing profiles can no longer be used and a different name needs to be used instead. You can leave the password empty for the local account, however it's recommended to have it provided to keep your computer more secure.

  5. Click on Sign out and finish.

  6. Once it's done, you can already sign in to Windows 10 with the local account that was switched to.
