jetta48 say:

1. Insert your flash drive & wait for it to be recognised.

2. Launch Windows Explorer.

3. Select the files to be transferred using single left click of mouse. (Hold down the CTRL key to select multiple files)

4. Press CTRL + C (at the same time) to copy the files to your clipboard.

5. In Windows Explorer double click on your flash drive.

6. In right-hand pane of Windows Explorer, right click on a blank area and select Paste. The files will then start to copy.

 

There are a number of ways of doing it - the above is one example. Dragging and dropping is another way.
