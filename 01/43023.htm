ruwim.b say:

The new Skype is capable to save and load about 2 years history kept now on the Cloud servers. History older than 2 year may still be saved on your computer with the old Skype 7.X version. However, this implies that this old Skype 7.X version was previously installed on your computer and was used in the past 5 years.

 

This old history can be accessed using e.g. this free SkypeLogView application.

 

https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/skype_log_view.html
