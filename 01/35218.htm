The article you quote refers to older versions of Windows. With current versions, Microsoft dropped the limit to 1024 characters (probably unintentionally . . .).

reg add "HKLM\SYSTEM\CurrentControlSet\Control\Session Manager\Environment" /f /v Test /d 12345
