I noticed that the option for Searching in file folders for system files had been left unchecked.  Even though I would never have considered a PDF a system file, nor is the folder (D:\Documents\Personal) in any way stored under systems files, nonetheless, checking that box magically made the sought after files appear!  And this works both with wildcards, * and ?, as well as AQS instructions.
