_elaine_ say:

Hi, pablo.masoero

Unfortunately, no.  Skype does not support what is called in telephone industry jargon as inbound number portability.  In other words,

- we cannot bring land line or mobile numbers in to Skype,

- we cannot import numbers from other services into Skype, for use as Skype Numbers. 

Skype Numbers are land line numbers that Skype obtains from local carriers.  We can select a Skype Number only from the list of numbers provided during the Skype Number subscription start-up process.

Elaine
