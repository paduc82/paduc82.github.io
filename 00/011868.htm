monique tang msft say:

Hi Bgonski,

To share the conference room calendar with an external user, you can refer to the following steps:

1. Assign yourself Full Access permission to the room mailbox.

In EAC, navigate to recipients> resources> Edit> mailbox delegation> Full Access> +> add yourself to the list.

Note: It may take some time for the assigned permission to take effect.

2. In OWA, click on the account name button on the top-right corner and select Open another mailbox.

3. Search for the room mailbox that you wish to open and the account will open in a new window.

4. In the new window, go to Calendar page and then click share>fill in the external user> Search> choose permission> Send.
