* Stop Skype Desktop From Starting Automatically in Windows 10

1. Open the Skype Desktop app on your Computer. You can do this by clicking on the Skype Shortcut on your Desktop or by clicking on Skype from the list of apps.


2. Sign-in to your Skype Account (in case you are not already signed-in).

3. Next, click on "..." next to your image profile and click "Settings"

4. On the settings screen, go to "General" and uncheck or turn off "Automatically Start Skype"

After this you will not find the Skype app starting Automatically on your Windows 10 Computer, it will only start when you click on the Skype shortcut and launch the App on your Computer.

_________________________________________________________

* Stop Skype UWP From Starting Automatically in Windows 10

1. Click on the Windows 10 Start button and then click on Skype UWP.

2. On the next screen (Skype UWP), click on your Profile picture/icon located at the bottom left corner of your screen.

3. Next, scroll down to the bottom of the screen and click on the Sign Out option.

This will sign you out of the Skype UWP and you won’t find it starting automatically on your Windows 10 Computer.

I hope this information has been useful, please let me know if you still need assistance.
