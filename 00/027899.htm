Hi Bo Dobbs,

If you would like to remove a Shared Calendar, right click (or Ctrl + left click) and select Remove from View:

Meanwhile, you can also ask the people who shared this calendar to remove the permission:

  1. At the bottom of the navigation pane, click Calendar.

bobgreenblatt say: Paul, the calculations option is a little strange. The calculation state (automatic vs manual) is indeed saved with the sheet. However, this is an Excel wide option.
