Either dimension your range at the top of the module, after the Option Explicit statement, which makes it public to all subs in the module, or explictly make it public to all subs in the project with, for example: 

 

Public rngName As Range

Sub Test()
Dim rngA As Range

Test2 rngA  'Pass a range object variable
