One way, assuming the records begin in A2:

In any cell you wish (even a different sheet or workbook) use the formula:

=A2&B2&C2&D2&E2

Then copy it down for as may rows as necessary based on the number of records.

r.ritchey say:

Installed programs

Programs installed on a Hard Disk Drive other than the one you have installed Win10 on ( in the same computer of course) will be more likely to persist in the next major update.  Win 10 has a habit of Not bringing Programs with the next build if they are on the same drive and they are not MS programs.
