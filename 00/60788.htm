nick dich say:

Hi Shaz,

One of the cause of this issue is the hard disk was set to turn off on a specific idle time. Kindly check this setting by following the steps below:

  1. In Cortana, search for Power options.
  2. On your selected Preferred plan, click on Change plan settings.
  3. Click on Change advance power settings.
  4. Look for Hard disk and make sure that the Turn off hard disk after settings is on 0.
  5. Click Ok and Save changes.

After doing this, monitor the changes and see if it will still shut down.
