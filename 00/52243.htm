brandonstiff say:

I had this same issue.  In my case the issue was with the Microsoft account the game was purchased under.

-  You *must* own the game and be signed into the XBOX live app as *that* account while you are playing.  

In my case, my son thought he had purchased the game, but he actually didn't.  He went through the purchase process, but missed some step at the end.  He was able to play on one of my PCs signed in as himself (even though he didn't own it) because I had purchased the game and was not currently signed on.  This gave us the impression he owned it.  However, I could not see it attached to his account.  

Even after I signed into the store as him and purchased the game for him, he still received the same error.  

The second issue was the XBOX live (Microsoft) account he signed into inside the game was a different one than the MS account we purchased the game under.  He has two accounts, unfortunately.  One he made for his XBOX and an older MS account he uses for the PCs.  There is no reason he needed both, it just turned out that happened. 

So, if you get this error, verify the following:

- Your MS account owns the game.  Check your "My Library" section in the store and verify SOD2 shows up there.

- While in the game, make sure you are signed into XBOX live with *that* account.  

We share PCs here so that is an issue for us.
