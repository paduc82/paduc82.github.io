charles kenyon say:

The vertical line is a paragraph border.

What is being added is for the space-after in the paragraph formatting.

If you change the paragraph formatting for this paragraph to have no space after and then change the formatting of the following paragraph to have space before of the same amount that you removed from the paragraph with the border, you will accomplish what you want.

Doing this manually will also make your document harder to edit.

I would suggest using three styles:

  1. Your Base style without the line - perhaps Normal
  2. Your line style
  3. A new style for the next paragraph for situations where you are typing and apply your line style.

Change your line style to have your left paragraph border and no space after.

The style for the following paragraph should be the new style.

The new style should have both space before and space after, no line

The style for the following paragraph should be your original base style.
