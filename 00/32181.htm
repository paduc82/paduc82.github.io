Rise of the Tomb Raider FAQ

Greetings, Tomb Raider fans! We are super excited to talk about Rise of the Tomb Raider, and we hope you are just as excited to play it! In this FAQ, we will go over some common questions you may have, in addition to covering some of the known issues to keep a look out for at launch.

Q: When will fans be able to experience Rise of the Tomb Raider on Xbox One/Xbox 360
A: Rise of the Tomb Raider is expected to release on Xbox One and Xbox 360 on November 10, 2015

Q: When will fans who've pre-ordered digitally through the Xbox Store on Xbox One be able to dive into Rise of the Tomb Raider?
A: If you already pre-ordered through our Xbox Store on Xbox One, you can expect to jump right in at 12:01 AM EST on the release date.

Q: What does the Season Pass include for Rise of the Tomb Raider?
A: The Season Pass for Rise of the Tomb Raider will include a variety of content that will extend the campaign even further. Fans will be able to take part in Endurance Mode, explore a new the new tomb in Baba Yaga: The Temple of the Witch, and waves of infected predators in Cold Darkness Awakened. Keep an eye out for when you'll be able to hop into those new modes!

In addition to those exciting new modes, Season Pass owners will receive a steady stream of outfits, weapons, and Expedition Cards beginning on Day 1 and continuing over 4 months after release.

Q: What are Expeditions?
A:  Expeditions are extra gameplay modes which allows you to play Expedition Cards to modify your experience.

Additionally, you can take part in challenges to earn credits towards purchasing new cards or complete the campaign to earn card packs! If you desire cards instantly, you have the choice to purchase cards with real money.

Q: What are the different Expedition Modes?
A: Check out the following list for a rundown of each of the new Expedition Modes:

  • Score Attack: An arcade style mode where you replay certain campaign missions to rack up points through kills, combos, and time bonuses.
  • Remnant Resistance: An objective-based mode where players can play in Rise of the Tomb Raider's large hub spaces. Players can choose to create their own mission, or play their friends and the Tomb Raider community missions. Each mission can vary from taking down targets, hunting animals, collecting intel, and more. The harder the mission, the better the credits reward will be!
  • Chapter Replay: Replay campaign missions with a pre-determined amount of gear and skills.
  • Chapter Replay Elite: Similar to a 'New Game+' type mode, you'll get to carry on from your previous campaign with all of your gear and skills.

Q: What is Endurance Mode?
A: An Expedition Mode that focuses on Lara's survival in the Siberian forest. Players will have to find food and keep warm while seeking artifacts and find a path to escape.

Q: Will Xbox 360 players have access to these DLC?
A: Yup! All DLC's, in addition to the Season Pass, will be available for Xbox 360 players.

Q: What are the different versions I can purchase?
A: Check out the list below for the different offers we have! (prices in different regions vary)

Rise of the Tomb Raider Deluxe Edition: $89.99 USD

  • Includes Season Pass 

Pre-ordering either of these from the Xbox Store on Xbox One before release will include the 'Remnant Resistance Pre-Order Pack'.

  • Resolved an issue where the player may encounter an invisible wall inside the interior of the Soviet Copper Mill when returning after completing the game
  • Resolved an issue where moveable objects in the Abandoned Mines may act erratic when coming into contact with the player
  • Resolved an issue where the player will be unable to use the climbing axe to melee attack NPCs after the flamethrower battle in Geothermal Valley, until meeting up with Sophia again
  • Resolved an issue where, rarely, an NPC will not spawn and prevent the fight in the Gulag prison from progressing
  • Resolved an issue where, rarely, the player will encounter an invisible wall where the gate was after the first combat encounter in Path of the Deathless
