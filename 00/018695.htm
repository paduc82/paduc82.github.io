jay freedman say:
If you check the box for the "Automatically update document styles", the copying occurs when you click OK in the Templates & Add-Ins dialog.

If you leave the box checked, I believe the update happens again each time you open the document (although I haven't tested to be sure of that). That belief is based on a statement in http://word.mvps.org/FAQs/MacrosVBA/UpdateStyles.htm, 'The “Automatically update document styles” setting is document-specific, and if left switched on when a particular document is saved, will update that document's styles whenever it is opened by anyone (until the document is saved again with the setting switched off).'

That article also describes a restriction, 'It is important to realise that the only styles that are updated when you update a document's styles from its  template are the ones listed in the template itself under “Styles in use” (in the Style dialog, where it says “List”).' I don't know whether that still applies to the latest versions of Word.
