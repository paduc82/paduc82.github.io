a thameem say:

Can the browsing history of a tab be deleted on exit when the tab is closed?

"Delete browsing history on exit" will not delete just the browsing history of a tab on closing the tab, it will delete the entire history of the browser on exit of the browser itself.

To delete a particular website history from the computer you can try the following:

Open the Internet Explorer browser by locating it in your "Start" menu or double-clicking on the blue "e" icon on your desktop or task bar.

Press the "Ctrl" and "H" keys simultaneously to bring up your browser's history over intervals such as one day ago, one week ago and one month ago. You can expand each date by clicking on the calendar icon to the right of the text.

Delete specific websites by right-clicking on the website name and selecting "Delete" from the drop-down menu. Click "Yes" in the pop-up menu to confirm the deletion.

Click on the "History" tab at the top of the window pane and select "Search History" to search through your entire browsing history for a specific website. Internet Explorer will search for the website name and bring up any links in your browsing history. Delete these links in the same manner.

a. user say:

Recover missing contacts after phone upgrade

  1. If you upgraded to Windows 10 Mobile and are missing contacts, email, or calendar items, make sure your phone is updated, and re-add your Microsoft account.
    1. On Start, swipe over to the All apps list, and then select Settings > Update & security > Phone update.
    2. Select Check for updates and follow the instructions to update your phone.
    3. Go back to Settings, and select Accounts > Your email and accounts.
    4. Under Email, calendar, and contacts, select Add an account. Add your main Microsoft account email address again.
  2. Your contacts should now be restored.
