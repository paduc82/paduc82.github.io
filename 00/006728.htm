bob jones aka: cybertaz say:

It sounds like the measurement system for your printer's driver software may be Metric [as well as your Mac's, perhaps]. That's the beauty of 'intelligent computing' -- the printer driver has something defined that is close to what you're calling for, so it's assuming that you are wrong... it's making the 'correction' for you :-)

Keep in mind, though, that Word has nothing to do with this other than to present the dialog windows. The management of custom paper sizes is handled by OS X & your printer's metrics &  driver software.

Try setting up your Custom Paper Size as 17.78cm by 25.40cm to see if it makes any difference.

If you still have a problem, confirm that your driver software is properly updated on the Mac. Even for the same physical printer, the drivers for OS X & Mac often differ. It may also be a good idea to contact Epson support or Apple's Community for Printing issues.
