popinss say:

Outlook 2010 - Exchange 2010 - Advanced Find

Hello,

 

I would like to use "Advanced Find" in Outlook 2010.

 

 Subject   contains    (OK)

 

it finds all mails with (OK) and  (OK?)

 

I need to find the mails having :  ( and ) and the exact word: OK between these ()  (I do not want any other characters).

 

How to do it ?

 

Thanks

andrea jones - all about resources say:

Go to the mail folder you want to search and click the View Settings button in the Current View group on the View ribbon.  Now click the Filter button and click on the SQL section of the filter window.  Click to place a tick beside 'Edit these criteria directly' at the bottom of the window and type the following in the search criteria box:

("urn:schemas:httpmail:subject" LIKE '%(OK)%')

Click OK and then click OK again and your folder should be filtered for the matching messages.  To remove the filter, click the View Settings button again and click on Filter, then click the Clear All button and click OK.
