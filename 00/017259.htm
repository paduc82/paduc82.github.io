So when I originally replied to this thread a little over two years ago I did not ask (or mention) if you had connected to the Wi-Fi using a Windows 10 computer. If the answer is yes, you could easily find the Wi-Fi key

To view your current Wi-Fi password on a Windows 10 PC, do the following:

  1. Open Control Panel.
  2. Click on Network and Internet.
  3. Click on Network and Sharing Center.
  4. On the left pane, click the Change adapter settings link.
  5. Double-click the wireless adapter you want.
  6. Click the Wireless Properties button.
  7. On the Security tab, under Network security key, check the Show characters option to view the Wi-Fi password.
