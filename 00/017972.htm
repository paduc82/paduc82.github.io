Hyperlinks won't open in outlook 2010 windows 10 - Microsoft Community

joe6pak say:

Hyperlinks won't open in outlook 2010 windows 10

I'm using Outlook 2010 on Windows 10.  I also use Firefox and Chrome.

When attempting to open a hyperlink, I received the message: "This operation has been cancelled due to restrictions in effect on this computer ".

After reviewing a number of suggestions I found on the web, here's how I resolved my issue.

In the registry, I found that the "Default" value for HKEY_ROOT\.html\OpenWithProgids was blank, zippo, nothing there! I clicked on (Default), then Modify... and added the value "htmfile"in the blank field, then OK!

Problem solved!  Hope if works for someone else.
