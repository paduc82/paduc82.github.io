Sub splitter()
' splitter Macro
' Macro created by Doug Robbins to save each letter created by a mailmerge
' as a separate file, retaining the header and footer information.
Dim i As Long, Source As Document, Target As Document, Letter As Range
Set Source = ActiveDocument
For i = 1 To Source.Sections.Count
    Set Letter = Source.Sections(i).Range
    Set Target = Documents.Add
    Target.Range = Letter
    Target.Sections(2).PageSetup.SectionStart = wdSectionContinuous
    Target.SaveAs FileName:="Letter" & i
    Target.Close
Next i
End Sub

ScreEn Frozen Black ? Finally an Answer . Nokia Lumia 925 - Microsoft Community

noahhub say:

ScreEn Frozen Black ? Finally an Answer . Nokia Lumia 925

I have found a solution to the screen freezing black, at first it it happened to my own Nokia Lumia 925 smartphone, I checked every web and looked through all the answers and couldn't find anything. Was finally thinking about shutting it off and getting my money back until I gave it one more try. If your phone is frozen black but still on, where you can see it still blinking on and off at times then follow these steps. Please check both steps. Second Set of Steps is way faster and a lot more easier but could not work so check Second set of steps before checking first, it could save you a lot of time.

First Set of Steps

1) Make sure not to charge your phone, please try to turn up your volume if you can and remember to act like your phone is still on, turn up your volume then take a snapshot, the snapshot button for my phone is by holding the power and volume up button up at the same time, if you can hear a sound of a picture being taken then continue, if not then I'm unsure, you can keep trying I guess.

2) Basically wait till your phone dies since its still on and remember not to charge it, it could take a while, it took 2 days when my phone was at 70% to die so be patient, you can try to use it to waste battery I guess.

3) When your phone has died and you will know when you can't hear anything and there's no lights turning on, no vibrating or anything, plug in your charger, it should then turn on as if it just started again and you will be able to use your phone again.

Second Set of Steps

1) After having the same issue again I decided to try something new, this time check to hear the screenshot button, then if you can, try to turn off your phone, by doing this hold the power button, for about 5 seconds, then slide your finger from the top of your phone down just like you would to power it off

2) Then just like in the first set of steps plug in your charger after about a minute of letting your phone officially power down then plug in your charger and enjoy your phone.
