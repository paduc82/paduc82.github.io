Actually, adjusting "Preferred width" for the balloons can help quite a lot, if you are not afraid to make them quite wide.  Also, if you don't mind keeping the document in Landscape layout during the review phase, it will help even more.  Exactly how much to adjust it may require a little trial and error for each document.
