From MVP Andre Da Costa 

To cancel reservation follow the below steps:
•Click/tap on the Get Windows 10 app icon on the taskbar notification area.
•Click/tap on the "hamburger" style menu button at the top left corner in the Get Windows 10 app.
•Click/tap on View confirmation under Getting the upgrade.
•Click/tap on the Cancel reservation link.
•Click/tap on Cancel reservation to confirm.
•Click/tap on Close.

This will cancel the reservation

From Ruth Buruga - Microsoft Engineer

To uninstall the Reservation completely from my computer, Please follow the below steps:

1. Click Start (or press Windows key + S for Windows 8.1 users)

2. Type: view installed updates

3. Hit Enter on your keyboard

4. Look through the list for the KB3035583 and KB2976978 update.

5. Right click each update and click Uninstall.
