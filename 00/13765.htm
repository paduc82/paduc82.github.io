scottgem say:

No, Since an ACCDE removes the ability to enter Design mode, trying to do so will raise an error. But why do you need to enter Design mode?

You can change the Caption property of labels, the visible property of textboxes, the Top and Left properties of controls, as well as the Controlsources all using VBA. The only thing you would not be able to do is add or delete controls. So you can modify the look of a form (or use multiple forms and just open the one you need) rather than modify a form in Design mode. So why is doing so not practical? Especially since the alternative (opening in Design mode) is not available under ACCDE.

scott mcdaniel say:

You can't change the name of a Form/Report Control in an ACCDE file.

You can change the ControlSource of a Control, if you want to save the contents of that control to a different location at times.
