jan karel pieterse say:

Pro vlookup: simpler formula than an INDEX/MATCH combination.

 

Against VLOOKUP

1. The fixed Column number argument (which can be made dynamic, but INDEX/MATCH makes this simpler because you just refer to the columns you need).

2. Lookup column has to be to the left of the returned column

3. People tend to fill multiple columns with many vlookups with incrementing lookup column numbers, causing multiple searches against the same table.
