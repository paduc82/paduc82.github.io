david wiedeman say: Sorry for the late reply. This issue was happening for me, so when I searched for answer in Google this was the first result. Here is the
Outlook 2010 saves a sent message in the Sent message folder AND your IMAP mail providers does the same, thus creating two sent message every time you send a single message. Turn off the "Save copies of messages in the Sent folder" in Outlook or your IMAP mail provider.

To do in Outlook 2010:
1. Click "File" tab
2. Click "Options" button
3. Click the "Mail" button
6. Scroll down to the "Save messages" section
5. Uncheck "Save copies of messages in the Sent folder"
