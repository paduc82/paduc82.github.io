a. user say:

Hello,

Thank you for posting your concern here in Microsoft Community and welcome to the Windows 10 family. I would be happy to assist you.

First of all, thank you for showing your interest in the development plans of Microsoft for its Windows 10 insider users.

From the description, I understand that you delete the Windows.old folder, every time one is created, which is what your question is, if it will prevent you in future from the final up gradation to Windows 10 (July 29th).

Please be informed that Windows.old is a folder is created if you have performed a custom installation. The Windows.old gives you an option to revert to the previous build/version of Windows.

However, the option to revert to previous; as well as the Windows.old folder is deleted through a scheduled automatic maintenance task which takes place right after the 30th day since the current installation.

So the answer is, you wont face any issue with the up gradation, even if the Windows.old folder is deleted (manually/automatic).
