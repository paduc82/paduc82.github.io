The current incoming mail flow is Internet > Cisco EAS > Trend IMVSA > EX2016

With CMT -  Internet > Cisco EAS > Trend IMVSA > EX2016 > Forcepoint DLP > EdgeTransport  > Trend Micro (IMVSA AV and spam filter) > Cisco EAS > Internet > EOP > EOL > EOP > Internet > Cisco EAS > Trend IMVSA > EX2016 >  > Forcepoint DLP > EdgeTransport  > Trend Micro (IMVSA AV and spam filter) > Cisco EAS > Internet ? a. user say:

Hi R.Arokiasamy,

 

Thanks for your detailed description and please check my following answers to your question.

 

1)Yes, you should use Edge server in HCW if you have it to handle your on-prem mail flows.

 

2)The HCW will help set up the required connectors to establish the mail flow between your on-prem Exchange and EOP. And yes, based on my experience there shouldn't be any servers, services placed between them but the third party gateways can help secure inbound and outbound flows with the Internet.

 

Flow with Internet inbound: Internet > third party gateways > ET Server.

Flow with Internet outbound: ET server > third party gateways > Internet.

Flow with EOP: only ET server and EOP communicate with each other.

 

3)No, there is no Address Rewriting feature in Exchange online and you need to use the on-prem ET server to achieve that.

 

Hope these information helps.
