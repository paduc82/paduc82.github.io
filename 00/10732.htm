louie del say:

To help you further on how to forward or redirect email automatically, you can follow the steps below:

  1. Click the File tab, then select Manage Rules & Alerts.
  2. In the Rules and Alerts dialog box, on the E-mail Rules tab, click New Rule.
  3. Click Apply rule on message I receive under Start from a blank rule, and then click Next.
  4. Under Step 1: Select condition(s), select the conditions that you want the messages to meet for the rule to apply.
  5. Under Step 2: Edit the rule description, click each of underlined text, specify values, and then click Next.
  6. Under Step 1:Select action(s), select either forward it to people or public group or redirect it to people or a public group.
  7. Under Step 2:Edit the rule description, click the underlined text for people or public group, then specify the people or public group you want to forward messages to, and click Next.
  8. Under Step 1: Select exception(s) (if necessary), select any exceptions that you prefer.
  9. Under Step 2: Edit the rule description, click each piece of underlined text, specify values, and then click Next.
  10. Under Step 1: Specify a name for this rule, enter a name
  11. Under Step 2: Setup rule options, check the boxes for the options that you want, then click Finish.
  • Check the Run this rule now on messages already in "Inbox" box, if you want to run this rule on the existing messages in the Inbox.
  • By default, the new rule is turned on. To turn off the rule, uncheck the Turn on this rule box.
  • Check the Create this rule on all accounts box to apply this rule to all email accounts set up in Outlook.
