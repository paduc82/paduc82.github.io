    Summary

    Learn to change the Microsoft account associated with a specific Windows 8.1 Windows account. Only applicable to Windows 8.1.

    Product Support Link
    OneDrive for Business Contact Office 365 for Business support


    Details

    Sometimes it's necessary to change the Microsoft account associated with a specific Windows account, whether it's because someone else owns the computer or you accidentally created a duplicate Microsoft account when you purchased a new Windows 8 computer. Follow these instructions to remove the current Microsoft account and attach the correct Microsoft account:

    1. Open the Charms bar, click on "Settings", and then click "Change PC Settings"
    2. Click on "Accounts" and then click on "Your account"
    3. Click "Disconnect" to remove the currently attached Microsoft account

    Once disconnected, follow these steps to add the correct Microsoft account:

    1. Open the Charms bar, click on "Settings", and then click "Change PC Settings"
    2. Click on "Accounts" and then click on "Your account"
    3. Click "Connect" and follow the prompts to attach the correct Microsoft account
