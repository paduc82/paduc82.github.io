A data connection always has both sent and received packets. It's like a phone conversation, only between computers instead of people - sometimes one talk more than the other.

I suggest reset the data counter in the phone. It is difficult to keep track of the usage when it starts counting in Gigabytes, and easier when it counts Megabytes.
