hal hostetler - mvp-outlook say:
If you delete an email on the server and it also then deletes from your Inbox on your computer, you are using IMAP protocol, not POP.  Deleting an email on a POP server will have no effect on your Computer's Inbox.  IMAP keeps everything on the server so it can sync with all your email clients.  Since deleting an email on the server also deletes it in your Inbox, it means the change you make on the server is being synced back to your desktop and only IMAP and Exchange Server protocols can do this.
