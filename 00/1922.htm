If your copies of Win7 are OEM versions, you may not be able to activate either copy on either machine and MS will likely not help either.  OEM versions of Windows are meant to only be used on the system it was originally installed on and are not transferable.  There are likely "methods" to get it to work, but you will no longer be running a Genuine license on either machine, so it's not really any different from running pirated copies of the OS on them.  Also, because neither system will be running a Genuine license, the free update to Win10 will not be available on either machine and Win10 will likely not activate on either.  Good luck!
