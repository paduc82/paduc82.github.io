How to remove Connected Services from Office for Mac Open dialog - Microsoft Community

mads nissen say:

How to remove Connected Services from Office for Mac Open dialog

When you have had connections to Office365, SharePoint locations or OneDrive or Business that you no longer want to keep in your Office open dialog, the Office for Mac client applications does not supply a "remove" option on the Mac.

Here is what to do to clean it up:

1. Go to e.g. Word on the Mac and click your account icon. If this is associated to the account you want to leave (e.g. the job you left), you need to sign out, and then sign back in with another account (e.g. your personal outlook.com account). Notice that this account needs to own a license for office (e.g. Office 365 Personal) to be able to use the office applications.

2. Grab your mobile device and repeat the same operation using e.g. the iOS Word app.

3. On your mobile device in the Word App go to settings, then connected services. Your main account should now be your personal account, while your old business accounts still seems connected. 

4. Swipe left on the connected services you want to remove on your mobile device.

5. This will sync across devices, and you can now restart your Office applications on the mac and observe the connected services being gone.
