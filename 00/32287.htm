dominicp say:

Laptops supply one standard USB power to be shared between all the USB ports. This preserves the laptop's battery.

It is common for USB bus powered devices to work only on the first port in the sequence. The connections between the ports have some electrical resistance and the second and subsequent ports often cannot deliver enough power, particularly for devices with motors such as external disks.
