It is not a good idea to unhook a external drive after a shutdown, believe it or not... The reason being a feature called Fast Startup, which does not actually "Close" all drives when doing a shutdown, but kind of hibernates them... Unhooking them under this circumstance can lead to data loss...

4) Any program that monitors HDD\SSD activity, performance, or temperature..
