jay freedman say:

If you have no other need for Japanese or another Asian language in your work, there's a simpler way to set the option :

  • Press Alt+F11 to open the macro editor.
  • Press Ctrl+G to open the Immediate window at the bottom right of the editor.
  • Copy/paste the following line into the Immediate window and then press Enter:    ActiveDocument.Range.ParagraphFormat.WordWrap = True
  • Close the macro editor.

If your new blank documents start with lines breaking in the middle of words, open the Normal.dotm template by copy/pasting the following line into the Immediate window and pressing Enter:

   NormalTemplate.OpenAsDocument

and then use the steps above. Close the Normal.dotm template and start a new blank document to check it.
