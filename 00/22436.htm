How can I get a report of grammar and style flags? - Microsoft Community

bigoldart say:

How can I get a report of grammar and style flags?

Is there a way to get a report on a document that has the word, sentence, and paragraph counts, the readability indices, and countd of the kinds of grammar and style exceptions found in a document
jay freedman say:
The only way to produce a report of the information from the word count and readability dialogs without taking screen shots is to run a macro like the one below.

No version of Word produces a count of grammar/style exceptions separated into counts of the rules that are violated. Even with the macro, only a gross count of all the grammar errors is available.

Read http://www.gmayor.com/installing_macro.htm if you need instructions for copying the macro to your template.

Sub Report()
    Dim inputDoc As Document
    Dim outputDoc As Document
    Dim dlg As Dialog
    Dim rs As ReadabilityStatistic
   
    Set inputDoc = ActiveDocument
    Set dlg = Dialogs(wdDialogToolsWordCount)
    ' update statistics
    dlg.CountFootnotes = True
    dlg.Execute
   
    Set outputDoc = Documents.Add
    With outputDoc.Range
        .ParagraphFormat.TabStops.Add Position:=InchesToPoints(6), Alignment:=wdAlignTabRight
        .InsertAfter "Report for " & inputDoc.FullName & vbCr & vbCr
        .InsertAfter "Statistics:" & vbCr
        .InsertAfter "Pages" & vbTab & dlg.Pages & vbCr
        .InsertAfter "Words" & vbTab & dlg.Words & vbCr
        .InsertAfter "Characters" & vbTab & dlg.Characters & vbCr
        .InsertAfter "Characters (incl Spaces)" & vbTab & dlg.CharactersIncludingSpaces & vbCr
        .InsertAfter "Paragraphs" & vbTab & dlg.Paragraphs & vbCr
        .InsertAfter "Lines" & vbTab & dlg.Lines & vbCr & vbCr
       
        .InsertAfter "Readability:" & vbCr
        For Each rs In inputDoc.ReadabilityStatistics
            .InsertAfter rs.Name & vbTab & rs.Value & vbCr
        Next rs
       
        .InsertAfter vbCr
        .InsertAfter "Grammar error count:" & vbTab & inputDoc.GrammaticalErrors.Count
    End With
End Sub
