doug robbins - mvp office apps & services (word) say:

In all Windows applications, CTRL+c will copy the selection, CTRL+x will cut the selection and CTRL+v will paste the contents of the clipboard at the location of the selection.

 

In addition, if you click the right mouse button, you will see the Cut, Copy and Paste items in the short-cut menu.

 

Buttons for those actions are in the Clipboard section of the Home tab of the ribbon.  Perhaps you need to double click on a ribbon tab to expand the ribbon so that you can see them.
