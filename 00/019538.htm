dan at it associates say:

The HUP license is just like the FPP license, it allows two concurrent activations of the product, one on your main computer and one more on your portable computer.

 

First, as best you can, ensure that no other computers besides the one other permitted have your Office installed.

 

Next, rerun the activation wizard, choose Telephone Activation and call the phone number displayed.  When the automated system answers, do NOT say anything or enter anything into the system.  Eventually you'll be offered an option to speak with a live activation representative, or will eventually be connected with a live activation representative.  Explain your circumstances and answer their questions, and assuming they are satisfied that you are using the software in accordance with the licensing terms, they will give you an activation code to enter into your computer.

 

Post back to let us know how it goes.

Yes.  They are stored as pairs, so to delete a pair you just delete the username in the usual way, e.g. from its drop down list.

If you want to stop saving a password for an account and not be prompted about saving it each time you use it you may have to delete the pair and resave just its username.
