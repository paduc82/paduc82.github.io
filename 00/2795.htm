For Canadians: How to use the New Bell Email at Bell.net on your Windows Phone

Bell Canada introduced a new email service last month.  You want to continue to access your email via your Windows Phone.  Your email address and password are the same as before when your bell email account was provided by Microsoft and was actually a Hotmail and later Outlook or Live account.  The email service has been moved from Microsoft servers to Bell's.  When you log in for the first time your email, contacts, calendar and tasks are ported over to the new server but you need to keep your Microsoft account for two reasons: you need access to the MS store via you Windows Phone and you want to keep OneDrive.  Bell will not tell you how to do this because they do not support Windows Phones.

Here is how to access Bell Email on your Windows Phone:

- Add a new email address: input the details you use to login at Bell.net then change the name to so you can tell it from your MS account.  You can use either POP or IMAP.  If you want o access your email from a desktop running Outlook.exe and your windows phone (but no other devices) POP is good enough.  If you want access from more than that use IMAP.  Look on Bell.ca or your Outlook.exe installation for requirements such as incoming/outgoing server names, security, etc.

- Link the new email address to the inbox of your Microsoft account.  You won't be getting any email from that account so you have effectively only one email service.  The MS account is the primary one and it cannot be deleted anyway so this is a reasonable solution.
