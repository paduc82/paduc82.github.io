Dear namiraarnbjorn,
I am an independent advisor, and I am trying to help other users in Community with my experience in Microsoft products.
If your Microsoft account was deleted due to inactivity, that means that you have not been using it for more than 5 years. After it was deleted the "name" for that account was free so anyone could have registered that account in the meantime. So the best option to check if this account exist will be to try to login to it https://outlook.live.com/mail/inbox . Now if you will enter you previous account and you will be brought to the password page, it will mean that someone else already registered that account. If not you will get error saying that this Microsoft account does not exist, so you will be able to recreate it.
Let's say you have recreated the account (whew). The server will "see" that account as new one, with new inbox ( and etc. ) You can try to recover the password for your FB account, but even if the verification code will be send to that email ( that was in the FB system) it might not reach your "new" inbox, but will go to the "ghost old inbox". Anyway it worth a shot!

Sorry for making it long (but at least informative) and I hope that you will be able to retrieve both of your accounts. Fingers crossed!
