OneNote 2013- Not Syncing From Computer To One Drive/Phone - Microsoft Community

thesaltysealion say:

OneNote 2013- Not Syncing From Computer To One Drive/Phone (Resolved)

Good day,

I had an issue where, after changing the names of some of my Notebooks on my computer, they stopped syncing with my OneDrive, and therefore stopped syncing with my phone (Iphone 6).

After contacting the Microsoft Help Desk, and was not able to receive a solution, I found one on my own.  

First, verify that this is your issue

Make sure your Notebooks are syncing successfully:

  • Open OneNote on your computer.
  • Right click on one of your Notebooks and click "Notebook sync status".
  • On the right will show if the Notebook sync status is up-to-day or is failing (indicated by a red "x").  
  • If your Notebooks are not syncing successfully, then use this community to find a different solution to your issue.  If your Notebooks are syncing successfully, continue to the next verification step.

See if the content between OneNote on your computer and OneNote on OneDrive are conflicting:

  • Go to onedrive.live.com and sign in. 
  • Do the titles and content of the Notebooks here match that of which is on your computer?
  • If not, proceed with the troubleshooting document.  If they do match, then use this community to find a different solution for your issue.

Resolve the problem

Relocate your Notebooks locally (do this for each Notebook):

  • Open OneNote on your computer.
  • Right click on the NoteBook and click "Properties".
  • Click the "Change Location" button.
  • Select a location on your computer to store the Notebook. (I created a "OneNote" folder location on my desktop).

Delete your Notebooks on OneDrive (Important- only do this step after you have relocated your Notebooks locally):

  • Go to onedrive.live.com and sign in. 
  • One the landing page you will see your Notebooks.  Click the checkmark at the top right-hand corner of each Notebook.
  • Used the delete option at the top to delete the Notebooks.

Share your locally stored Notebooks with OneDrive (do this for all Notebooks):

  • On OneNote from your computer, click on the Notebook.
  • Click the "File" option in the top left-hand of the program.
  • Click the "Share" option.
  • Click the "Move Notebook" button.  The Notebook will be synced to your OneDrive.

Verify the fix

  • After having done the previous step for all Notebooks, go to OneDrive and verify that all of the Notebooks are appearing with the correct titles and content.
  • (If applicable) Open OneNote on your phone and verify that the newly synced Notebooks are appearing.  If the old Notebooks with the conflicting titles/content are there also, used the option under the tools to close the Notebook and it will not appear again.

Hope this helps!!  :D

Hold CTRL key and click on application icon and don't release the CTRL key till it asks you for Safe mode confirmation.
