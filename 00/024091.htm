It sounds like you might have a Bill of Materials type of application. You have a configuration and choose from multiple items to build that configuration. You need to have, at least, three tables here. One table will list the configuration and the attributes of the configuration.     5. Plugged the phone into the PC.
    6. The Windows Device Recovery Tool recognized the phone and updated the software.

      3. I clicked through the battery warning.
      4. If the "failure" appeared, I would repeat.
