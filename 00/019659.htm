Column chart to show percentage of numbers within a range - Microsoft Community

code numpty say:

Column chart to show percentage of numbers within a range

I have a dynamic named range which will consist of randomly generated numbers between 2 specified numbers, in columns and rows, e.g.

 

The Lowest Number & Highest numbers are entered, along with the number of decimal places and may be different in every instance.

From this data I need to produce a column chart showing (in this instance) what percentage of numbers fall between the integers 59 & 61, 61 & 63, 63 & 65, 65 & 67.

I can't even work out how to arrive at the percentages, let alone the chart.

Please help.

hansv mvp say:

Let's say your range is named MyRange.

We'll create a data range for the chart in B1:C5. (If you use another range, shift the cell references as needed)

In A2, enter 59, in A3 61, in A4 63 and in A5 65. You can, of course, use formulas based on your Lowest and Higest values instead of entering the values.

In B2, enter the formula =A2&"-"&(A2+2) and fill down to B5

In C2, enter the formula =SUMPRODUCT((MyRange>=A2)*(MyRange<A2+2))/COUNT(MyRange) and fill down to C5. Format these numbers as a percentage

Enter captions in B1 and C1.

Create a column chart based on B1:C5.
