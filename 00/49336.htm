Hi sdog879

It is a really bad idea in Windows 10 to store files/folders in the Recycle bin. Windows 10 has a function called Storage Sense, which has a habit of turning itself on, when it senses low disk space and the first thing it empties is the Recycle Bin

If EaseUS was unable to recover your files, they may not be recoverable . .

Once you have exhausted all possibilities of recovering your files, your recycle Bin may be corrupt and that will need to be fixed

Please only do this once you have either recovered or been unable to recover your files, because this will delete the old Recycle Bin and create a new one:

Open a Command Prompt window as administrator:
(Click your Start Button, type cmd, right click Command Prompt in the resulting list and choose 'Run as Administrator')

Paste the following command and hit Enter:

rd /s /q C:\$Recycle.bin

Close the Command Prompt window and restart your system

When your system starts up:

Create a new text file on your desktop and delete it to recreate a new recycle bin on your C Drvie
