The File tab only looks like it's selected because it's dark -- but it's always dark. If it was really selected, you would see the big stripe down the left side with Info, New, Open, etc. instead of seeing your document.

What you're seeing is that the ribbon is minimized. When you single-click a tab such as the Home tab, the ribbon temporarily opens until you click a button on it, then it minimizes again.

To make it stay visible, double-click any of the tabs. (Accidentally double-clicking a tab is how you got it minimized -- it toggles back and forth.) Other ways include:

  • Right-click any tab and check/uncheck "Collapse the ribbon" on the menu.
  • Press Ctrl and F1 together.
  • Click the little up-arrow at the right end of the ribbon while it's visible (this only collapses the ribbon; there isn't any arrow to expand it).
  • Click the  icon in the top right corner of the Word window, next to the minimize and maximize icons, and choose the Show Tabs and Commands item in the menu.
