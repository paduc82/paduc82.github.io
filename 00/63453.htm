a. user say:

Here's how to know for 100% sure if your computer can upgrade to Windows 10:

1- Make a list of all the hardware components in your computer. Don't forget things like the chipset and the storage subsystem.

2- Go to the websites of the manufacturers of the hardware you listed above and find out if the manufacturers are supporting Windows 10. If you get a positive response for all your hardware components, go on to the next step.

3- Make a list of all the software installed in your computer.

4- Go to the websites of the developers of the software you listed above,  and find out if the developers are supporting Windows 10. If you get a positive response for all your software applications, go on to the next step.

5- Go to the community forum for your computer (usually found on the manufacturer's website) and ask for advice about your particular model. If others have successfully upgraded, then you're good to go.

P.S. I did all the above. It was several days' work, and worth every minute.
