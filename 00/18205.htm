jeeped say:
It sounds like you have converted your data to a Table.

            

The little reversed L mark in the lower corner (circled in yellow) notes the extent of the Table. You have a couple of options.
  • If you select any cell in the defined table range, you can access the Table Tools menu from the ribbon and choose Design, Tools, Convert to Range. This removed the table definition and you are left with a standard range of cells.
  • You can grab the little reversed L and drag it down to fully encompass your data. The table definition will remain but it will be expanded to include the rows that are no longer referenced by your VLOOKUP().
I prefer to avoid table definitions myself, simply using standard cell ranges but tables do have their advantages; notably automatic re-sizing to reference data inserted immediately below their last row of values.

  6. Open Control Panel and look for Internet Options.
