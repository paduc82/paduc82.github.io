Hi Paul,

Can you tell us more about your concern? When you say that that the icon for OneDrive is missing, did you mean it is no where to be found under the taskbar? Have you tried accessing OneDrive by searching it via Cortana? And when you say you have lost access, what error message are you getting? We need these information so we can help you with your concern.

In the meantime, if what you meant was to see the OneDrive icon on your taskbar, kindly follow the steps I have provided below:

  1. Click Start.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Go to Personalization.
  4. Click Taskbar found on the left pane.
  5. Look for Select which icons appear on the taskbar.
  6. Make sure that OneDrive is turned ON.
  7. Close the window.
  8. Check if you already see OneDrive icon on the taskbar.
  9. If not, restart your computer.

Kindly provide us with a feedback.
