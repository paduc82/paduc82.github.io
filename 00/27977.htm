How Do We Disable Clutter For Internal Email? - Microsoft Community

anthonyphillips4324 say:

How Do We Disable Clutter For Internal Email?

Our office is using a feature called Clutter.  Over the past couple weeks, I've been receiving complaints by multiple employees that emails from our own users are ending up in clutter.  How do we keep email sent from our domain, from ending up in our Clutter?  Some people like Clutter, but are missing important internal messages that Clutter is automatically sorting.

alexsun_2012 say:

Hi Anthony,

The email server keeps track of the email you read and the ones you don't . Once Clutter is turned on, it takes action on all the incoming emails automatically including the internal emails. End users can disable the Clutter feature from Outlook Web App-> Settings Options > Mail > Automatic processing > Clutter. However, this will also disable Clutter for the external emails as well.

If your users want to keep using the Clutter for the external emails, my suggestion is to create transport rules or inbox rules on the internal emails. Clutter respects your existing email rules. If you have created rules to organize your email those rules continue to be applied and Clutter won’t act on those messages.

Here is an official article you can refer to that suggests admins to create a transport rule to bypass Clutter: https://technet.microsoft.com/en-us/library/dn896639(v=exchg.150).aspx.

If you need further assistance, please let us know.

Thanks,
Alex
