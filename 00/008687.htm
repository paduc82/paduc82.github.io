The big change that took place a couple of weeks ago is that the domain outlook.com suddenly took on a massive new role - as host to a new collection of free email accounts to supplement the othe Microsoft domains for free accounts such as hotmail.*, live.* and msn.com. At the same time, a new user interface was introduced.

Prior to this, the outlook.com domain had been used for several years to act as a proxy for, for example, mail accounts for Outlook Live accounts such as your UQ account which was hosted on the University's Exchange server.
