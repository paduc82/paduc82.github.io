john e fredricks say:

There is no option to find all empty text boxes in the excel application.

 However, you may search for objects in a sheet.

Press F5 key and Click on special and then Click on the object and Click on OK. This would highlight all objects which inlcudes text boxes and you may hold the CTRL button to unselect the object which you dont want to delete.
