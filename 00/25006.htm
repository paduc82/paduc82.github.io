john korchok say:

Word has had this feature for many years. Bookmarks do exactly what you describe.  Select text (it can be just a space character or a paragraph mark), then press Command + Shift +F5 (or choose Insert>Bookmarks from the macOS menu or Ribbon) to open the Bookmark dialog. Give it a logical name, then click on Add.

Then to navigate among bookmarks, Press Command + Shift + F5 again, select a bookmark and click on Go To.

You can make bookmarks visible as gray square brackets by opening Word>Preferences>View and checking Bookmarks.
