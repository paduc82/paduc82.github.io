suzanne s. barnhill say:
There is no way to do this without adjusting the text manually. I suggest you wait to do this until editing is complete. Then put as much text as will fit on the page before the landscape section and continue it after the landscape section. If you want to give the appearance of wrapping, that is, if the previous page needs to end in the middle of a paragraph, just break it there and note the following additional workarounds that may be required: 
  1. If the text is justified, press Shift+Enter at the end of the last line on the previous page and then format the paragraph mark (on the next line) as 1 pt. or hidden.
  2. If the style has a first-line indent, then apply an unindented style to the paragraph that begins the page after the landscape section.
