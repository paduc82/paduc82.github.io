jeeped say:

Here's my formula for one string: =IF(ISERR(FIND("CBC",M7)),"",LEFT(M7,15)) ...


Try,
=IF(SUM(--ISNUMBER(SEARCH({"CBC","PT"},M7))),LEFT(M7,15),"")
... or case-sensitive search with,
=IF(SUM(--ISNUMBER(FIND({"CBC","PT"},M7))),LEFT(M7,15),"")
... or with CBC and PT in Z1:Z2 then,
=IF(SUM(--ISNUMBER(SEARCH($Z$1:$Z$2,M7))),LEFT(M7,15),"")

All three of the above are array formulas and require Ctrl+Shift+Enter‚Üµ.
