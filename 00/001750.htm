Try this (from here: http://skp.mvps.org/ppt00030.htm)

Sub RefreshSlide()
   Dim lSlideIndex As Long
   lSlideIndex = SlideShowWindows(1).View.CurrentShowPosition
   SlideShowWindows(1).View.GotoSlide lSlideIndex

and you can simplify your code to:

Private Sub RollDice_Click()

  End With
 RefreshSlide
