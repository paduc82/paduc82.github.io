robert__p say:

hi joe,

based on your description, i understand you want to redirect message and leave a copy of the message with the transport rule.

this can be done in office 365. here are the steps:

in mail flow, click + to create a new rule…, select more options. set apply this rule if... – the recipient is this person and select the recipient. set do the following... – add recipient to the bcc box and add the address you would like redirect the message to. then add the exception you want under the except if... area. click save at last. a screenshot for your reference:

as for your requirement “but if the message was sent to *** Email address is removed for privacy ***, then do not redirect it.”, if you don’t want the emails sent to *** Email address is removed for privacy *** redirected, just do not add *** Email address is removed for privacy *** under the recipient area of the transport rule.

please let me know if anything is unclear or misunderstood.
