I understand that you wish to use your webcam whilst the screen is locked. In order to enable Camera on Lock Screen, try these steps and check if they are helpful.

  1. Move your mouse cursor to top-right or bottom-right corner of screen to access Charms Bar and click on Settings option.
  2. Now click on "Change PC settings" option given at the bottom.
  3. Open PC & devices and click on Lock screen.
  4. To enable use of Camera on Lock Screen, set the option to "On".

However, there is no app published by Microsoft, as such, to click pictures automatically on lock screen for spying purposes. I would suggest you to use your favorite search engine to search for the app for spying purposes.

Hope this information is helpful. Do let us know if you need any further assistance, we'll be glad to assist you.
