Hi Mario,

 

Based on your description, you do not want to use OneDrive sync client to sync OneDrive for business files on your system, but you would like to open files in File Explorer without duplicating the files on your system.  In this scenario, you can use View in File Explorer feature. For this follow the steps below:

  1. Open Internet Explorer, navigate to OneDrive for browser
  2. Click the View menu and then click View in File Explorer.

Note: View in File Explorer/Open with Explorer works only in Internet Explorer 10 or 11. For Windows 10, Edge is the default, but Internet Explorer 11 is also installed. View in File Explorer/Open with Explorer does not work with Edge, Chrome, or FireFox. You can use Internet Explorer to do your file transfers, and then go back to using your preferred browser (if not IE).
