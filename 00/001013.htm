Hi Promila,

It's possible that Office isn't properly associated with the Microsoft account that you used to renew the Office 365 subscription. To associate the correct Microsoft account to your Office apps, we suggest following these steps:

Note: Save and close all open files before doing this to avoid data loss.

  1. Open an Office app, like Word.
  2. Click File.
  3. Click Account,
  4. Click Sign out.
  5. Click Yes.
  6. Close the Office program and restart your computer.
  7. Open an Office app.
  8. Click File.
  9. Click Account.
  10. Click Sign in.
  11. Enter the credentials for the Microsoft Account that you used to renew the Office 365 subscription.

Let us know if you're able to activate Office properly after following these steps.
