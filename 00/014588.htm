  Just to make sure we're not overlooking anything, did you get any error messages when downloading the song on your phone?  The purchase (confirmed in email you mentioned) does entitle you to a download the complete track, but to re-start this download you will have to access your purchase history in the Zune software client on your PC.  If you don't already have it installed, the software is available at http://www.xbox.com/en-US/Live/Partners/Zune.  Accessing your purchase history to restart a failed download can be done with these steps: 

 

  • Sign in to the Zune software using the same account as on your phone.  On your phone, this can be confirmed by tapping Settings > Email + Accounts, typically the Microsoft Account is the first one listed.
  • In the Zune software, click Settings > Account > Purchase History > Songs.

  • After downloading, sync the new song to your phone.

a. user say:

Hi 1smallstep,

 

For E1 plan, it provide online Business services but doesn't provide Office apps. If you only need basic online features (Exchange online plan 1, mailbox is 50 GB, doesn't contain Exchange online plan 2's features like In-place/Litigation hold, Data Loss Protection, etc.) it would be a proper plan for you and it cost $8.00/user.

 

Both E3 ($20) and E5 ($35) contains Office 365 ProPlus app and advanced online plans like Exchange online plan 2. These will give you more flexibility and secure measures in your business. Additional, E5 contains extra add-ons like Skype for Business Phone System which is mandatory for Calling plan and Audio Conferencing, Advanced ATP features, etc.

 

PowerApps is a service that lets you build business apps that run in a browser or on a phone or tablet, and no coding experience is required. With PowerApps documentation, you get expert information and answers to address your needs, regardless of how you use PowerApps. For Power app licensing, please refer to this article.
