How do I uninstall a printer or other software?

Original title: Uninstalling software

I am new to windows 8. How do I uninstall a printer or other software?

john rubdy say:

Hi Richard,

To uninstall the printer in Windows 8 follow the steps below:

  1. Press the Windows key and X key on the keyboard at the same time
  2. Click Control Panel
  3. Click View Devices and Printers under Hardware and Sound
  4. Select the printer to be uninstalled, and click Remove Device at the top of the window.
  5. A message shows to confirm the removal of the printer. Click Yes to remove from Devices and Printers.
  6. After removing the printer, the printer driver needs to be removed as well. Click the windows and X key on the keyboard.
  7. Select Programs and Features
  8. In programs, Select the model printer driver and click Uninstall
  9. A message will display to confirm uninstalling the driver. Click Yes and the installation wizard will open. Follow the onscreen instructions.

If you want to remove installed softwareâ€™s you can follow step 6 to 9.

Hope this helps. If you need further assistance with Windows, let us know and will be glad to help.
