Sometimes folks think that by installing more and different AV programs on their system at the same time, their system will be better protected - but what usually happens is those programs start to interfere with each other or they start to fight to see who is going to be the "boss".

You should pick one that you think is going to do the best job and stick with that, but generally never more than one at the same time.

Many AV programs have gotten too complicated to be removed from Add/Remove Programs and have their own uninstaller programs and avast! and AVG are not exception.  If you do not use the special uninstall programs from the manufacturer, they may not get uninstalled properly and there could be its and pieces leftover on your system.

If your SR works okay in Safe Mode that hints that there still may be something from avast! and/or AVG that is loading in Normal mode that prevents SR from working since these programs may be trying to "protect" your system from changes.

It could be that there is something else loading in Normal mode that is interfering and even MS hints in their KB articles that if SR doesn't work in Normal mode, you just might always have to use Safe Mode to restore.

http://www.avg.com/us-en/utilities

The uninstaller for avast! is here:

http://www.avast.com/uninstall-utility
