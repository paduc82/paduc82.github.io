SFC scan would scan for corrupt system files on the computer and replace them.

 

Follow the steps:

 

a. Press “Windows Key + Q” to openCharms Bar.

b. Type “cmd” without quotes in the search box.

c. On the left pane, right clickon the “cmd” option and select “Run as Administrator”.

d. Type “sfc /scannow” without quotes and hitEnter.
