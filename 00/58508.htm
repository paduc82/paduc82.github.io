solaterra say:

Slimes are found most frequently 4 blocks above bedrock. Dig down to 4 blocks above bedrock. Then dig out a 50 x 50 square chamber and install plenty of torches so that none of the other creatures spawn except Slimes. Digging several of these 50 x 50 chambers 4 blocks above bedrock in different locations of your map is one of the most effective ways to find Slimes.

Plus. Make sure the difficulty setting on your map is set to Easy or Higher. Slimes, like other creatures won't spawn on Peaceful Mode. Finding Slimes is alot like prospecting for oil or any other natural resource. You have to several chambers in several locations through-out your map then just wait.

Check each location often. I have found that most of the time that Slimes won't spawn in an area when you're just hanging-out waiting for them to spawn. So the stratagy in my opinion is to goto different parts of your map and dig-out several 50 x 50 chambers that are 4 blocks above bedrock making sure they are all well lit with torches. Then just leave them and go build some buildings or other structures. Then check back on each of the chambers as often as you can. Then after awhile some of the chambers that you've dug should start to produce Slimes.

 

Also. When you finally find some Slimes in some of the chambers. Make sure to have your Bow and Arrow ready. Using a Bow and Arrow to take out Slimes will produce the maximum number of slime balls from each Slime creature.

 

I hope this helps you out.
