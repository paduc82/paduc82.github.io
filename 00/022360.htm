Hi Nancy,

First of all, you will want to navigate to sway.com and sign in as the owner of that Sway.  Next, find the Sway on your My Sways page and click to open it.  This should take you into the edit view of your Sway.  The first pane you see is the Storyline and you should scroll down it until you find an image you want to change.  Then, in some order, you will want to delete the old Image card and insert a new one.  For the new Image you can choose from a variety of sources in the Insert pane that will appear after you insert the new Image card.

Here are you getting your information? Office 365 (2019) if subscribed to Home version is either $9.99 month to month or $99.00 for a year at a time.  Now that is for Home users (or possibly small Business). I don't know the price of the permanent license version. 

Microsoft also has Business oriented Subscriptions and Permanent License versions. You will have to go to Microsoft and look in Business software section find out Prices.
