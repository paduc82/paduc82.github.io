That can be very misleading ... it usually occurs when there is also a Windows Defender definitions update to be installed, and the "awaiting install" refers to it after it has downloaded, when the new build has not yet begun to show as downloading ... then, after a little waiting time, the "downloading" notice appears, referring to the new build.
