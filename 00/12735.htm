This fix worked like a charm for me in Word 2010 with Acrobat Pro 9.5. Neither "Save & Send to PDF/XPS" or printing to PDF (both under the File tab) worked. Here's what did work:

1.   In Word, click on "Save As" (also under File)

2.   Under "Save as type" choose "PDF."

3.   Click the Options tab. A new pop-up window will appear.

4.   Uncheck "Document structure tags for accessibility" and click OK.

5.   Back on the "Save As" page, click "Save."

Doing this, all my hyperlinks in the Word footer (attached to thumbnail images) worked fine in the PDF.
