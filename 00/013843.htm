roady [mvp] say:
  1. Create a separate mail profile (can be without an email account).
  2. Start Outlook with that mail profile.
  3. Connect your pst-files to it.
  4. Let Outlook index the pst-files.
  5. Restart Outlook with your regular mail profile.
  6. When you now need to search for an archived item, open the Start Menu and type;
    store:mapi <your search query>
