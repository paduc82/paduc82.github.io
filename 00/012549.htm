Public Sub listForms()
    Dim f As Form
    For Each f In Forms
        Debug.Print "[" & f.Name & "]"
    Next f
End Sub

Public Sub listControlsOnForm(ByVal strFormName As String)
    Dim c As Control
    For Each c In Forms(strFormName).Controls
        Debug.Print "[" & c.Name & "]"
    Next c
End Sub

In the Immediate Window enter:

listForms

and hit Enter.

It will list the running forms, so you can inspect the name of what you think is FrmDateRange.

listControls "FrmDateRange"

and it will list the control names, hopefully including BeginningDate.

The reason for the brackets is to catch any spurious leading or trailing spaces, or other funny characters.
