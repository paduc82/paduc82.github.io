As an alternative to a VBA solution, what's the criteria or formula for the conditional formatting that leads to the cell being coloured?

In line with your original idea of "a formula at the top of each column", conditional formatting of any type works by evaluating some condition and seeing if the answer is true or false, and if true it applies the conditional formatting. So by counting the cells that evaluate to true, it should be relatively easy to get an indicator at the top of the column.

For example, in A1 something like =IF(COUNTIF(A2:A200,">225")>0,"There are pink cells","There are no pink cells")
