Hi Narrotam,

You can change the name or customize the tags any time. Follow the steps below:

  1. Open Outlook, select Home.
  2. In the Tags group, click Categorize then click All Categories. (Note: For calendar items, the Tags group appears on the Appointment or Meeting tab.)
  3. Click a category, and choose Rename.
  4. Type the new name for the color category, and then press Enter.
