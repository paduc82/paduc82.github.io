Sorry, this feature is gone for now. As a workaround, you can record your presentation with Quicktime Player:

  1. Open QuickTime Player.
  2. Choose File>New Screen Recording. A small setup window appears.
  3. Click the red dot in the setup window to begin the recording.
  4. Start your presentation in Slide Show mode.
  5. When the presentation ends, close it, right-click on the QuickTime icon in the dock and choose Stop Screen Recording.
  6. Edit the recording in Quicktime, to trim off the beginning and end.
  7. Save the video file.
