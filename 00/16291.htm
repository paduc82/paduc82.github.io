The short answer is no. You cannot animate part of a paragraph. However, you can fake it. Leave a blank space where the answer is supposed to go.   • Go to Settings on the phone and turn Wi-Fi "Off"
  • Power down the phone.
  • Wait, because within minutes the phone will turn back on (perhaps because it is connected to the computer?)
  • Go  to Settings on the phone and turn Wi-Fi "On"
  • Input your router password.
