=IFERROR(INDEX($D$2:$D$100,MATCH(0,INDEX(COUNTIF(A$1:A1,$D$2:$D$100)+($D$2:$D$100=""),,),0)),"")

Note: Update "A$1:A1" in the formula to the column where you wish to extract uniques ex. if you want to extract uniques in column C from C2 downwards then update this to "C$1:C1".

Enter below formula in cell B2 & copy down - this returns the SUM of each account no from column L;

=IF(A2="","",SUMIF($D$2:$D$100,A2,$L$2:$L$100))
