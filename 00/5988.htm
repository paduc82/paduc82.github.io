Hi MandyShaw,

 

Whether your emails are stuck in Outbox and cannot reopen the email in Outbox for at least a minute? Are you able to move it to draft folder?

 

Cancel All button is used for cancelling the pending send/receive request. If emails stuck in Outbox, generally, you may try the following steps to move the emails from Outbox to draft folder then re-edit it:

 

1. In Outlook, select the Send/Receive tab, and then select Work Offline, this stops Outlook from trying to send all email messages.

 

2. Restart Outlook->Select the Outbox-> Move the message to the Drafts folder. You can either drag the message to the Drafts folder or you can right-click the message, select Move > Other Folder > Drafts folder from the list.

 

3. You may change to a different network environment->de-select Work Offline, this restarts your connection, and then you can send the edited emails.

I was recently having issues with SkyDrive syncing too... My weird issue was that the folder type Documents folder on SkyDrive got changed to Pictures. See if that might fix your issue.

  1. Go to SkyDrive on your computer, not your phone.
  2. Open the Documents folder.
  3. On the right side of your screen, click Information to get information about the folder.
  4. At the bottom of the list of information, there's an option called "Folder type". If it says "Photos", click "change" and choose the "Documents" type and click save.
  5. On your phone, go to Settings>applications>Office and tap reset Office **WARNING** resetting Office will delete any files stored locally, so be sure to back them up by emailing them.
  6. Go to the Office Hub and try syncing files with SkyDrive
