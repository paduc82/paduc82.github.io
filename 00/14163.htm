bob jones aka: cybertaz say:

I believe the problem is that although the Document Dimension of the Word document is 6x9 the Default Paper Size for your printer is set to 8.5x11. When you generate the PDF the 6x9 content is being printed to PDF on the larger paper size. Try this...

  1. From Word go to File> Print
  2. Open the Copies & Pages dropdown
  3. Select Paper Handling
  4. Check the Scale to fit paper size box
  5. Open the Destination Paper Size list
  6. Select your custom 6x9 Preset
  7. Click the PDF button
  8. Select either Save as PDF or Save as Adobe PDF
