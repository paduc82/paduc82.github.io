jay freedman say:

Hi David,

You're correct, quite a lot of that recording is unnecessary. The macro recorder throws in most of the settings in the dialog, even when their values are the defaults. A trimmed-down version that works exactly the same is this:

Sub WordEnd()
'
' Go to end of word
'
    With Selection
        .MoveRight Unit:=wdWord, Count:=1
        With .Find
            .Text = "^$"
            .Forward = False
            .Execute
        End With
        .MoveRight Unit:=wdCharacter, Count:=1
    End With
End Sub

Older versions of Word tried to preserve the last setting used in the Bullets and Numbering dialog box. To revert to the factory defaults, you can click each of the number (or bullet) formats and then click the Reset button.
