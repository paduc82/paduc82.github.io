To change or disable the sound, please follow the steps below:

1. Right click on the speaker icon at the right bottom corner. 

2. Click on sounds.

3. Under "program events", look for "default beep" and highlight it.
4. Under "sounds", choose "none" from the drop down list.
5. Click ok and check if it helps.

You can always use this feature to change the sound scheme for different events according to your preference.

To change the sleep setting, please follow the steps below.

1. Type power options in the search bar and open it. 
2. Click on "change when the computer sleeps" in the left pane.
3. Choose the time from the drop down list.

4. Click on "save changes"

  • Engaging in communications that claim or imply that you are Xbox staff
  • Having a Gamertag or profile content that contains hate speech or profanity
  • Using in game exploits to gain an unfair advantage over other players
  • Hacking, Modding or Cheating
