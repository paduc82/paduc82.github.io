    Me.cboShipperID.DefaultValue = """" & DLookup("ID","Shippers") & """"

don varnau say:

Hi,

 

If the Status Bar is not showing at the bottom of the Internet Explorer window, right-click the IE toolbar and enable Status Bar. Use Zoom on the right side of the Status Bar to get the page back to 100%.

 

Ctrl key and 0 (number zero) will usually return zoom to 100%.  [Ctrl and +] [Ctrl and -} will also zoom in/out.

 

If the chosen Zoom setting isn't used for new windows and tabs, go to Internet Options> Advanced and check "Reset zoom level for new windows and tabs."
