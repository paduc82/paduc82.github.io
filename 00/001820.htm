amit tandon say:

Presuming: Cell E1 contains the start date. Cell Q1 contains the annual salary (50000). The Month / Year ("April 2018", "May 2018", "June 2018") are entered as text in cells A2, A3, A4, and so on ...

Option 1:

Enter below formula in cell B2 & copy down:

=MAX((DATE(RIGHT(A2,4),MONTH(1&LEFT(A2,LEN(A2)-5))+1,)-MAX($E$1,DATE(RIGHT(A2,4),MONTH(1&LEFT(A2,LEN(A2)-5)),1))+1),0)/DAY(DATE(RIGHT(A2,4),MONTH(1&LEFT(A2,LEN(A2)-5))+1,))*$Q$1/12

Option 2 (using DateValue & EOMONTH):

Enter below formula in cell C2 & copy down:

=MAX((EOMONTH(DATEVALUE(A2),0)-MAX($E$1,DATEVALUE(A2))+1),0)/DAY(EOMONTH(DATEVALUE(A2),0))*$Q$1/12

Note: Though you have mentioned the monthly amounts as 4,246.58 for May / June this should actually be 4,166.67 because for a full month the salary should be 50,000/12 = 4,166.67. The above formulas return accordingly. If the date of joining (in cell E1 is 22nd May 2018, then for "May 2018" salary is computed for 10 days - 1,344.09).
