zorvek say:

We're going to use the MONTH function to do this:

=SUMPRODUCT((MONTH(A2:A366)=MONTH(Z1))*B2:B366)

The above assumes that your dates are in column A starting in row 2 and the values you want to sum are in column B in the same rows. The date you are entering for the query (filter) is in cell Z1. The formula will sum all values in column B where the month of the date in column A is equal to the month in the date in Z1.
