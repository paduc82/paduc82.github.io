Microsoft word - disable automatic capitalization of first letter of sentences

When I type a document every sentence begins with a capital letter. How do I undo this?

***Subject edited for clarity by the moderator.***
[Original Title: Microsoft word]

doug robbins - mvp office apps & services (word) say:

If after you press space following the first word in the new sentence, the "AutoCorrect" that is capitalizing the first letter will be undone.

To prevent it happening in the first place, go to Office Button, Word Options>Proofing>AutoCorrect Options and on the AutoCorrect tab of the AutoCorrect dialog, uncheck the box for "Capitalize first letter of sentences"
