chris de herrera say:

Hi,

What is the exact error code you see when you sync?  Do your Contacts have embedded attachments in the notes?  Are the notes for the Contacts longer than 16,000 characters?  These can cause problems with synchronization.

 

Also, FYI, ActiveSync stops synchronizing when it has an error.  So to identify an issue with your contacts, try the following:

1. Create another Contacts folder.

2. Move the Contacts to the new folder.

3. Sync - this will remove any contacts that did sync and this is normal.

4. Move the Contacts from the new folder back in small groups.

5. Sync - this will add the contacts.

6. Repeat at step 4 until you identify the contacts that won't sync.

jomel_g say:

Hello

To delete a contact, below are the steps on how you can proceed:

  1. Sign into Skype.
  2. In your contact list, find the contact you want to remove. Right-click the contact name and select Remove from Contacts. Then confirm by clicking Remove.
  3. The contact is removed from your contact list but will still appear in the Recent tab with a question mark.

The steps on how you can edit your contacts' information, please follow the below steps:

  1. Right-click the name of the contact you want to edit.
  2. Select View Profile.
  3. On the pop-up window, you can:
  • Modify the name of your contact (available for both Skype for Windows 10 and classic Skype)
  • Add a phone number (available for both Skype for Windows 10 and classic Skype)
  • Remove and block a contact (Only available in the Skype for Windows 10 version)

I hope this answers your query.
