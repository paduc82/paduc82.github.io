john korchok say:

The Slide Master is the larger slide at the top. Below it are the Slide Layouts. Once inheritance gets badly messed up, it can be difficult to get the slide layouts to take formatting information from the master again.

You can try opening the Slide Master, deleting the main text area, then using Master Layout and checking Text to reinsert it.

If that doesn't clear the problem, you may have to insert a fresh Slide Master, format it correctly, recreate the Slide Layouts, then apply the new layouts to the existing presentation.
