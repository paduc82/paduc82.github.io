vijay a. verma say:
Use the below Array formula where E7 has D

=SUM(($B$2:INDEX($B$2:$B$1000,IFERROR(MATCH(1,($B$2:$B$1000<0)*($A$2:$A$1000=$E$7),0),ROW($B$1000))-1)*($A$2:INDEX($B$2:$B$1000,IFERROR(MATCH(1,($B$2:$B$1000<0)*($A$2:$A$1000=$E$7),0),ROW($B$1000))-1)=$E$7)))

Note - Array Formula is not entered by pressing ENTER after entering your formula but by pressing CTRL+SHIFT+ENTER.
