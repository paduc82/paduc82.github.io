diane poremsky mvp (slipstick.com) say:

Remove all delegates rule with MFCMapi  - http://mfcmapi.codeplex.com/ - (use a non-cached profile) and try sharing calendar again in outlook.

Double click the Mailbox to open and expand Root Container, Top of the information store, Inbox folder. Right-click the "Inbox" folder, and then click "Display Rules Table".  Delete the Delegate Rule with "PR_RULE_PROVIDER" column property shows "Schedule+EMS Interface".

If this doesn't help, the admin needs to use pfadmin to reset the dasl's.
