You can pin apps and data to your Start Screen and your lock screen.  Discover and use images from the web to add to your Windows lock screen or pin your favorite apps to show Weather, Finance, and Sports data on the live tiles in your Start Screen.

 

How to add Bing App images to your Lock Screen without having to leave the Settings page:

 

1.       Go to the Charms bar by pressing Windows key + C

2.       Click Settings then Change PC Settings

3.       Click Personalize and choose Lock Screen

4.       Click Browse and you can choose any Bing image as your Lock Screen image

5.       Once your choice is made, click Choose Picture

 

How to add Weather data to the Start Screen:

 

1.       Go to the Weather App and right click

2.       At the top, click on Places and select the City to be added to your Start Screen

3.       Right click again and at the bottom, choose Pin to Start and confirm

 

How to add Weather alerts to the Lock Screen:

 

1.       There are two settings for lock screen apps in the Personalize section of PC Settings.

          a.      The first setting is to Choose apps to run in the background and show quick stats and

                   notifications, even when your screen is locked.  If you add the Weather app to this list,

                   the Weather app will only display a notification if your chosen default city has an alert such

                   as Severe Weather Alerts or other such warnings issued by NOAA.  Otherwise, you won‚Äôt see

                   anything from the Weather app on the lock screen.

          b.      The second setting is to Choose an app to display detailed status.  Only one app can be

                   specified here.  If you select the Weather app here, then you will see the current weather for

                   your chosen city next to the time on the lock screen.

2.       Go to the Charms bar by pressing Windows key + C

3.       Click Settings then Change PC Settings

4.       Click Personalize and choose Lock Screen

5.       Your two options will be under Lock screen apps

 

How to add Finance data to the Start Screen:

 

1.       Go to the Finance app and right click

2.       Click Watchlist at the top

3.       Right click on the stock in the list that you want to add to your Start Screen

4.       At the bottom click on Pin to Start and confirm

 

How to add Sports data to the Start Screen:

 

1.       Go to the Sports app and right click

2.       Go to the specific League or teams page you want to add

3.       Right click and select Pin to Start, then confirm
