You'll be able to find the problem more quickly if you turn on non-printing characters:

  1. In Word, click on the Office button, then on Word Options.
  2. On the Display panel, check Show all formatting marks. OK out.

Those are the most likely culprits, but if those don't solve it, tell us what you do see, or post a screen shot here and we'll narrow it down.

tom van stiphout say:

Access does not keep track of who changed what when, so it does not know if a participant count was changed. It is up to you the developer to add a field (say: LastUpdated) and set it to Now each time an update is made. A good event to do that is the Form_BeforeUpdate event, or if you only want to know that the ParticipantCount field was updated then the ParticipantCount_AfterUpdate can be used.
