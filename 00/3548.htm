Hello Allan,

From the provided description, I understand that you want to know how to create/attach reminder for sending emails.

I will help you with this issue.

First create the reminder in Outlook and then send it as an attachment informing the recipients to copy it to their calendar.

1) Create the reminder in mail originator's Outlook.

2) Compose the email and click on Attach Item > Outlook Item > select Calendar > now select the corresponding reminder and click on OK > add recipients and send it.

3) Inform the recipient to open the reminder attachment and click on Copy to My Calendar.

4) Now check whether reminder displays in Outlook calendar.
