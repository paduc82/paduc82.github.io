joeu2004 say: Ean wrote:

I have two cells and need to work out the time difference between them in hours - how would I write that?


Is this what you are looking for?



The formula in C2 is shown:  =(B2-A2)*24.  Copy it down the column and format as Number with 2 decimal places.

Excel dates are represented as integers (days since 12/31/1899), and time is represented as a fraction of 24 hours.  So 1 hour is 1/24; 1 minute is 1/1440; and 1 second is 1/86400.
