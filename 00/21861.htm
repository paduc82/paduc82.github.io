Try this formula in cell D2:

=TEXTJOIN(", ",TRUE,IF(E2:AE2<>"",E$1:AE$1,""))

john korchok say:

For each slide, when you click on View>Slide Master, it takes you to the Slide Layout associated with that slide. The Slide Layout is the intermediate stage between the Master Slide and your final slides. If the footer and/or slide number placeholder are missing from the Slide Layout, it can't display on the slide.

Choose View>Slide Master and check Slide Master>Master Layout>Footers. If it's checked already, but a footer or slide number is missing, uncheck, then re-check it. If the footer or slide number was missing, PowerPoint will add one.
