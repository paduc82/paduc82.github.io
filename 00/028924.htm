OMG!!!

I think Kimi's advice is terrible! Do not follow it!

The problem is simple. If Kimi actually used office or paid attention to the questions and answers here in the forum, Kimi would have known what to tell you.

Here's what I think is happening.

A file was open in Word, and Word crashed while the file was open and the file was damaged.

When you open Word, Word uses AutoRecover to locate any files you had open when Word crashed, and it tries to resurrect the file and restore you to a point shortly before the crash. The trouble with this procedure is that if the file that Word is trying to restore is bad and causes a crash, you wind up in an endless loop where Word crashes every time you try to open Word. I think that's what's happening here.

The cure is simple. Hold the Shift key down when you click the Word icon in your dock to open Word. This is called starting Word in safe mode. I bet doing this will fix the problem you are having with Word.
