Sum totals relating to weekdays

mike h.. say:

Hi,

 

With dates in A2:A31 and mileage in B2:B32

 

 

Sum of weekdays

 

=SUMPRODUCT((WEEKDAY(A2:A32,2)<=5)*(B2:B32))

 

sum for Saturdays

 

=SUMPRODUCT((WEEKDAY(A2:A32,2)=6)*(B2:B32))

Hi,

 

Because I used the optional argument of 2 in the weekday formula then Sunday becomes 7 and not 1 so weekdays 1 to 5 are Mon to Fri inclusive.

1 or omitted Numbers 1 (Sunday) through 7 (Saturday). Behaves like previous versions of Microsoft Excel.
2 Numbers 1 (Monday) through 7 (Sunday).
3 Numbers 0 (Monday) through 6 (Sunday).

suzanne s. barnhill say:
When you press Ctrl+Spacebar (ResetChar), you're applying the Default Paragraph Font to the text. Since in this case that is defined as Italic, this shortcut does not remove italic formatting. In my opinion, it should be possible to define a character style that is explicitly Bold, Not Italic, but in fact it does have to be Default Paragraph Font + Font: Bold, Italic to work. Unfortunately, this means that it will work properly only in your italic paragraph style; elsewhere it will actually apply Bold Italic formatting.

kenneth benson say: Figured it out, and it's really bass-ackwards. Italic is a toggle. If you apply an italic character style to italic text, you get roman.
