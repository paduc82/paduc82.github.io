------------
Go to Settings >> Phone Settings >>Keyguard settings

Select Automatic Keyguard and Choose ON. Now set the delay time after which your phone is to be locked(Normal 00:10 which locks keypad after 10 seconds of inactivity at the standby screen). To unlock press Menu and * without interval.

--------------------------
Go to Settings >> Phone Settings >>Keyguard settings
Select Keyguard code
