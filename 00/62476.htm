Contacts in Live Mail - Microsoft Community

bill rodham say:

Contacts in Live Mail

My contacts have disappeared from windows livemail how can I import them back

¡firedog say:

You probably have at least two different contacts lists in Windows Live Mail: 

  

When you're not signed in with a Microsoft Account, the account button (the right-most button on the Home tab of the ribbon) reads Sign in and the contacts list you see is the default list.

  

  

  

  

When you are signed in with a Microsoft Account, the account button reads something other than Sign in and the contacts list you see is the one that belongs to the account you're signed in with. The list synchronizes (unreliably) with the one at contacts.live.com. Any change you make to this list in Windows Live Mail is supposed to propagate to the online list and vice versa, but don't bank on it working as expected. In particular, categories synchronize wrongly or not at all.     

  

Use the button to change your signed-in state and hence the contacts list you see. If you sign in with a different Microsoft Account, you'll see a different contacts list. There is no connection between the different contacts lists you see in Windows Live Mail.

Because of the unreliability of the synchronization, it may be best simply to abandon the signed-in state. If the contacts you need are in the account list, they can be transferred to the default list so that they're available when you're not signed in.
