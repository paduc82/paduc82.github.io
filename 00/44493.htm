I went through some posts here about the issue but found no helpful answers, most of the time people just don't understand the issue, so i'm going to clarify it as much as possible. Note, windows sonic for headphones exibits the same behaviour, so it's not dolby's fault.

With creators update microsoft gave us the possibility to use surround virtualization, which is nice. Except, most of the time it simply does not work, or to be more precise, there's a glaring design oversight from microsoft.

You see, most of the modern games base their sound output on windows' own speaker setup, with no possibility to select something else. Ticking "enable virtual 7.1 surround" does nothing as the control panel still reports stereo to games and reverts any prior configuration to stereo anyway. So dolby atmos tries to expand stereo, which is useless especially when it's not free...

Before somebody asks me to reinstall stuff or run useless tests (nothing is broken, well badly designed maybe) I will try to explain what we need in simple terms:

Basically dolby atmos for headphones is designed to take in a 5.1/7.1 surround signal, which is generated by games or movies, churn it trhough a magical algorithm and spit out a stereo signal that sounds just like being in the middle of a bunch of speakers.

It works as designed BUT, the vast majority of modern games do not let us select the desired output, like a 7.1 configuration when available. INSTEAD, those games look at the control panel, look at the speaker configuration, see stereo speakers and output plain stereo, and this is BAD. It does work with games that have the possibilty to change the audio output configuration (but those are rare), we do know it is actually working.

What we need for the virtualization tech to work properly is a virtual 7.1 device that would be created when the virtual surround is enabled. And yes it is possible, because razer surround software operates in such manner, it creates a 7.1 sound device which appears in the control panel and is properly seen by games as such.

I hope i've been clear enough and this will get to the proper channels.
