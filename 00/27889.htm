You haven't care to mention any of hte worksheet names so I will assume that they are all on Sheet1.

¹On the A1 cell of Book1 i need two insert a formula which will give the sum of the 6 cells A1:A6 of Book2.


In Book1's Sheet1's A1 use,

=SUM([Book2.xlsx]Sheet1!$A$2:$A$8)

²how to get  the sum of A7:A12 of Book2 inot[sic] the A2 cell of Book1


In Book1's Sheet1's A2 use,

=SUM([Book2.xlsx]Sheet1!$A$7:$A$12)

In Book1's Sheet1's A3 use,

=SUM([Book2.xlsx]Sheet1!$A$13:$A$18)

Alternately, you could use the following in In Book1's Sheet1's A1,

=SUM(OFFSET([Book2.xlsx]Sheet1!$A$1,(ROW(1:1)-1)*6,0,6,1))

... and fill down to A3. Note that OFFSET() is a volatile formula that will recalculate whenever there is a workbook calculation cycle and not just when one of the cells referenced changes. Additionally, both workbooks must be open for OFFSET() to work.
