jan karel pieterse say:

I guess you're looking for the INDIRECT worksheet function. Suppose the sheetname is selected from cell A1:

=INDIRECT("'"&$A$1&"'!"&CELLl("Address",C1))

will give you the content of cell C1 on sheet entered in cell A1.

I could have used this syntax:

=INDIRECT("'"&$A$1&"'!C1")

But that syntax has the drawback that you cannot copy the cell with the formula and have the cell address update accordingly, since the cell address is hard-coded in a string.

So I deliberately use the CELL function here, which you can supply with a cell reference which will update on copying.

One drawback is that inserting or deleting rows/columns in the sheets your pulling data from will NOT affect the formulas on your summary sheet.
