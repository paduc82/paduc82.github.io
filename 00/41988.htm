1. Create a shortcut to iView.mcl, PLUS7.mcl, TEN.mcl, FIXPLAY.mcl and SBS.mcl in:

C:\ProgramData\Microsoft\Windows\Start Menu\Programs\Media Center\Media Center Programs

- Google.mcl
