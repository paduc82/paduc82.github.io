dirk goldgar say: There's nothing online-specific about the ACCDE format.  You can certainly use it locally, offline.  All that the ACCDE format does is disable the design elements of the database and remove all the source code.

dirk goldgar say:
Thank you.  Then in that case, any specific direction I need to give to the end users to strictly follow while copying the CD (containing Front end and Back end files) in their computer to enable the data base function normally as in my computer.


Presumably all these users have Access installed?  Because you're distributing an ACCDE, they must have the same version of Access installed, in the same folders.  Otherwise references will break, and they can't be fixed in an ACCDE.

 

Also, either they must install the back-end in exactly the same folder, with exactly the same path to it, as on your system, or else you need to have a mechanism in the front-end for locating and relinking to the back-end.  Do you have that?

 

Distributing an ACCDE can tricky, if you don't have control over your users' PC setups.

jiaxing bian msft say: Hi Manuel,

Here are my answers to some of your concerns:

Exchange Online Mailbox / Options if a users deletes a message
2nd stage: 30 days from the recoverable items folder, after that the message is gone forever

In fact, the emails will be moved to the purge folder when theyâ€™re disappeared from the recoverable items folder, and remain 14 days there. For this situation, we can use eDiscovery search to restore the emails.

Meanwhile, if litigation hold is enabled, all deleted emails can be recovered.

Onedrive (Personal)
2nd stage: Is there another option for Sharepoint site admins to recover personal Onedrive files after 90 days for the user?

No. By default, each user is the administrator of his own OneDrive for Business site collection. If he deleted a document from the 2nd stage recycle bin, the file is permanently deleted.

Office Groups (Documents deleted within a library, Office groups sharepoint site)

1st stage: ? Same as with personal Onedrive? (90 days?), Self restore by the user?

2nd stage: ?

Office 365 Group teamsite is a customized SharePoint site collection, and it has the same retention time as common site collections and OneDrive for Business.

For your reference: How long are deleted items kept in the Recycle Bin?

Office Groups (whole group deleted by the administrator)

Currently no backup / recovery option in place / right?

When you delete a group, you are permanently removing the group team site, group conversations, email messages, Yammer messages, files, calendar events, and any other related information. There is no way to recover the information once it's been deleted. (Reference: Delete a group)

Sharepoint Sites
2nd stage: ? Another 90 days by site collection admin? Right?

According to this article, the retention time for site collection admin (Site Collection Recycle Bin) is 30 days.

Jiaxing
