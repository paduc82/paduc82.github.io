The error message you're reporting isn't directly related to blank emails.  Are you getting complaints from your email recipients about blank emails in addition to the "temporary problem" error message?

Apparently, Google will start giving you that error message if you regularly send messages to multiple recipients and at least one of them reports you as a spammer.  See https://www.askwoody.com/2017/working-around-gmail-delivery-incomplete-message/

So if blank emails from your email address have been going out to your entire contact list and at least one person complained to Google instead of you, that's probably the reason for the "Delivery incomplete" messages.

Now you have to figure out why you're contacts are getting blank emails from you.  If none of the devices from which you send email have a record of these blank emails, it would seem as if some spammer has been spoofing your gmail address.  If you can get a copy of one of these blank emails with complete header information, you may be able to figure it out.  If you can't figure it out on your own, you'll have to try the Official Gmail Support Forum:  https://productforums.google.com/forum/#!forum/gmail
