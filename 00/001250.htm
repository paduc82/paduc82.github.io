stefan blom say:

If you update Office 2007 with the most recent service pack, you should get the Save As PDF function back.

Alternatively, you can install one of those freeware programs which install themselves as (virtual) printer icons. Examples include CutePDF and PrimoPDF. To convert to PDF you simply choose the Print option in Word and choose an installed PDF printer/converter. 

suzanne s. barnhill say:
As Stefan says, installing SP 3will restore the Save As PDF add-in. But that will not give you the ability to open, read, or edit PDFs in Word; that feature (PDF Reflow) was introduced in Word 2013. To open and read PDFs, you'll need to use Adobe Reader or another PDF reader such as Foxit.
