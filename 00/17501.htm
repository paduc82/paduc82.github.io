dianeoforegon say:
What type of account(s)? (POP, IMAP, Exchange)

If it's POP you need to check your settings for delete from server in Accounts.

If you have IMAP or Exchange, and you move a message from the server folders to "On My Computer" folders it will delete from the server.

Check your rules to see if you are moving messages.

sanfordsimon say: I have an IMAP account and I don't believe that there is a server.   ALL messages that are older than two weeks are disappearing.  However, from searching online, it does not appear that OUTLOOK 2011 on Mac has an auto archive feature.  This has me worried because I am loosing important emails for work.

sanfordsimon say: Strangely, I have two different IMAP accounts:
- one through a university
- one is a GMAIL.

dianeoforegon say: If you are connecting to the account on two devices and one is POP, it might be deleting off the server. In that case even if you have IMAP on the other device, the POP account is deleting the messages from the server.

Even POP accounts are on a server. How long they stay on the server depends on your settings and the size allow by the provider.

Check schedules to see if there is a delete after two weeks set.
