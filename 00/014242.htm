Swiping Issue with Dolphin Browser (Android) - Microsoft Community

azdayton say:

Swiping Issue with Dolphin Browser (Android)

Just wanted to report that on Android, when using the Dolphin browser, Sway has an issue.

This appears to be because Dolphin hides the Tab bar and Address bar.  That makes the "available screen" change, which Sway seems to sense.  When that changes, it appears that Sway re-calculates its screen use - more often than not causing it to display the wrong page, or start over at the beginning, over and over again.

One "fix" for this is that when you're scrolling (left-right) with Dolphin, swipe with a slant, either upward or downward.  This will keep the Tab & Address bars either visible (down) or hidden (up). 

The trick is to remember to do it consistently with regard to the up/down component of the swipes, which is easy when going in one direction.  The "gotcha" is that when one reverses direction, one's natural tendency is to also reverse the up/down component as well, so you have to watch out for that.

Hope this helps someone!
