I'm looking at two recent builds of Word in Office 365, and they behave differently.

In Version 1808 (build 10730.20102), the Change All link in the Editor behaves the same way as the Change All button in the old Spelling dialog of previous versions did: It immediately changes only the currently highlighted instance, and all the other instances change only when the spell check is completed.
