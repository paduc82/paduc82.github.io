cmdrkeene say:

You misunderstand what the rollback period is and how it works.

The "rollback" period begins after you finish upgrading to a new version (you then have 10 days to decide if you want to keep it).

It doesn't matter how long you wait to install something, the "10 day timer" doesn't begin until after you do it.

So as long as you didn't finish installing 1709 more than 10 days ago, you can still roll back.

If it's been more than 10 days, or if someone ran the disk space cleanup tool and removed the old files, then you're stuck:  You'll either have to factory reset, or directly fix the issue by downloading the driver from the computer manufacturer's website.
