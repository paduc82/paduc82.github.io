This is not a particularly explicit problem description. However, I'd hazard a guess that this 'blinking' represents the rapid loading of two different pages until one of them eventually gives up. You may be able to glimpse the page URLs in the address bar while this is happening.

This can occur if two sites are attempting to exchange data but are prevented from doing so. The two sites might be mail.live.com (which is where your email is) and login.live.com (where you have to log in). A common reason is that the sites are in different Internet security zones, which effectively prevents them from exchanging data. So, close all browser windows, then in Control Panel, open Internet Options and its Security tab. Check the entries in Trusted sites. If there are any live.com, hotmail.*, outlook.*, msn.com or similar entries, remove them. Leave any support.microsoft.com entries so that you'll continue to be able to conduct your private conversations with the support agents.

Restart your browser and visit http://mail.live.com. Any joy?
