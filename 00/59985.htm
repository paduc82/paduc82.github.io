 and hit the Enter key

Hi,

 

The issue here can be one of several issues. The following may apply:

 

         The HDMI graphics card may support HDMI Video, but may not support HDMI Audio

         You may need to set the HDMI audio device as the default sound device

         You may need to update your HDMI video card drivers for your version of Windows

         If your HDMI video card does not support audio, you may need to connect additional audio cables between the PC and TV

 

Check out the manufacturers website and download the latest version of audio and video drivers. Also, check in the Device Manager to see if the HDMI Output is set as default device. This should solve the issue.

 

Method 1: Follow these steps to set the device as Default Device

 

a)    Click on Start-> Control Panel-> Click on Hardware and Sound-> Now click on Sound.

b)    Now under the Playback tab-> Right click in the blank space-> Select both "Show Disabled devices" and "Show Disconnected Devices".

c)    Right Click on HDMI Output. Click on Properties-> Under the General tab click on "Device Usage" and select "Use this Device (enable)". Click Apply. Click OK.

d)    Repeat Step 1 and in the step 2 -> Under the Playback tab-> Click on HDMI Output and Set as Default.

e)    Click Apply. Click OK.

 

Method 2: No sound in Windows
