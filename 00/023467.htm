If it is a matter of "Presentation" then i believe you should know that in Excel the logic is a bit opposite from Word. You don't subdivide cells (unless they have previously been merged). That is because Excel has fixed number of Rows and Columns and there cannot be an intermediate row/column.

What you can use is:

Insert Cell (pushing cells in same row/column OR adding new row/column)

Merge Cells for headlines to occupy more cells than data.


Later you can write-click and Format to add borders to your data. Experiment with single cell and selection of many cells to see how it works...


Practice a little and you'll see it is very easy, just different
