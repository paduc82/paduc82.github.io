There is an alternative to using a Tab control. While I think a Tab control is an easier interface, you can try changing the Source Object.

A subform control is a special control that is used to display another form, especially one that can be linked to the main form. Method 2: Possible solution for Error Code 5100:

 

 

a) Clean the timing slit film.

 

 

b) Foreign material or paper debris that obstructs the carriage movement; remove foreign material.

 

 

c) Ink tank conditions;

 

i) Re-seat the ink tanks.

 

ii) Replace timing slit disk film.

 

iii) Replace Carriage unit.

 

iv) Replace Logic board.

 

v) Replace Carriage motor.

a) Turn off printer

c) Keep holding down Power button and let Resume button go.

e) Green lights will flash and then stop blinking.

 

f) When green lights are solid, press the Resume button 4 times.

 

g) Press the Power button and the printer should turn off, if not, press the Power button once more.

 

h) Your printer should respond as normal.
