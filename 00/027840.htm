jay freedman say:

I don't know the reasoning behind this, but the NUMPAGES field doesn't update automatically when it's in the body of a document, only in a header or footer.

To work around this, click in the (now empty) first-page header or footer and insert a text box. Position the text box at the place on the page where you want it. Put the {PAGE} of {NUMPAGES} expression inside the text box. Optionally, set the text box's borders to "No line".

The NUMPAGES field may or may not update immediately when you change the number of pages, but it will update when you display the print preview.

lexus567 say:

OK all - I did it !!!

Answers to my own questions:

After setting up my email account information in my Outlook profile (did mine via the Mail icon in the control panel):

1) OE should be running in order to export messages.  Outlook should not be running.

2) How does OE know where to put the messages?  It ask you after selecting the export messages !!!!  Yes, it asks which profile they should be exported to and then even shows you all your file folders and allows you to select ALL folders or you can check which folders to export. OE then shows a progress bar on how things are moving along.  My system took less than 2 minutes to export over 1,000 emails.

3) Yes the process places these messages into your exising .pst file. It just gets larger in size.

4) Yes all the OE Folders appear in Outlook Mail with the messages in them.

5) Yes the headers, original dates and times are there (though for some reason my times were now 1 hour later than the original - but no big deal there.

6) One caution, in OE you can right click on a name in the header and select "properties" to get people's email address, and you can cut and paste the name into a new email to send.  After the export, the names are still there, but Outlook does not recognize them as anything but plain text now. At least in my Outlook 2003.

 

Regarding my Address Book, I imported that from within Outlook with Outlook running and OE not running.  Again the process asked and all went as expected.

 

Hope this helps others.
