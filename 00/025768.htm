dianeoforegon say: FWIW, it's recommended that you re-index your entire drive after moving to Lion.

HOW TO RE-INDEX FOLDERS OR VOLUMES

From the Apple menu, choose System Preferences.

*  Click Spotlight.
*  Make sure Mail Messages is checked under the Search Results tab.
*  Click the Privacy tab.
*  Drag a folder or even an entire volume (your hard drive) to the list.
*  Remove the item or volume you just added.
*  Spotlight will re-index the contents of the item you initially dragged to the list.

Depending on your Mac and the contents of its hard disk, re-indexing may take several hours to days. Until this process is finished, Spotlight wonâ€™t know the contents of your disks and neither will Outlook. All lists like contacts, events, notes etc. are generated by the Spotlight searches.
