Please be informed, in people.live.com you can only add your photo which will reflect in other person's People address book who have saved you as his/her contact. You cannot add a picture to a contact in your People address book, neither while creating a new contact one, nor while editing it.

The picture that reflects in other person's People address book who have saved you as his/her contact, and that shows on the messages when you send it to your contacts is nothing but your Skype profile picture.

I suggest you to try the steps provided below to add/change your Skype profile picture:

  • Open any email in your inbox or sent folder.

  • Place the mouse cursor on your profile name (In the top left corner of the email)

  • When you place the cursor, you will see a drop down, just click on edit profile details.

  • Under My profiles, click on Skype profile.

  • Click on the Skype icon (Located exactly on top of the page and the first icon/option)

  • On the left panel, click on your profile picture.

  • Click on the Profile picture again to upload a new one.

  • Select a picture and click on Open.
