When you got the Surface 2, did you run Word at least one time during the setup and did you perform all updates?

Most people make a mistake of not preparing their machine for use when they get a computer.  They just jump right into it.  The Office suite on RT tablets; you need to run Word at least one time for the suite to perform a setup.  during this setup, it will assign file associations to the applications (docx for Word, etc.).  Without doing this, the tablet doesn't know what to do with the file.

You also need to make sure that all the updates are installed as well.  You do that, you should have no problem.
