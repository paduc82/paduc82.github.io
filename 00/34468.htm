cmdrkeene say:
In general, you cannot move emails outside of the email program.  Even when possible (with Outlook), it is generally a bad idea because it orphans the email message from the database and you lose the ability to search, sort, or find the email.  A better practice is to create a folder inside your email program.

If you absolutely want to put it in your Documents, you should be able to simply open both your email program and your Documents, then drag-and-drop.  But this may vary depending on the email system you're using.  It will work with Microsoft products like Windows Mail and Outlook.
