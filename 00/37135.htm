Hi,

This seems to be a very frustrating experience. The driver partitions on your PC may be preventing you from reinstalling Windows 10 or from getting out of the automatic repair loop. To troubleshoot this, kindly try these following methods:

Method 1: Removing Hardware

If you have additional hard drives or external USB devices, try removing them. Also, if you are using a desktop, we recommend removing and re-attaching your RAM before rebooting your PC.

Method 2: Disabling the Early Launch Anti-malware Protection

Sometimes, the early launch anti-malware prevents the booting of a PC. You can disable this by following these steps:

  1. Power on your PC and when the Windows Logo appears, force shutdown your PC by hold-pressing the power button.
  2. Do step 1 for 3 times or until the Windows Recovery Environment appears.
  3. In the Windows Recovery Environments, go to Troubleshoot>Advanced Options>Startup settings.
  4. Choose Disable early launch anti-malware protection.
  5. Restart your PC and check if your issue has been resolved.
