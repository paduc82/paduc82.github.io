how do I transfer files and programs from windows 7 to windows10.

lemp say:

There are many ways to transfer "files" -- that is, your personal data such as documents, photos, videos, and music.  The simplest and easiest way is to just copy the files.  You can copy the files directly from one computer to another over your local network or you can save the files on an external hard drive, USB flash drive(s), or optical (CD or DVD) media and then copy the data from whatever media you used onto your new computer.

Transferring "programs" is more difficult.  The traditional and safest method is to install the programs onto your new Windows 10 system using original installation media (DVD or downloaded installer file) and any necessary product key or license information.

When Windows 10 was introduced, Microsoft offered a discount on Laplink's PCMover Express.  This software will move your "files and settings" -- but not programs -- to Windows 10.  See https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/transfer-your-data  The discount no longer seems available. 

Laplink has a "Pro" version of PCMover that is more expensive.  This software claims to be able to move at least some application programs.  There's also a new version called PCMover Home that also claims to be able to move programs. I have no personal experience with either of these programs.
