Is there a recommended workflow to bring art from Illustrator into Visio?  I have also tried saving as EMF, WMF and SVG and none seem to have the expected results.  The EMF fromat seems to have the same quality as the copy/paste method.  SVG didnt really work at all, and WMF looked much worse than EMF.

I put my test files together into a zip archive and uploaded it to Hotfile, if anyone wants to take a look at it.

barb way say: Visio is not really intended for image editing, and does not handle gradients fully, as you have seen.  Your step of copy/paste using Enhanced Metafile option, or saving as EMF, is likely the best you will get.  If you want the media to appear a little smoother in Visio, you can save it natively from Illustrator 2- or 3- times larger than intended size.  Then when you resize it in Visio, the rough bits will be smoothed out.
