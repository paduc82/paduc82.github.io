Recover deleted or missing Outlook folder from Deleted Items folder


1) In Outlook, go to your email folder list, and then click Deleted Items.

To recover a contact, right-click it, and then click Move > Other Folder > Contacts, and then click OK.

2) Make sure Home is selected, and then click Recover Deleted Items From Server.

3) Select the item you want to recover, click Restore Selected Items, and then click OK.

hansv mvp say:

Open the subreport in design view.

Reduce the width of the report.

Activate the Page Setup tab of the ribbon (under Report Design Tools).

Click Page Setup.

Activate the Columns tab of the Page Setup dialog.

Set the number of columns to 4.

Under 'Column Layout', select 'Across, then Down'.

Click OK.
