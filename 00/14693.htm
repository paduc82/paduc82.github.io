I'm assuming here we don't have to check the diagnosis in the lookup table, we simply look for a match for A2:L2 in column 1 of the lookup table

 

=IF(SUMPRODUCT(--(A2:L2=Sheet2!$A$2:$A$100))>0,"Dementia","")

Excel treats number as days by default.  .25 equates to 6:00:00 when formatted  hh:mm:ss.

If you have a sheet with multiple formats you may have to use some formulas to convert

This might be helpful if you need to just do a little converting on an existing sheet without starting from scratch.
