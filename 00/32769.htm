hairyfool say:

You are on the right track with the Code 10 error. The HDMI graphics chip has its own audio "card" separate from the Realtek Audio System. This is used to feed audio to the monitor over a digital connection. Until that is resolved the Sound Control panel applet will not offer the second Digital output as an option.

Have you tried reinstalling the graphics driver from Asus?

Remove all peripherals not needed to eliminate conflicts.

If there is an option in the BIOS make sure Audio over HDMI is enabled, it is not unusual for Windows to detect hardware even if disabled in the BIOS but cannot drive it properly.
