brian tillman [outlook mvp 2007-2019] say:
The Hotmail folders should always be separate from the Gmail folders, provided you are not using POP for both accounts.  Only POP accounts can share the same folder set.  With the usual Exchange ActiveSync for the Hotmail account and IMAP for the Gmail account, unless you have configured Hotmail to pull from Gmail or Gmail to forward to Hotmail, or you have created a rule to move messsages, there's no way Gmail messages could be arriving in the Hotmail folders.
