yassenkantchev say:

Folks, let me describe an issue i experienced with outlook, Microsoft's team inability to resolve it (simply they have no clue what is going on) and the resolution.

The Issue:

On certain phones, after an update to Android 7.0, or maybe even in lower versions, Outlook does not display any messages in any folder windows (Inbox, Sent, Trash, etc.), whether the All messages, focused, particular account (if more than one).

Microsoft will:

Ask you to perform some irrelevant tasks. Like - clear cache, reset account, delete account, BS in general. Something completely irrelevant.

The solution:

Outlook is not "responsive" enough to display content on higher resolutions. Hence, if your phone is configured to display content in >HD (1280x720), it won't work. 1280x720 is the maximum supported resolution. Period. Lower the resolution of your phone to 1280x720 and have a beer. Cheers.
