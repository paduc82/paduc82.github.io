    1. In PPT go to File> Send To> Microsoft Word
    2. In PPT go to File> Save As, open the Format: list, select Outline/Rich Text Format (.rtf)

    In both cases only text content from Title, Content Placeholders will be included. Text in drawn Text Boxes or Shapes will not export, nor will any graphic objects... IOW, just what you'd see in PPT's
    Also, the resulting file will be pretty ugly to look at :-) I'd suggest that in Word you Edit> Paste Special to paste the content back in using either the Unformatted Text or the Styled Text option.

         b) On "Import Data" dialog check the "Table" and "Existing worksheet" options, and select the cell on the grid where you want to place your new table, click OK.
