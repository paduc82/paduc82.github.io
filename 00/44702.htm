      jeeped say:

      You likely have the settings for your background/slideshow at Fill rather than Stretch or Fit. When an image is not at the same aspect ratio (e.g. relationship of width and height) as the monitor screen, you have a couple of choices.

      • Fill - expand the shorter length or width to fill the screen while cropping the excess of the other dimension so as not to distort,e.g. do not distort original image.
      • Stretch - fit the larger dimension to the screen and shrink or stretch the other to fit, image distortion will appear if the original image is not the same aspect ratio as the screen (got a very funny story about this one)
      • Fit - fit the larger dimension to the screen and provide 'letterboxing' in the other dimension so as to have no distortion of original image while providing the full image

      So, it is back to Start button, type change desktop background, enter. Try out some other options within the Picture Position drop down. Save your changes.

      The other option is to maintain a standard length × width aspect ratio by editing and possibly cropping your slide show pictures beforehand.
