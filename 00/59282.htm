Secret Character Battles - Microsoft Community

ruthless tenjin say:

Secret Character Battles

I figured re-porting this might help those in question of how to obtain the achievements for battling the secret characters on the arcade ladder. Best to try these on Beginner difficulty to make things easier.

 

Reptile - You must fight on the 'Pit 2' stage at night. Wait until you see a shadow of a witch or Santa cross the moon in the background and get a double flawless victory, then perform a stage fatality. It's best to reload the ladder and wait until you get the stage as the first or second battle.

 

Noob - On the 'Temple' stage, a priest and a corpse are usually seen at the altar in the background. Instead, to face Noob, you must see Noob standing next to the altar and nobody else. He will be surveying the fight. You must win the match without blocking to engage him.

 

Smoke - Possibly the easiest one. In the 'Living Forest' stage, head to the right to see if Smoke is leaning out from behind a tree. He shouldn't be too hard to miss. If he is, hit D + Back Button/Select (whichever system you're playing on). Your opponent should instantly explode and the fight with Smoke will commence.

 

Jade - Possibly the hardest one to achieve. You must go through the ladder without losing a single match or round while reaching Shang Tsung. From there, you must double flawless Shang and perform a fatality on him. You cannot lose a single round to him, either. You cannot cheat with having it set to one round wins. Two rounds, at least, have to be set! If done correctly, you will see a new loading screen and not the ladder. Jade will be waiting. A trick to getting this; if you get hit, continue fighting and win against him still. At the "FINISH HIM" screen, instead of delivering the final blow, choose player select and choose who you want to try again. Better than running through the ladder over and over.

andre da costa say:

The only way to convert the Windows 7 Home Premium installation is to do an Anytime Upgrade using the Windows 7 Professional product key from Home Premium. The problem is, the Windows 7 Home Premium license needs to be activated to use Anytime Upgrade (if its not you will run into complications and you might have start all over by dong a clean install). You could always risk doing the Anytime Upgrade using the unactivated Windows 7 Home Premium and try reactivating by telephone. If you can't bother with this option, its best you back up and do a custom clean install using the Windows 7 Professional disc.

 

How to do the Anytime Upgrade:

 Click Start, type Anytime Upgrade, click the option to enter a key, enter the Windows 7 Professional key when requested, click Next, wait while the key is verified, accept the license agreement, click upgrade, wait while the software upgrades, (it might take 10 minutes or more depending on if updates are needed), your computer will automatically restart, after restarting, Windows 7 will notifiy you its updating the computer, the computer will restart one more time automatically and the upgrade will be completed, a windows will pop up notifying the upgrade was successful and your computer is ready to use, click close, you should be upgraded to Windows 7 Professional your files, programs and settings preserved.

If Anytime Upgrade fails, Windows Anytime Upgrade Fail:

Download the hotfix containing a tool called CheckSUR, this tool will look into the servicing and package folders and fix any corrupted data the tool is listed under kb947821 it can be found at the following link http://support.microsoft.com/?kbid=947821

If Anytime Upgrade still fails, disable User Account Control:

1. Go to User Accounts in Control Panel

2. Change User Account Control Settings

3. Pull the Slider down to lowest level possible

4. Reboot the PC

5. Go through the "Anytime Upgrade" as normal

When you try to put a Windows Vista-based computer to sleep or into hibernation, you receive an error message that resembles the following:

STOP 0x000000FE(parameter1 , parameter2 , parameter3 , parameter4 )  BUGCODE_USB_DRIVER




Microsoft has come up with a hot fix, for this error message. To help your self go to this page & request for the hotfix:

http://support.microsoft.com/kb/930568



On the other hand update your computer for the Service Pack 2.
