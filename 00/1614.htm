frank fei msft say:

Hi rudyroots,

It's not correct regarding "One drive for business is only for outside syncing and syncing to older existing Sharepoint servers". OneDrive for Business sync client is used to sync your OneDrive for Business library or SharePoint Online libraries.

Library here means a document library in SharePoint Online (OneDrive for Business is also a document library). For OneDrive for Business library URL, you can click OneDrive icon from the app launcher, and then get the URL from the address bar:


To get SharePoint library URL, firstly access the site, click Site contents and then click the document library app you want to sync, in the document library page, copy the URL from the address bar.


Actually, there is a simple way to initiate a sync without typing the URL. When you want to sync a library, you can just go to the document library page and click Sync button there to trigger OneDrive for Business sync client to sync this library.

Besides, I'd like to tell the differences between OneDrive and OneDrive for Business: OneDrive is online personal storage associate with a Microsoft account, e.g. *** Email address is removed for privacy ***.

However, OneDrive for Business is a service included in Office 365 for Business plans, which associate with your Office 365 for Business account, e.g. *** Email address is removed for privacy ***.
