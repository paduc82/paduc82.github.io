Transitions changed the timing of my photos with my music in windows - Microsoft Community

gensylvers say:

Transitions changed the timing of my photos with my music in windows moviemaker

I added effects to some of my pictures; particularly fades and wipes and they made my photos out of sink with my music. How do I solve this problem?

Transitions changed the timing of my photos with my music in windows moviemaker. How do I solve this?

considerate_guy say:

This is the nature of adding a transition.

Let me try to explain. Let's say you have two photos that are each 7 seconds long. Their total duration is 14 seconds.

Now if you put a transition between them (say for 1.25 seconds), that's a period of time where the first picture is transitioning to the second so essentially both photos are running simultaneously; they overlap on the timeline and that shortens the overall duration of those two photos.

Visually:

[ ..... 7 seconds .... ][ ..... 7 seconds ..... ]

<------7.00------><-------7.00------> = 14 seconds

After adding the transition:

[ ...... 7 seconds ... ]

.......................... [ ..... 7 seconds ..... ]

<----5.75---- |1.25| ---5.75 -----> = 12.75 seconds

So what basically happens is you lose the amount of time devoted to the transition from the total duration.

There are a couple ways you can workaround this:

  1. Add the pictures and apply the transitions first, then add music and synchronize.
  2. For any transition you add that shortens the total duration, add the corresponding amount back to the duration of one of the photos.
