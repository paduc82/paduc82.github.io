david a matson say:

Greetings hirababulogantha!  Welcome to the Windows Phone forums!

It's my understanding that Windows Phone itself isn't capable of logging into all of the nearest WiFi hotspots automatically just because all share the same SSID (name).   This usually takes software on the University side to pass your MAC Address around from hotspot to hotspot to let each piece of University equipment know you are ok to log into each with the same credentials.  Otherwise, you will need to log into each of the major hotspots manually.  

Having said that, as you start creating a profile for each hotspot, over time the phone will begin to automatically log into those that you already have a profile for; presuming the MAC Address of the current hotspot is the same as it was previously.  

The downside to this kind of environment (having a Network with the same SSID on all network points) is that it becomes easier for an evil 3rd party to attempt a "Man in the Middle" attack, mimicking the known SSID used by the University.

I would suggest you have a quick chat with your University's IT staff to find out what you should and shouldn't be able to do...
