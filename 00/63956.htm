To find your Access number:

  1. Sign in to your account, then select Skype To Go.
  2. Your Skype To Go Access number is displayed, along with any Skype To Go numbers you've set up. Simply call your Access number from any mobile or landline and follow the voice prompts to dial international numbers at Skype‚Äôs low rates.

  1. Sign in to your account.
  2. In the Manage features section, select Skype To Go.
  3. In the Learn more section, click Access Number and Speed dial.
  4. Call your Access Number. When greeted by the voice menu, select option 2 and enter an international number starting with the country code (for example, +1 for the US or +44 for the UK) followed by the number itself.
