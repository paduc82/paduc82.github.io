When you first go anywhere in a Word document, what you see is not "blank", it's "empty".  If it were "blank" like a sheet of paper you could write in the middle and your writing would stay in the middle, because the paper would keep it there.

The thing that causes text to depart from the top left corner is the Formatting Properties of whatever object you are typing in.

The problem is HP SimplePass, specifically, OPBHOBrokerDsktop.exe and HPSimplepassBHOBroker. Killing both of those processes solved the problem. They are running by default on the new machines.

I uninstalled HP SimplePass and all is good.

A web search of SimplePass Windows 8 brings up hundreds of hits where people are having difficulty with it working on Win8.1.  Fact is, working or not it's killing performance in a big way.
