Thieves: Know Your Shadowmarks - Microsoft Community

xxw0lfxx tfd say:

Thieves: Know Your Shadowmarks

I just thought I'd share this for any fellow thieves out there, whether they may be new to the noble proffesion or seasoned sneaks who have stolen thousands of valuable items. What do I speak of? Why, Shadowmarks of course. These pictorial signs are employed by the thieves guild and can be found throughout the five major Holds: Whiterun, Riften, Windhelm, Markath an Solitude. They are used to provide a variety of information to potential thieves, such as which houses contain the best loot, potential dangers, where fences are located and even where to find escape routes in jail cells.

----------------------------------------

Below I've listed the different marks that can be found, but if you want an in-game reference simply search the Thieves Guild premises for a book entitled Shadowmarks, written by a certain Mr. Delvin Mallory.

------------------------------------------

"THE GUILD"
This sign indicates that there's a member of the Guild nearby for certain, and it also provides a safe place to stop and just chill out. Break open a beer or two, watch some TV and generally try to work the irrational fear of flying lizards out of your system. If you're a member of the Thieves' Guild then you'll have seen this symbol many times when entering and exiting via the graveyard.

--------------------------------

"SAFE"
This symbol indicates a safe way around a dangerous obstacle such as a corridor full of traps. Be sure to head in the direction it's pointing so that you don't get turned into a shish-kebab. If this symbol is on a house it means it has been cleared out, so don't waste your time.

--------------------------------------------------

"DANGER"
The exact opposite of the above, this sign indicates that you're taking your life into your own hands past this point. Consider investing in some shiny new gear and coming back later, preferably with a small army. If you're still determined to go on, at least write out your Will before you do so. I can have your stuff, right?

-----------------------------------------------

 

"ESCAPE ROUTE"
As the name implies, this handy little marking is found inside jail cells. Look nearby for an escape route and then enjoy your new-found freedom. Not to be confused with the above "danger" sign.

------------------------------------------------------

"PROTECTED"
Anyone within this house is under the protection of the Thieves Guild, and therefore should not be robbed or assaulted. I'm unsure if there actually is any penalty for ignoring this symbol and robbing/assaulting the houses occupants. Presumably the penalty would being stripped naked and forced to spend an evening locked inside the Night Mother's coffin.

-----------------------------------------------------

"FENCE"
Every thieves favorite symbol: this little baby means there's someone nearby who'll buy your totally legally acquired goods for a fair price. Or it could mean that there's a fence you're going to have to climb over, so ensure that you're feeling healthy and not wearing anything heavy, after all we don't want to straining yourself, now do we?

---------------------------------------------------------


"THIEVES' CACHE"
You'll find these emblazoned on the top of chests or barrels. Open it up to find a gift of some sort. And for god's sake don't panic: dragons don't fit inside barrels so you're not going to be ambushed. Mind you, Cicero might fit inside a barrel, so maybe you should be careful when opening them.

----------------------------------------------------------


"LOOT"
One of the most beautiful symbols ever created, this pretty little thing indicates that there's something worth stealing here. On another note I did try drawing this symbol on a little box I own to see if some loot would magically appear inside. None did, and I felt sad.

--------------------------------------------------


"EMPTY"
This little sod is the worst of the worst, indicating that there's nothing of note inside. Presumably that's why it's emblazoned on the side of my head.

-----------------------------------

So there we have it: a short guide to the wonderful world of Shadowmarks.. Hopefully this little post will help out some people out there or at least provide a little bit of extra information on the world of Skyrim and one of the oldest proffesions in the worlds.

-

Wolf
