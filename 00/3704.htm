Instead, I wanted it like this:

author: author1, author2, author3
title: publication1

The easiest way to do that is base a report on the PublicationTable and in it include a subreport based on a query which joins AuthorPubTable and AuthorTable, linking the report and subreport on PublicationID exactly as you'd do with a form/subform.  Design the subreport as a multi-column report with across-then-down column layout.  The authors will then be listed horizontally across the page.  You'll find an example as ClubMembership.zip in my public databases folder at:
