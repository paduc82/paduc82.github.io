This is documented here: https://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/ff701703.aspx What is happening is your account is being rate limited. This happens for one of two reasons when using a basic or trial key. The first reason is that your account is making requests at a frequency that will likely exceed the free terms of use. Upgrading to an Enterprise account will significantly reduce the likely hood of rate limiting occurring.
