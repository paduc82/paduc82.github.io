By default, when you base a style on another style, the numbering of the "parent" will be inherited (similar to the way other style properties are inherited).

You can do the following: For each of the styles that are based on Body Text, display the Modify Style dialog box. In the dialog, click Format, and then click Numbering. Click the "None" option, even if it already appears to be selected, and click OK twice.

Also, you are describing a problem with copying numbered styles between documents. This is a known bug with numbered styles in Word.
