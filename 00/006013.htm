bob jones aka: cybertaz say:
You're correct that you cannot access the direct paragraph formatting dialog while in Notebook Layout [NBL] View. However, the Line Spacing is controlled by the Note Level Styles which are based on the Normal paragraph Style. My guess is that you've modified your Normal Style to be double spaced & saved the change in your Normal.dotm template.

In order to correct the issue while in NBL View, use the Styles pane of the Toolbox to restore the setting for your Normal paragraph style. When you modify the style be sure to check the Add to template box... otherwise, the change will affect only the current document. By checking the box it will update the style in your Normal.dotm template so that future documents will be single spaced, but you'll have to make the change individually in any existing document.

FWIW, I personally recommend that the Normal paragraph style not be modified unless you have a thorough understanding of how styles work in Word. Many other styles are based on Normal & altering it can result in unexpected results further down the road -- such as this one :-)
