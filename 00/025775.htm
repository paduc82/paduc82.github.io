The WYSIWYG view with three panes, slide, slide "thumbnails" and optionally Notes text is called Normal View.  

Normally after typing the text for the slide title (the text directly next to the numbered slide icon) in outline view, you'd press ENTER then TAB to switch to typing text into the body text placeholder.

If you have other text placeholders, I don't think you can access them from Outline view.
