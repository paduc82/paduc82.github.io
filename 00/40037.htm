  3. In the left pane of the Registry Editor, navigate to HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion directory.
  4. Click on an empty space in the right pane of the Registry Editor, hoverover New, then click on DWORD (32-bit) value.
  5. Name the file PreferExternalManifest, then press Enter.
  6. Right-click the new DWORD value, click Modify, then type 1 into the Value Data.
  7. Change the value's Base to Decimal, then click OK.
  8. Once done, close the Registry Editor.
