young yang msft support say:

Hi Carlos,

This is an expected behavior. There is no direct way to resolve it.

This mail contact (which corresponds to the on-premise mail enabled PF) is synced to Office 365 via the scripts, but not via Directory Synchronization (DirSync) service. So, this mail contact is not included in DirSync service. And DirSync will not update the member information to Office 365. Therefore, the PF contact is not a member of the online DG.

Due to this fact, when online users send emails to the DG, emails are not delivered to the on-premise PF.

===============

After analysis, one possible workaround is: Create DirSync filtering to filter out the DG, then this DG is deleted in online. After that, manually create DG in Office 365. Then, we can add the PF contact to the DG member.

If this is not feasible in your organization, please ignore the workaround.

Thanks,

Young
