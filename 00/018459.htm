stefan blom say:

The problem is how section break works in Word. The text on a section break displays the properties of the following section (for example, it tells us that the following section is the "Continuous" type of section), but the break itself stores the properties of the preceding section.

The simplest fix is to open the Page Setup dialog box and change the "Section start" option to your preference.

You can open the dialog box by double-clicking anywhere on the vertical ruler or by double-clicking the upper part of the horizontal ruler. Alternatively, click the dialog launcher button on the bottom right of the Page Setup group on the Page Layout tab.
