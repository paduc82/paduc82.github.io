ken sheridan say:
I'd suggest creating a table as follows:


TransactionMonth          TransactionQuarterNumber         TransactionQuarterText              YearAdjust

1                                  2                                              2nd Qtr                                     -1
2                                  2                                              2nd Qtr                                     -1
3                                  2                                              2nd Qtr                                     -1
4                                  3                                              3rd Qtr                                     -1
5                                  3                                              3rd Qtr                                     -1
6                                  3                                              3rd Qtr                                     -1
7                                  4                                              4th Qtr                                     -1
8                                  4                                              4th Qtr                                     -1
9                                  4                                              4th Qtr                                     -1
10                                 1                                              1st Qtr                                      0
11                                 1                                              1st Qtr                                      0
12                                 1                                              1st Qtr                                      0

You can then join this to a table of transactions in a query like so:

SELECT TransactionDate, TransactionAmount,
YEAR(TransactionDate)+YearAdjust & "-" &
FORMAT((YEAR(TransactionDate)+YearAdjust+1) MOD 100,"00") As AccountingYear,
TransactionQuarterText as AccountingQuarter
FROM Transactions INNER JOIN AccountingQuarters
ON MONTH(Transactions.TransactionDate) = AccountingQuarters.TransactionMonth;

The format for the accounting year can be whatever your wish of course; I've simply used the 2010-11 format which is the format we conventionally use for the accounting year in reports and the like.

You can group a query by quarter as follows:

SELECT YEAR(TransactionDate)+YearAdjust & "-" &
FORMAT((YEAR(TransactionDate)+YearAdjust+1) MOD 100,"00") As AccountingYear,
TransactionQuarterText as AccountingQuarter,
SUM(TransactionAmount) As TotalTransactions
FROM Transactions INNER JOIN AccountingQuarters
ON MONTH(Transactions.TransactionDate) = AccountingQuarters.TransactionMonth
GROUP BY YEAR(TransactionDate)+YearAdjust & "-" &
FORMAT((YEAR(TransactionDate)+YearAdjust+1) MOD 100,"00"),
TransactionQuarterText;
