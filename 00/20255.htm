PFC Ski wrote:

Close, but not quite. It is returning a value, but that value is 0.00

I suspect that all of H6:H82 and/or J6:J82 are text, even though they might look like numbers.  You can confirm by entering =ISTEXT(H6) and =ISTEXT(J6) into parallel columns, then the formulas copy down through row 82.

The double negate (--) has the effect of converting numeric text into a number.  Note that -(-H6) = H6.
