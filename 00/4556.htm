john sr wilson say:

Not sure I understand why you think you need several macros. At most you need one for each shape and maybe one to read the tags. You could actually do this with just one macro that checked which shape was clicked but that might be too complex at this stage.

Problem one though is you cannot have a hyperlink on a shape AND a run macro action. It's one or the other so your macro will need to BOTH increment the tag counter AND jump to the correct slide.

Lets look at the macro for Image1 ((assuming it hyperlinks to slide 10)

Sub Image1()
Dim iCount As Integer ' this will be zero at this time
'If the tag exists set iCount to its value otherwise leave iCount=0
If ActivePresentation.Tags("Image1") <> "" Then iCount = Val(ActivePresentation.Tags("Image1"))
'update the tag
ActivePresentation.Tags.Add "Image1", CStr(iCount + 1)
SlideShowWindows(1).View.GotoSlide 10
End Sub

You would need to write a similar macro for Image2 and Image3 just changing the names where they appear and the hyperlink slide.

To read the tag count

Sub recoverData()
MsgBox "Image1 clicks " & vbTab & ActivePresentation.Tags("Image1") & vbCrLf _
& "Image2 clicks " & vbTab & ActivePresentation.Tags("Image2") & vbCrLf _
& "Image3 clicks " & vbTab & ActivePresentation.Tags("Image3")
End Sub

As I told you on the TechNet forum, you have to put in a delay factor of about 1 second and the place to put it is in a AutoNew routine that you place in the ThisDocument class. The following code works...

Option Explicit

Dim weThereYet As Integer, StopCount As Integer
Sub AutoNew()
    HavePatience
End Sub
Sub AutoOpen()
    HavePatience
End Sub
Sub HavePatience()
    If StopCount > 9 Then
        MsgBox "Sorry, your document failed to open."
            StopCount = 0
            End
    End If

    StopCount = StopCount + 1

    weThereYet = Application.Documents.Count

    If weThereYet < 1 Then Application.OnTime _
        Now + TimeValue("00:00:01"), "HavePatience"
    
End Sub

Private Sub Document_New()
    MsgBox "Test"

End Sub

Private Sub Document_Open()

End Sub
