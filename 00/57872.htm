considerate_guy say:

Add the clips you want to edit into Movie Maker.  If you want to trim items from the beginning or end of a clip, use the Trim facility to set the Start / End points of the clip.  If you want to remove something in the middle, use the Split facility.

 

Imagine you have a clip that looks like the following:

GGGGGBBBBGGGGG

 

You like the good parts (G) but not the bad parts (B).  So play the clip until you are just before a bad part.  Now either choose Video Tools | Edit | Split, or just press M on the keyboard.  That will split the clip into two parts.

GGGGG|BBBBGGGGG

 

Now play it until you are at the end of the bad part.  Again, press M on the keyboard to split the video clip again.  The result will be the following 3 clips:

GGGGG|BBBB|GGGGG

 

Right-click on the middle (bad) clip and choose Remove.  The result will be just the good parts of your movie.

GGGGG|GGGGG

 

To now save this as your final movie, choose File (Alt+F) and then Save movie.  Give it a new name and that video file will only have the good parts joined together.
