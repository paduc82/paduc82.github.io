suzanne s. barnhill say:

Word doesn't actually have a way to restart numbering in each "chapter" per se. You can restart numbering in each section, but if a chapter contains more than one section (as a result of section breaks inserted for landscape pages or multicolumn sections), numbering will restart even if you are using endnotes at the end of the section and have suppressed them in some sections so that they appear at the end of the chapter. There is no way around this, unfortunately.

If your chapters and sections have a 1:1 correspondence, however, and you are still experiencing problems, they may well be caused by vestiges of the Nota Bene product in the document.
