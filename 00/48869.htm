Hi Brett. I'm Greg, an installation specialist and 9 year Windows MVP, here to help you.

An Upgrade to Pro is premised on the Home license which is subsumes. The PC will permanently have a Digital License for either Home or Pro.

You might be able to move Home or Pro to new hardware but Home can only be moved without Pro if Pro is full retail, because as stated otherwise an Upgrade subsumes the lower version license into it.

Moving to new hardware has other rules to consider. If this was a WIndows 10 Upgrade from WIndows 7, 8 or 8.1 then those down-level editions must have been retail for factory or builder's OEM to change motherboards. This was confirmed to MVP's by the head of Support recently who said we could disseminate it.
