bernie deitrick, excel mvp 2000-2010 say:

Using a macro is overly complicated for this simple task. Set up your range in X1:X100, and then add a column to your data, with a formula like

=NOT(ISERROR(MATCH(IDNumber,$X$1:$X$100,FALSE)))

where IDNumber is the value or cell within that column in your datatable.  Then use that column of formulas as a page, row, or column field, set to TRUE to display values that match your list.

When you modify the list, refresh the Pivot table, and it will update based on the formula results.
