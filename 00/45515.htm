Win 10 Start Menu--can't move tiles around - Microsoft Community

demask77_mc say:

Win 10 Start Menu--can't move tiles around

I too had this issue and after several days of research I found a fix. 

In the search bar type

Regedit

Run application.

Navigate to the following

HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\Software\Policies\Microsoft\Windows\Explorer

Change the "LockedStartLayout" value from 1 to 0

Right click on the "StartLayoutFile" value and click delete

Click "Yes"

Close Regedit 

Right click on the desktop and click "Refersh"

Restart the computer 

If this fix is available why hasn't' Microsoft provided this information.
