In C2, array-enter (enter using Ctrl-Shift-Enter) the formula

=IF(COUNTIF(Data!$A:$A,$A$2)>=ROW($A1),OFFSET(Data!$A$1,LARGE(IF(Data!$A$1:$A$600=$A$2,ROW($A$1:$A$600)),COUNTIF(Data!$A:$A,$A$2)-ROW($A1)+1)-1,COLUMN(A$1)-1),"")

and copy down and across until it returns blank values.
