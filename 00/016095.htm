SOLVED... Well, I think I found the guilty component and a workaround. It went like this:

1. On top of trying to open Word at Safe Mode, I tried to restart Windows in Safe Mode, and then the Word screentips worked ok.

2. Then I started Windows normally and tried to kill the unknown processes (from Task Manager) one after the other. When killing the "DpAgent.exe" and the "DpAgent.exe *32" processes, Word screentips suddenly started to behave normally.

3. Searched the web... This is "DigitalPersona" software for the fingerprint reader of my Dell Vostro.
