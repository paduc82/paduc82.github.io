vijay a. verma say:
Hi Andreas.Cable,
I am Vijay, an Independent Advisor. I am here to work with you on this problem.

Your research result is correct. (But Volume licensed versions of Office 2019 use Click-to-Run. Hence, if you can arrange Office 2019, Visio 2019 retail will go hand in hand with Office 2019 volume)

Two different installation techniques can not survive starting 2016 versions.

Click to Run - Retail versions purchased from stores / online
Msi - Volume versions usually supplied by organization.

Now what happens that organizations, in general, have volume licensing agreement with Microsoft for Office. Hence, they get Msi version of Office.

Softwares like Project, Visio are used by very less people, hence it doesn't make sense for organizations to get into volume licensing agreement with Microsoft. Hence, they tend to purchase retail version i.e. C2R.

Now, C2R Visio can not be installed with Msi Office and vice versa. Both have to be either C2R or Msi. (Only for 2016 / 2019 )

You will not be able to change the version of Visio from Retail to Msi as Msi is only for volume license holders.

https://technet.microsoft.com/en-us/library/mt7...
