johan van mierlo say:

Facabook app is installed already and as i mentioned before ,there is no gesture allowing me to save the photo.

 

The public Facebook app won't allow you to save a photo to your device. However if your Facebook account is connected with your Windows Phone (add your Facebook account in account settings) you can find the person who posted the picture in your People Hub (Contacts)

When you find that person , you can tap on the name for their Profile and then slide over to "What's new"

Here you can find the image you want. Tap on it and it will open in a larger screen. Now tap on the three dots on the right bottom and select "save to phone" The image will now appear in your saved pictures album in the picture hub.

When you install the New FaceBook Beta app you indeed can do this directly from the app.  You can download this Beta by using this link directly.
