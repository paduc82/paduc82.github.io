taylor oh say:

I disabled my touchpad and I can't turn it back on. Please help. Windows 10. I can't click because I can't use the mouse!

Depending on the "severity" of your disabling it has different solutions. I would recommend trying out IronMan's method first.

If that doesn't work you may have changed some settings in your device manager.

Step 1: Press Windows button and X to open a menu. Click Device Manager.

Step 2: Find "Mice and other pointing devices" and open the drop down menu. You will see your mouse and your touchpad options. 

Step 3: Right click your touchpad and click "enable". There you go! Hopefully it works out.
