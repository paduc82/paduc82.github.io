Select the cells where you want this to apply to and right-click and choose Format Cells from the drop-down. On the Number tab choose Custom and enter this string in the box:

0" AD";0" BC";0;@

This will show positive numbers as 1066 AD and negative numbers (-27) as 27 BC, with year 0 being shown as 0.
