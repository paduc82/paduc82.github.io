Remove all 3rd party AV, Security, Cleanup software.

Remove all USB, SD, Compact Disk cards attached to the computer except mouse and keyboard.

One desktop owner had to remove the card reader as it too was USB.

You should have kept up with the updates as they were issued.

Also check your registry;

Right click Start, click on Run and enter regedit and hit enter.  This takes you to the registry.

Navigate to Computer\HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Microsoft\WindowsSelfHost\Applicability

Under EnablePreviewBuild change the Dword to 1

Also if under Applicability there is RecoveredFrom.  You will find a build number.  Change the Dword to 0

Then get out, reboot, and go to Settings, Updates, Check for updates.
