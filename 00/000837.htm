Set up sound notifications for text messages

By default, your phone displays a message on the screen when a text message arrives. You can also make your phone play a sound when you get a new text message.

To set the sound for a new text or MMS message:

¬∑         Go to the Apps > Settings > Sound screen.

¬∑         Tap Messages.

¬∑         Tap a sound from the list of sounds available to select it. Your phone will now play that sound when you receive a new text message.          

 

Tip:

¬∑         If you want to preview the sound first, tap the arrow on the right side of the screen that corresponds to the sound.

 

Accept or dismiss a message notification

No matter what you‚Äôre doing on your phone, a notification will pop up when you receive a new message.

¬∑         Tap the notification to open the conversation and read the message.

¬∑         Tap the X to dismiss the notification and continue what you‚Äôre doing.

 

Note:

¬∑         If you have configured a PIN number that must be typed before the screen unlocks, new message notifications will pop up when your screen is in PIN lock, showing new message text. If you want to hide the text in new message notifications during screen lock, turn this setting off. Go to Global Settings > Screen Lock > Pin & Security >Message Display to configure this setting.

Configure your phone to download MMS messages automatically

You can choose to have MMS messages download automatically, or only when you open the message.

This setting is on by default (MMS messages will download automatically). To change this setting, go to Apps > Settings > Phone.
