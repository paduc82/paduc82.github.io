rightisray say:

Hi K.,

Were your settings on the software set to "items I choose"?  If it is, that is why it is deleting things off of your device.  With that setting, if your remove something that you have synced with your device from your collection on the PC, it will also remove it from the device.  So if you have this setting on, your new software is thinking that the music has been removed from your collection and therefore is going to remove it from your device as well.  Set it to "manual" and it should not remove anything from your device.  Then, once you get everything you want onto the computer with the new software you can go back and change the setting to items you choose if that is what you want.  Hope this helps you.
