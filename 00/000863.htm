Alright, I have determined what the problem is and fixed it:

The Office telemetry service was going haywire due to my Pi-hole blocking it from dialing home. I changed my settings to connect to my ISP's DNS and suddenly Word started behaving properly. So I have disabled telemetry in group policy and now all is well (I hope).

  4. I then defaulted back to my current PST file, deleted all of the rules and imported the rules using the latest export (the above step)
