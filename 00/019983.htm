Sub Paytotal()
Dim pag As Page

don_sadler say: Just for the sake of closure here is the solution.  First, I dropped the boolean field as it was not need in the final solution.  Here is the  macro that does what I need (to sum the salaries).  Thanks to David J Parker (Microsoft MVP) for helping me out on this.

Sub Paytotal()
Dim pag As Page
Dim shp As Shape
Dim pay As Long
'Set reference to correct page
Set pag = Application.ActivePage
pay = 0
'Loop through all shapes on page
For Each shp In pag.Shapes
    If shp.CellExistsU("Prop.SALARY", False) = True Then
                    pay = pay + shp.CellsU("Prop.SALARY").Result(visNone)
    End If

Next shp

MsgBox "Total salaries are $" & pay
End Sub
