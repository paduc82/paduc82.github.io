Look at the two same-named layouts and check for anything different.  I'm pretty sure any different shapes (and probably the same shapes but in a different order) will trigger this.  Whether the same shapes/same order but different position/formatting/any/other/little/thing will trigger it?  Exercise for the reader. ;-)
