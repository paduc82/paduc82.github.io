just to familiarize yourself with the interface.

Samples')

pane...drill down to the folder your files are saved in.

appear in the top right pane. (You'll notice...there is a
'Name' column and a 'New Name' column...so you
can see the changes that will be applied.

the changes and you cannot rename unless the files
are selected/highlighted)

the drop window and choose 'Fixed'...now type PUB
in the blank field.

the 'Rename' button...then...OK / OK.

  3. Enter this app password where you would enter your normal password.

The way you enter an app password can be slightly different, however, depending on the app or device you're trying to sign in to. Several of the most common ones are listed here. Tap or click the ones that apply to you.
