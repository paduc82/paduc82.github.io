steve rindsberg say:

For hyperlinking purposes, shapes with text have TWO hyperlink "targets" ... the shape and the textframe within the shape. 

If you apply a link to the shape but click directly on text that ISN'T linked, I don't think anything will happen (I don't have 2013 here to test with). 

Either click on the part of the shape that doesn't include text, apply the same link to both text and the shape, or apply the link to another shape atop the shape with text ... this one should be filled with a solid color but set to 99% transparent.
