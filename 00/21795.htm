If the figures and their captions are all "floating" (not in line with text) and all anchored to the same paragraph mark at the top of the page, it can be almost impossible to get them in the right order. The solution is to press Enter once on the page for each picture on that page (easiest to see what you're doing by clicking the Â¶ button on the Home ribbon), and drag the the anchors to the paragraphs in the correct order -- the first picture and its caption anchored to the first paragraph, the second picture and caption to the second paragraph, and so on.
