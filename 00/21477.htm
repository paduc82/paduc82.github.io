    Hello suzanneWhalley,

    Thank you for writing to Microsoft Community Forums.

    To address your concern, kindly help us with the following queries:

    1. Are you facing issues only with the music within the CDs or does the issue occur with the music on your system as well?

    2. Are you receiving any error code or error message when you attempt to play the music?

    3. Is your music CD being detected by the system and are you able to access it through the File Explorer (Windows + E key)?

    In Windows Media Player, you can play audio CDs, data CDs, and data DVDs that contain music or video files (also known as media CDs). If you have a DVD drive and a compatible DVD decoder installed on your computer, you can use Windows Media Player to play a DVD-Video disc. If your Music CD is not being detected by your system, please follow the methods listed below and see if it helps.

    Method 1: Update DVD/CD ROM driver from Device Manager.

    Sometimes, the issue persists if the drivers aren’t updated. So, I would suggest you to update the DVD/Cd driver manually. To do that, you may refer the article on Update drivers in Windows 10.   

    Once after updating the drivers, you may check if you are able to play any songs. If not, you may try out the following method.

    Method 2: Uninstall the driver and Reboot the machine.

    If updating the drivers doesn’t fix the issue, go ahead and uninstall it from Device Manager. To uninstall, you may follow the below mentioned steps:

    • Right click on the Start menu and select Device Manager.

    • Narrow down to DVD/CD ROM driver, right click on it and choose Uninstall driver option.

    • Once the process is complete, you may restart your machine.

    • After that, go back to Device Manager and select Scan for hardware changes from the top listed menu which will automatically scan your PC and install the DVD/CD drivers.

    If that doesn’t help, you may visit your manufacturer’s website to install the compatible DVD/CD driver and see if that helps.

    We await your response.
