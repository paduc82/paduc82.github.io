bitseu say:

It is possible that the subscription already renewed (charge is placed on the same date) before you have disabled the renewal.

For example,

If you have started the trial May 2, 2018, the renewal charge is placed on the same date June 2, 2018 like any other services. If they cannot charge you on that date, the service will still be active and that charge will stay on a pending state. If you have disabled the renewal June 4, 2018 then the service will not renew after June 2, 2019 as technically the service has already finished renewing last June 2, 2018.

You can still follow the steps online to cancel the service. If the charge is still pending, it will be removed after the cancellation.  But if it is already processed and you cancelled the service within 30 days, you will receive a refund.

Sometimes the renewal email is confusing since it will tell you that the Office has renewed but does not clearly state if the charge is already processed or still on a pending state. (Microsoft please work on this!)

Before contacting the support for a refund request, double check your bank statement or PayPal account if a charge was successfully processed as if the service is already cancelled but the charge was not refunded by the system automatically, they will just refund it as well so no need to be angry or what not.

Hope that helps!
