Hi AngadSingh_27,


The latest update for the Lumia 520 is "Lumia Black", and that comes from the mobile operator.  Each operator customizes the OS to hanle their specific network settings and they may preload some apps.  You can't download the update directly from a PC.  It's pushed to your phone from the mobile operator.


What you may want to do is to go somewhere where you can get a fast Wi-Fi connection, like a coffee shop or library.  Another option would be to take the phone to your mobile operator and see if they can update the phone for you.

I want to clarify this.

The fact that mobile operators approve or modify updates has nothing to do with whether or not you can install it via a PC. The same update process was used for Windows Phone 7 and that phone got its OS and firmware updates via the Zune software or the Windows Phone app for Mac.

Windows Phone 8 doesn't use the Zune software and was designed for over-the-air (OTA) updates. The truth is the replacement Windows Phone app for Windows Microsoft has developed a friggin mess and has not replicated all the features Zune had, including installing OS and firmware updates. So you MUST find a WiFi point to install OS updates. I realize that with the popularity of the lower-end Lumia models in places like India, this has become an issue because WiFi isn't as prolific as it is in North America or Europe.

Here's hoping Windows Phone 8.1 gives us more ways to install OS and firmware updates.

To embed a excel sheet into a word document you can try these steps:-

1.     In Excel, click on the chart and do Edit > Copy (or, click on the chart and do ctrl-c). Or, select the cells you want to appear in your Word document and do Edit > Copy or ctrl-c.
