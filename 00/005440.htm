Well, if they really are Headers/Footers [as opposed to content copied & pasted from page to page] you shouldn't have to delete any more than you created... And I doubt you created 553 :-)

Headers & Footers are a Section property, so if you delete the H/F for a Section it will be removed from all pages in the section. Unless you did something special (such as Left/Right) it should only be necessary to delete the Header/Footer in each Section -- i.e., 10 Sections, 10 deletions.
