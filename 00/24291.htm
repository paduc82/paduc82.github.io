Sharing Collapsible Text Outside of Word - Microsoft Community

phildorge say:

Sharing Collapsible Text Outside of Word

I am making a document using multiple layers of collapsible headers to create a step by step guide for certain working procedure. I want to share this document with people who do not have access to word.

Is there a way to share a word documents outside of word and still retain the collapsible function? 

jay freedman say:

Short answer: No.

The collapsing and expanding of headings and their contents is strictly a display function of the Word program in versions 2013 and 2016. There is nothing stored in the document file or exportable outside of Word that manages the collapsing/expanding. In fact, the same document from Word 2013 or 2016 can be opened in Word 2010 or earlier, and the headings there will not be collapsible. Conversely, a non-collapsible document from Word 2010 can be opened in 2013, where the headings can be collapsed.
