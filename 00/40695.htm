Windows Search 4.0 is not always problematic (as you have experienced on your other PC), but it does cause serious problems on enough systems that I advise against its installation for most people.

Regarding optional updates, avoiding them is wise (in my opinion) for the simple fact that trying to install each and every framework, etc. introduces more chances of issues down the road. They are simply not necessary. If someone needs a PARTICULAR optional update, that is different. I just believe that installing them just for the sake of installing them is potentially problematic. There are so many posts made here where people experience these kinds of problems.

Hardware updates are the worst. There have been plenty of posts here where folks wind up unable to boot and get BSODs after installing incorrect drivers.

Drivers should only ever be downloaded from the hardware manufacturers' web sites.
