  • Click the  Contacts  button.

  • Click either the pencil icon next to their name (to change the display name) or click Add number (to add a number to an existing contact).

The new Skype on Desktop, Mac and Linux version 8.13: Edit a contact's name.

  • Click the Contacts  button and locate the name of the contact you wish to edit.
  • Right-click and choose View profile.
  • Click the pencil icon beside the contact's name.

Note: Currently, the option to add a number to an existing contact is not available on the new Skype.
