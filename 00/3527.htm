Forward Outlook.com Email to Another Email Address


To set up Outlook.com to forward incoming messages to a different email address automatically:


1. Click the settings gear in the Outlook.com toolbar.
2. Choose More mail settings from the menu.
3. Follow the Email forwarding link under Managing your account.
4. Make sure Forward your mail to another email account is selected under Email forwarding.
5. Select Don't forward to halt forwarding.

7. If you cannot add another email address:

9. If you want to retain copies of forwarded mail at Outlook.com:

11. With Keep a copy of forwarded messages in your Outlook inbox. not checked, forwarded mail will not be available in Outlook.com at all (not in the Deleted folder either).
12. Click Save.

 

Forward Certain Messages Using a Rule in Outlook.com

To set up a new filter that forwards certain messages to a different email address from Outlook.com automatically:


1. Click the settings gear in the Outlook.com toolbar.
2. Select More mail settings from the menu that appears.
3. Now select Rules for sorting new messages under Customizing Outlook.
4. Click New.
5. Specify the desired criterion for matching emails for forwarding under Step 1: Which messages do you want this rule to apply to?.
6. To forward all messages from "*** Email address is removed for privacy ***", for instance, make sure the criterion reads 7. Sender's address contains "*** Email address is removed for privacy ***".
8. Make sure Forward to is picked under Step 2: What action do you want to apply?.
9. Enter the address to which new messages matching the rule should be sent automatically under Forward to.
10. Click Save.
