Yes, this does seem to be an oversight with Office installations nowadays, it is hard to create or find shortcuts . ..

Here is a workaround . ..

Double click a Word Document so Word opens

Open Task Manager (Ctrl + Shift + Esc)

Find Word on the Processes Tab, right click Word and choose 'Open File Location'

Then close Task Manager

In the resulting File Explorer Window, right click each Office Application executable and either choose 'Pin to Start' or Send To Desktop (create shortcut) depending on where you want your Office shortcuts to be . . .
