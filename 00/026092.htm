To be specific, if you're using Skype for Windows 10 you're signed in when the computer starts even if the app itself isn't running.

Skype for Windows 10 has two parts: the visible app and in a background process called skypehost.exe. If you prefer not to be signed in automatically on Skype for Windows 10, you can sign out from within the app. We won't sign you in automatically after that.
