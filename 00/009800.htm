Something went wrong during updates.

If the update were successful, all your apps would now be 16.8 121129. AutoUpdate will not normally leave you with a mish-mash of incompatible apps.

I think the first troubleshooting step to take is: Ensure your Mac and Office have the latest updates. Here’s how to install updates:

 

Make sure your Mac has a working internet connection, then:

 

  1. Update Mac OS X. Open the App store and click on the Updates button. Install the updates.
  2. After, in any Office application go to the Help menu and choose Check for Updates to launch the AutoUpdate app. Let AutoUpdate install updates and then restart your Mac.

If all your apps don't come up to 16.8 121129, then one of two exists:

1. You have multiple copies of Microsoft Office installed. Delete old versions of the apps and keep the current ones.

2. Somehow, your installation got totally messed up. In that case, do a complete remove and then reinstall.
