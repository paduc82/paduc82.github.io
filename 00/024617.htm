"under 400Mb" suggests a very large Excel file.

Safe mode inhibits the loading of add-ins and of workbooks that are stored in any of three folders Those folders can be located by typing the following lines in the immediate window of the VB editor
?Application.LibraryPath & "\XLSTART"
?Application.StartupPath
?Application.AltStartupPath 

The last may be blank.

Remove any files from those folders, and disable all add-ins of each type.

Then restart Excel normally and re-enable the things you disabled but think you need until you find the culprit.
