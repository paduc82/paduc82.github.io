    Sub LoadAddin()
    'Written by Steve Rindsberg, PowerPoint MVP
    'www.PPTools.com

            On Error Resume Next
     
            Dim oAddin As AddIn

    • Open the document in Word
    • Select the desired text by click-n-drag
    • Right-click and select Copy (or press CTRL+C)
