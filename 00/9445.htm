byhamc say:

As Jean-Yves states below, I believe your table_array is incorrect - you should either specify a range of cells or use a named range where you have put 'Color'. If Color is supposed to be a named range, check it is doing what you expect, and the spelling of it in the Name Manager.

Once you have the formula working correctly and not returning #Name, the answer to your original question is to 'wrap' the formula in a IFERROR function, which would look something like this:

=IFERROR(IF(VLOOKUP(B2,Color,8,FALSE)="Yes","Y",""),"")

If the IF(VLOOKUP....) part of the formula returns an error, excel will return the final "" part of the formula. Don't add it until you've sorted out the #Name issue though, otherwise this will just be masked by the IFERROR statement.
