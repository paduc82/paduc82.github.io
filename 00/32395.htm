a. user say:

Hi Michael,

Thank you for being a part of Windows 10 Insider Preview.

I appreciate you for providing details about the issue and we are glad to assist you.

Please be informed that, you cannot stop the automatic Windows updates. But you have an option to stop the optional updates.

Follow the below steps to stop the optional updates:

  1. Press Windows key + Pause/Break from the Desktop.

  2. Click on the Advanced system settings from the left pane.

  3. Click on Hardware tab and click on Device Installation Settings.

  4. Check mark on the No, Let me choose what to do option.

  5. Check mark on the Never install driver software from Windows update option.

  6. Check on Save Changes.

Kindly let us know if you need any further assistance with Windows. We are glad to assist you.

Thank you.
