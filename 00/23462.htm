I don't believe that there are any available licenses yet for Windows 10 Pro for Workstations Edition. I have not heard of pricing being announced yet by Microsoft.

You probably have a license for Windows 10 Pro or Home, but not "Pro for Workstations" Edition. Once your device is licensed, the license is permanent for that device, but only for the specific Edition of Windows 10 that was Activated.

You can always test Pro for Workstations Edition on a separate partition than your activated install is on, but it will not activate until you can purchase a Pro for Workstations license.
