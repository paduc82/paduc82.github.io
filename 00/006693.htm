dcjoshua say: I solved the issue with my Glance screen, along with two other problems!  I was also having trouble with the screen going black as soon as I started a phone call.  (Workaround for that, by the way, is double click your Power button.)  And notifications from apps or text messages were not waking the screen.

My original tempered glass screen protector only had cutouts for the speaker and for the IR camera (Iris scanner).  I gave it a one star rating on Amazon and returned it.
 
I bought a different one with a cutout along the top right from the speaker all the way to the edge.  So none of the sensors or even the selfie camera are covered by the protector glass.  It also has a cutout for the red light that comes on during Iris scanning. 
 
Once I switched to the new new tempered glass screen protector with the full cutout, all three problems were solved.  Glance screen works, phone doesn't go black during calls (except when I actually hold the phone up to my ear), and notifications can wake the screen.  YAY!

Sub hide_rows()

Dim Lastrow As Long, x As Long

Lastrow = Application.InputBox("Enter last row number", "Row", 23, Type:=2)

I would imagine the issue is caused by how Outlook for Windows and
Entourage EWS for Mac are handling "conversations". For Exchange
environments messages can be threaded by conversation ID, which is a
unique identifier assigned to a group of messages. This purpose of this
identifier is to do what you're wanting, which is to thread messages
from the same conversation together even if the subject changes. I'm not
sure if conversations support was ever included in posts. It should be
there for mail messages.
 
I don't recall Entourage 2004 having support for conversations, though,

just maintenance support. I urge you to consider implementing SharePoint
in your environment. This is Microsoft's suggested path away from public
folders.

then it probably will never work. At minimum be sure you've applied the
latest updates to both versions of Office just in case.
