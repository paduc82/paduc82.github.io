Hi Malte,

In Mac client, the OneDrive for Business client only syncs files in one’s own personal OneDrive for Business library. Files shared with a user won’t be synced because they are still stored in their original location instead of stored a copy at the user’s OneDrive for Business library. Therefore, your colleagues won’t see the shared files in their local sync folders.

As a workaround, we suggest you find the shared files via browser. Those files can be find in Shared with me folder in their OneDrive for Business library.


Regards,
Ryan

timliu_msft1261 say:

Hi Malte,
 
Both the personal OneDrive (consumer OneDrive) and OneDrive for Business will automatically sync files between local synced folder and cloud server.
 
However, the folders or files which other users shared with us won’t be synced. We can see them in another section from cloud. It is just a view for those files. They are not in our own libraries, so these files won’t be synced.
 
In personal OneDrive, there is a feature called Add to my OneDrive. After adding, the folders are in our libraries, they can be synced with other files now. OneDrive for Business doesn’t have this feature. If users want to sync the Shared with me items, they need to access that user’s OneDrive for Business library and then use the legacy OneDrive for Business sync client to sync those items (apply to Windows).
 
For Mac OS, it’s not feasible to sync the Shared with me items. In this case, if you create a folder in your OneDrive for Business library and share it with others, the folder will automatically sync on your Mac. For those Windows users, they can use the legacy sync client (the OneDrive for Business include in Office suite or a stand-alone version) to sync this folder. For those Mac users, they can only work with the cloud version. On the other hand, if you create the folder in your consumer OneDrive, other users can use the Add to my OneDrive feature, and then sync the folder to either Windows or Mac OS devices.
 
We understand it will be convenient if we can directly sync the items which other users shared with us. It is suggested you post your requirement via Uservoice. This is the best way to let related team hear your voice. Many good features are added from customers’ ideas.
 
Thanks for your understanding.
