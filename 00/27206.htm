Hey everyone,

 

If you press and hold the Shift key down on your keyboard, and then click and drag the website's Favicon to the Desktop or folder that you want to create a .URL shortcut instead of a .WEBSITE shortcut.

 

The .WEBSITE shortcut is the shortcut that is created when you click and drag the favicon to the desktop or other folders. The .WEBSITE extension is what provides the pinned site functionality you see when the new window is created.

 

The .URL shortcut will open the URL in normal Internet Explorer when opened from its location and won't cause a new window to be opened when it is created.

 

Hope that helps.
