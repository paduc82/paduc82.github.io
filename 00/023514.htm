jeeped say:

I must admit to a little confusion. AFAIK, if you use the Keep Text Only option, the content will come into the Word document as raw unformatted text and adopt whatever formatting is in the destination. While similar operations may use the Use Destination Formatting label, I believe that Keep Text Only performs the same task.

Addendum: There may be some paragraphing issues with the content coming in with line feeds instead of paragraphs. This is typical of content copied from a web site that uses combinations of <p></p> and/or <br/> HTML tags for their markup. The <p></p> would be the ¶ (picrow or paragraph) and the <br/> would be the ↵ (line feed). Tap Ctrl+Shift+8 to turn on Show Formatting Markup to determine whether this is part of your problem.
