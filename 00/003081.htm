Your problem is definitely not with your client software (this is why downgrading to Classic won't help), it's a clear problem with your contacts' 8.x Skype.

For some reason developers of Skype 8 decided it's a good idea to change the behaviour of Skype status. The new Skype sends "I'm online" (green) status only if the Skype app is open (e. g., is not only running) and if it's the top-level window. To solve the issue you have two options:

2. Ask all your contacts to keep the Skype window open instead of working with other apps.
