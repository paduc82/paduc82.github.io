hairyfool say:

When the 4 colour logo starts to build during initial boot press and hold the power button for about 6 seconds to ensure the computer goes off instead of standby. Unfortunately the timing can be a bit critical, sometimes you have to wait until the animation completes so can be a bit of a pain to get right.
