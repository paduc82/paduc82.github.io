a. user say:

A,
re:  determing retail price

Top left cell is B1 containing...  "W PRICE"
Cell C2 uses this formula...  =INDEX($C$1:$F$30,MATCH(B2,$E$1:$E$30,1),4)
       (note: the dollar signs fix the range size and must be used)
Cell D2 uses this formula...  =C2*B2
'---
The formulas are filled down to the bottom of the range used for this demo ($C$1:$F$30)
Column E must sorted in ascending order.

Yammer posting rules for Company group

Is it possible to restrict writing rights in our Company group  (and other groups) and choose only some members to be allowed to post something?

vincentchoy say:

Yammer best practice is to tell users not to post to All Company Group, rather post in relevant groups so that it reaches the right people and the right people comment.

In the context of a large company, posting it to the All company group will result in the post being buried by a large number of other posts.

You can set a group to private during creation or in the admin part (gear symbol) of the group (you will need to be the Yammer network admin, or the admin of the group to be able to do so).
