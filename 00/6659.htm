how to stop merge field causing document to shift-down - Microsoft Community

araarso say:

how to stop merge field causing document to shift-down

we have merge document with Excel as source.  There is one field that has a notes.  so when we merge this field into the document, it causes to shift down the document.
doug robbins - mvp office apps & services (word) say:

If you put the mergefield into the cell of a borderless table that has fixed dimensions, then that will stop the text below from being moved.  If the cell is not of sufficient size to contain all of the text in that field however, the excess over what can fit within the cell will not be visible. Therefore, if you want all of the text to be visible, the cell will have to be large enough to accommodated it and you will then, in some cases, have a gap between the contents of that field and the following text.

You can't have it both ways.

-- Hope this helps.

a. user say:

Hi,

 

It may be possible that conversation threading has been enabled on your account that's why you see these emails being grouped together into one. To disable this, please follow the steps below:

  1. Open your inbox.
  2. At the upper right part of the page, click on the gear icon/cog wheel then choose More mail settings.
  3. Under Reading email, click on Group by conversation and pre-load messages.
  4. Under Conversation settings, choose the first radio button Show messages individually.
  5. Click Save.

 

After following the steps above, you may then be able to go to the individual emails or respond to the earlier email.
