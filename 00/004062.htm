    You can't copy stuff from microSD because of the DRM rights management, but fortunately Linux OS doesn't care about them.
    So if you know somebody with Linux computer, take the SD adapter from you new microSD card, put the Nokia microSD card in it and plug it in with some SD card USB reader to Linux machine and here it comes, you can copy all the stuff without any problem.
