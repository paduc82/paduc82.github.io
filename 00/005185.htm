Probably a number of things. One thing you did right was to ask here. Unfortunately automatic numbering in Word in complex documents is not all that automatic. Unless done precisely, it will give you what is known by the technical term of "spaghetti numbering."

See How to create numbered headings or outline numbering in Word 2007 and Word 2010
The key is to tie numbering to the heading styles. These styles do not need to be formatted to look like headings. You want to follow all of the steps in this article.

I am far from an expert on this. I'm curious why you would need 15 different numbering formats, or is it that someone gave you something with fifteen formats and you are trying to fix it?

Another, older, harder, method, is to use SEQ fields.
