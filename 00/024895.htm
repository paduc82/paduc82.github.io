guy lima jr. say:

I believe SkyDrive is the best way to sync your notes between multiple devices.

 

If you're using OneNote 2010, go to File > Share and share it to the Web using your Live ID/Microsoft account.

 

Then your notebook will live in the cloud and you can access it from anywhere. You'll also be able to use any of our mobile apps (for Windows Phone, iOS, or Android) so that you can get your notes on your phone.
