trevor rabey say:

You haven't said how you define "Overdue" or how you are determining whether a task is "Overdue" or not.

However, assuming that it is based on a built in field or a custom field, you may have a field which has the value of either "Overdue" or "Not Overdue", in which case you are better off grouping rather than filtering.

If you want a count of the items in a group, use a spare number field, Number1, put in "1" as the formula and then check roll up to the sum for summaries and group headings, and you will get the count when you apply the group.

If you want a count of duration days of overdue tasks, then you could use a custom duration field Duration1 with a formula = Duration and roll up the sum, and you will get the count when you apply the group.

If you want to make a custom report, you will need a custom table, custom filter and custom group to go with it, and some use of custom fields will be necessary as well.
