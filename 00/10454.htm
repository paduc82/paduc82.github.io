gkinkead say: Thanks for your input guys. I have managed to get it sorted with the help of a tech guy from three. I'll run through it for you in case anyone else has the same problems.

Prior to making the post I had been using the icon in Nokia PC Suite to attempt to connect, however I kept getting an "Authentication Failed" Error. I also use an older version of PC Suite as I had a lot of problems with the latest one. The older one works great.

Anyway... to fix my problem:

1. Turned both phone and PC off.
2. Turned phone on.
3. Plugged USB Cable into both phone and laptop.
4. Turned on computer.
5. Opened PC Suite.
6. Selected PC Suite option on the phone where prompted. PC Suite indicated that it was successfully connected to the phone.
7. Opened Control Panel
8. Opened Phone and Modem Options
9. Selected the Modem Tab. A list of modems and the comports they were attached to was displayed. On the list I had "Nokia GSM Phone USB Modem", attached to "Not Present". I removed this item from the list.
10. "Nokia GSM Phone USB Modem #2" remained on the list and was connected to a comport. I clicked OK and exited out of the control panel.
11. I went back to the get connected item in PC Suite and clicked on the "Connect" Button.
Badda Bing Badda Boom, I connect at 460 Kbps. Howzatt Umpire!!

Thanks again for responding guys.
