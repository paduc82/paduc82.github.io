When an option is greyed out on a device, it means that the system does not allow it to be configured according to your own liking. It does not necessarily mean that the selected option is what the system carries out.

There may be a setting that disconnects the network connectivity when the device is in standby, when the screen goes off, or when it enters sleep mode. To check this, refer to these steps:

  3. Expand Administrative Templates > System > Power Management > Sleep Settings.

  6. Exit the Local Group Policy Editor.
