a. user say:

probably better not to tell people to blow in it.  that could introduce moisture that can cause more damage to the circuitry.  however i agree that it could just be overheating from a build up of dust.  cleaning them out can sometimes help.  if you do buy a replacement, here is a picture showing the 3 different power ratings: http://i.imgur.com/jRN4kMR.jpg

if you do get a 203w power supply, it can fit and work with any of the 360 models, because the console will only use the amount of power it needs.  the 175w will not work with a console requiring 203w, because it does not provide enough power, and the 150w will not provide enough power to the 203w or 175w consoles.

the 360S consoles have two different motherboards and two different power supplies, but interestingly you can use either one on either console.  you can also use any of the original 360 power supplies on a slim console if you get an adapter to make it fit the port, because the slims use less power than any of the "phat" consoles.  the 360E has it's own unique power supply that has just one circle on the port, making it the shape half of the slim port.

here is a link to Microsoft's Official page explaining the details and differences: https://support.xbox.com/en-US/xbox-360/console/power-supply
