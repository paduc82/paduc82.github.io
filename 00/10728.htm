jay freedman say:
Raju, any text formatted with a text color of "Automatic" will change from black to white (or vice versa) when the background is changed from light to dark color (or the reverse). It isn't necessary to specify a manual font color change.

Yves, I believe the threshold is based on an HSL value, but the calculation is hidden -- Word's settings are all in the RGB scheme. I experimented a bit with backgrounds of various shades of gray (that is, with R = G = B) and found that the font color switched from black to white as the background went from (61,61,61) to (60,60,60). According to a couple of online converters, both of those backgrounds correspond to an HSL value of (0,0,24).
