Hey guys, I had the same problem with my Lumia 1520 on an insider build after a hard reset where  I couldn't download Arabic text suggestions although the keyboard itself is downloaded and works. I had to take the hard route by reverting to WP8.1 then upgrading gradually to the latest NON-INSIDER build, then after a hard reset I was able to download the problematic keyboard WITH its text suggestions.

This is a very time consuming solution and took me 4 hours to complete, but it fixes the problem. If you really want the missing text suggestion there is no other shorter solution I could find:

* Use Windows Device Recovery Tool to revert your phone back to WP8.1 (Duration: 30 to 45 minutes)

* Then download the Upgrade Advisor app and use it to upgrade to the first version of W10M (10.0.10586.xxx) (Duration: 90 minutes)


* Then use Phone Update (from the phone settings) to upgrade to the Anniversary Update 10.0.14393.1066 (Duration: 45 to 60 minutes)

* Then Phone Update will do another big update to the latest build in that branch (which happens to be 10.0.14393.1715 right now)  (45 to 60 minutes)


* Then once no more updates reported by Phone Update I did a hard reset (Duration:  10-15 minutes)

And that's it! Took me about 4 hours but I am glad I did it and I got my missing text suggestions back.

At this point I could enroll again in the insider program and take my keyboard with me to the next branch (Creators Update) which is not officially supported on the Lumia 1520, but I am thinking to stick with the Anniversary Update for now.

Hope this helps.

In People, click on All v.
