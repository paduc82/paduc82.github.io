If you don't have a record of the 25 digit product key then you may be out of luck. Microsoft doesn't have a record of the number and there are no more keys to be had. astra2025 say:

Hi star_007, 

 

Welcome to Nokia discussions board! 

 

In order to create a new playlist and add songs to it from your memory card, please follow the steps below: 

 

1) Open any song from the gallery

2) Go to Options > Add to playlist > Create playlist

3) Go to the list of songs in your memory card, highlight your preferred song and go to options > add to playlist. Do the same for each song you would like to add. 

4) Access your playlist from the gallery. 

 

Hope this helps, 

Let us know how you get on and feel free to post any other questions you may have. 

Regards, 

astra2025
