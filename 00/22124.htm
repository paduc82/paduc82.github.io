Selection.PrintOut Copies:=1, Collate:=True, IgnorePrintAreas:=False

Selection.PrintOut Copies:=1, Collate:=True

Selection.PrintOut Copies:=1, Collate:=True ', IgnorePrintAreas:=False

If the problem goes away, an add-on is causing it.
