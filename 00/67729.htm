techfreak_ say:

UK telephone providers (and others) nowadays tend to automatically block all anonymous or generic inbound phone calls to combat spam calls for their customers. To get around this imposed block and inform the recipient you are calling, you can verify a mobile number for Caller ID purposes and enable Caller ID in your Skype account. The only time verified Caller ID can create a similar situation is when the verified number or country is blocked for spam or regional restrictions (like inbound calls from India).
