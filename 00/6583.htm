What happens is when you have Office 365 and add a domain to it, O365 automatically searches using that domain name and tries to find any available Yammer network. If it does find any it maps it with O365.

For e.g. Lets say you have an Yammer Enterprise Standalone with domain xyz.com

Now you purchased O365 for other features (Not Yammer) and added xyz.com to O365

O365 will automatically map your Yammer network (cause of the same domain) to O365 and hence the redirection.

The reason could be because Yammer Enterprise Standalone is retiring from January 2017.

Here's an odd workaround you can try (courtesy of Steve Rindsberg):

  1. With your presentation open in PowerPoint, choose View>Outline View. The outline pane opens on the left side of your window.
  2. Right-click in the outline pane and choose Show Text Formatting.
  3. Now click in the Notes pane, select the text and set the font size.
