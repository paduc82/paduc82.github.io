The Outlook.com team is moving users mailboxes from the old Hotmail backend to the new Exchange backend.  Most users mailboxes have been moved already.  After your mailbox is moved you will need to reconnect your accounts so that they show as Microsoft Exchange.  If you click File, Account Settings, Account Settings, look at the Type field.  If it shows ExchangeActiveSync then you will need to reconnect the account to make it connect as Microsoft Exchange.  Before you do that make sure your mailbox has been migrated.
