Defrag your Nseries smartphone and improve sluggish performance - Microsoft Community

wajahat---01 say:

Defrag your Nseries smartphone and improve sluggish performance

A fully loaded memory card with music or videos on your smartphone could make your phone crash and sluggish over a period of deleting and uninstalling applications, music or videos.

Just as you would defragment your hard drive on a computer to improve performance, its also possible to defrag a N-series memory card.

1. Connect the phone via USB in data transfer mode to a PC under windows.

2. On the PC running Windows, under My Computer, you should be able to detect a new drive letter for your phone's memory card.

3. Simply right click the new drive, then select properties -> Tools -> Defragment Now. This should open up the default defragment tool installed on your windows PC and can then defrag your memory card.

I defrag my memory card and it greatly improves the overall performance and stability of my N95. Hope this helps other users as well.
