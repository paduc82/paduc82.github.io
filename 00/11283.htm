Where you have adapted your formula to grab the old sheet's workbook/worksheet name, you need to the INDIRECT() function to take the text result that the CELL() returns and use it as an address.

So

CELL("filename"),LEN(CELL("filename"))-SEARCH("]",CELL("filename")))               '!$A$1

Becomes

INDIRECT( "'" & CELL("filename"),LEN(CELL("filename"))-SEARCH("]",CELL("filename"))) & "'!$A$1")
