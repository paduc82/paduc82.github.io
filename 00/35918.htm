wolverine58 say:

Yes that is correct. When the light is on, this indicates charging. If the light is off it is not charging.

The reasons for not charging can vary but they boil down to two possibilities:

1. There is something wrong with the power supply or ac adapter that is preventing it from providing power.

2. There is something wrong with the Surface that is preventing it from accepting the power.

If there is an obstruction between the surface charging port and the power supply connector this would prevent charging.

If the ac power cord is not pushed in all the way on the charger this would prevent it from charging. This is a very common problem.

If you have access to a different power supply try it.

If you still cannot get the charge light to come on, let the surface sit with no power cord attached, for 2 or 3 hours. Then connect the power.

If you have tried all of the above, the sad news is your surface may have a hardware problem within the unit. These units are not serviceable by most repair shops because of the way the surface is sealed.
