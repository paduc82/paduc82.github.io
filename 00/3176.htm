rangerx say:

I don't believe you can add a To: column to your inbox.

 

I have three alternative suggestions:

 

1)

Click on the search box and then click on "Advanced Search". Enter your email address in the To: box and search. This will show you all emails sent to that address.

 

Doing searches all the time will get tiresome so not a great solution for you...

 

2)

Create rules to move your emails to separate folders based on the To email address.

To do this:

  • Go to your outlook.com inbox.
  • Create three new folders, one for each of your email addresses by clicking on new folder on the left of the screen.
  • Cick on the gear in the upper right corner and then click on More mail settings.
  • Clck on "Rules for sorting new messages".
  • Click on New to create a new rule.
  • For Step 1 your rule should be "To or CC Line" "contains" (your email address)
  • For step 2 select the first option "Move to" and click the down arrow and select the new folder.
  • Click on save.
  • Repeat building the rules for your other two email address.
  • Send yourself some test messages to ensure the rules are working.

 

3)

Just like suggestion 2 above but  instead of creating three new folders you could create three new categories. But this is a bit more complicated than folders because you have a few more options.
