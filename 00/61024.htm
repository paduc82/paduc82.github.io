Not directly with Snipping Tool, but here's an easy way to do it after you've snipped your snip:

  1. Snip your image, then click "Copy" on the Snipping Tool's toolbar
  2. Open Paint and click Paste on the toolbar, then Crop on the toolbar to make sure the whole image is in view with no extra space around the edges
