-  What happens when you plug in the external drive to the computer? Does it install the drivers automatically?

 

The reason why the drive is not shown in the Windows Explorer is because a drive letter may not be assigned to it.  Let's try to assign a drive letter to the external hard drive and check if it being shown.

 

-  Click on Start and type diskmgmt.msc and press enter.

-  If the drive is being detected, right click on the drive and click on Change Drive Letter and Paths.

-  If there isn't a drive letter assigned, click on Add and click the letter that you want to use, and then click OK. 

- Restart the computer and then check if the External hard drive is detected.

 

For more info: http://windows.microsoft.com/en-in/windows7/change-add-or-remove-a-drive-letter

 

If the drive isn't detected, then download and install the drivers from http://www.vantecusa.com/en/product/view_detail/274
