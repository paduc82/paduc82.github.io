Those who already have the 1.0.1963 version of MSE installed and have no other issues should simply wait for the final release of the MSE 2.0 version sometime later this year.  As Steve mentioned above, the 1.0.2498 version is a minor upgrade that adds no real functionality and though it may contain some bug fixes, these are not likely to affect most users.
