My E-mail messages arrive from 701 - Dash WM 6.0 / Wing / Shadow

Because Windows Mobile 6.0 devices do not support My E-mail, all messages from My E-mail accounts arrive on the device as text messages from the sender 701. To stop text messages from 701 and avoid these text message charges, follow these steps:

  1. On a computer, log in to My .
  2. Click the Setup My E-mail link at the bottom of the page.
    • NOTE: If you are using a Wing or Shadow, the Setup My E-mail link does not display. To work around this, click Not your phone? in the upper-right corner of the page and change it to the MDA.
  3. Delete all of the e-mail accounts.
  4. Set up e-mail on your device.
