hansv mvp say:

Let's say you want to color cells in B2:B50 if the value of the corresponding cell in E2:E50 is greater than 10.

Select B2:B50.

On the Home tab of the ribbon, click Conditional Formatting > New Rule...

Select 'Use a formula to determine which cells to format'.

Enter the formula

=$E2>10

(The row number 2 in this formula is the row number of the first cell in the selection)

Click Format...

Activate the Fill tab.

Select a highlight color.

Click OK, then click OK again.
