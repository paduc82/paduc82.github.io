davem121 say:
Hi macarons19

The purpose of a Skype number is only so people with a landline or non-Skype enabled mobile phone can contact you, the Skype number does not affect call pricing . . .

If you call a landline or mobile phone number, even if you have a Skype number, it is you who pays for the call, not the receiver, you must therefore have Skype credit or a subscription to make that call . . .

The person you are calling will not be charged for that call . . .
