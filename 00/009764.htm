arnie est say:

Hello Lars,

There are several reasons why the phone is not being detected by the tool. The lightning with gear icon is visible when the phone is in a flashing state or phone memory is being flashed. This error is usually sorted out by performing a hard reset using the hard key buttons.

To reset your phone, follow the steps below:

  1. Press and hold Volume down and Power button until you feel a vibration.
  2. After the phone vibrates, release the buttons, and then immediately press the Volume down button until you see a large exclamation mark (!).
  3. Once the exclamation mark appears, press the following buttons in this order: Volume up, Volume down, Power, Volume down.

Since this method will remove your photos, contacts, music, videos and other files, you may lose some data on your phone if haven't created a backup.
