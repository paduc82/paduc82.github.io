richie weldon - msft say: Hi yaadbwoy1,

What might be happening here is that you've reached the maximum amount of data that your account can hold.  Whenever you synchronize, even to restore data onto a phone, the My Phone service first attempts to transfer data from the phone to the Web portal.  If you're out of room on the Web portal, it will prevent synchronization.

You might want to check the "Deleted Items" folder and explicitly delete items in there to permanently remove them.  When you delete an item, it goes to this folder and still counts against your total storage usage.  When you permanently remove the item, the space it was taking up should be come free.  If you free up enough space, you should be able to synchronize once again!

Hopefully this helps.  Let me know if it doesn't.

Richie Weldon

erfan nezami say:

Hello Tonda,

Welcome to Microsoft Community and thank you for your feedback!

Currently Word Online does not support rendering of multi-level lists. That is, only the lowest level of each
multi-level list item is displayed in the Online product. This is mainly due to a limitation on how browsers render list items.

Unfortunately, it is not possible to prevent this behavior at the moment.

Having said that, we are currently working on a new design which will fully render multi-level lists in Word Online. This new design is expected to be available to our customers within the next two months.
