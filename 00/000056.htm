bernie deitrick say:

Any time you have a $, that prevents that reference from being updated:

='[Boston.xls]Jan ''16'!$B$6    stays B6 always

='[Boston.xls]Jan ''16'!B$6     stays 6 always, but B changes to C, D, Etc..

='[Boston.xls]Jan ''16'!$B6     Stays B always, but 6 changes to 7, 8, 9,.....

='[Boston.xls]Jan ''16'!B6    Both B and 6 change when copied.

Each has its place - the default within a workbook is B6 - the default for between workbooks is $B$6.  You can cycle through the options by pressing F4 when the cursor is on or near the cell address.  So, when you create the link, type =, navigate to the cell and select it, and immediately press F4 three times before pressing Enter.
