nathan roberts (sunknath) say:

Hey there, AdvaitShiralkar
Welcome to the Microsoft Community forums.

To open the handwriting panel, please follow these instructions.
1. Right click on a blank space on your Taskbar,
2. Select show keyboard button,
3. A Keyboard button will be visible near date and time. Please select it,
4. A keyboard will appear on your screen. Select the settings icon at the top left hand side
5. Select handwriting panel which is a blank keyboard with pen on the top.

Once you have done, you will have a handwriting panel
