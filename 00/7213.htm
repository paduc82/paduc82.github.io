tj1776 say: ARus,

I had the exact same problem: my POP3 email account on Outlook 2007, deleted the emails from the server when I downloaded them to my Outlook 2007 client.  When I upgrated to Outlook 2010, suddenly, my emails were no longer deleted from the server when I downloaded them to the Outlook client.

To fix In Outlook 2010:
Open Outlook.
Click on the File tab.
From the Account Settings choice list, choose Account Settings.
On the Email tab, highlight your email account name.
Click the Change button (a new window opens).
Click the More Settings button.
On the Advanced tab, uncheck the "Leave a copy of messages on the server" checkbox.
Click OK.
Click Next.
Click Close.
Click Finish.
Click Close.

Voila...
