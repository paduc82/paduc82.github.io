greginmich say:

I’m actually glad to hear that your family is powering down their PCs when they’re not in use, prmykdee, because I’m a diehard environmentalist. MSE’s developers were aware of the problem of missed scans, and they built in a “catch-up scan” which automatically scans the computer after a certain number of scheduled scans are missed (or a certain amount of time has elapsed). The catch-up scan was later disabled in the registry because a startup scan can be very disruptive, but it was replaced by a prompt to scan the PC by clicking on the “Scan now” button. It’s the same idea as your second suggestion, but unfortunately the MSE prompt doesn’t appear for 2 or 3 weeks (?) after the first scan is missed. Many people feel that this results in too many missed scans if you’re on a daily schedule, and quite a few people actually expect the prompt to appear at the next startup. That expectation derives either from the fact that Task Scheduler itself has the option to run the task at the next startup if it’s missed, or from the fact that the official documentation erroneously claims that MSE does just that:

http://answers.microsoft.com/en-us/protect/forum/mse-protect_scanning/not-automatic-scan/6a33dea1-be48-4ac3-889d-431d358fe170

 

So if you want a timely catch-up scan the only alternative is to schedule your own task:

http://answers.microsoft.com/en-us/protect/forum/mse-protect_start/scheduled-scan-wont-start-after-missing-scheduled/059a0c60-6f78-4523-a7a1-dc8ead43aa67

 

But the better solution is also less “complicated” – because you can normally just avoid missing scans in the first place by rescheduling for a time when the PC will be in use. Then there’s no need for a catch-up scan. That answer isn’t obvious because people take it for granted that MSE’s default settings are the “suggested” settings; but in the case of the scheduled scan time settings, that’s emphatically not the case:

http://answers.microsoft.com/en-us/protect/forum/mse-protect_start/mse-scan-schedule-off-by-days/f0e68e51-02d6-4fce-bf0b-999b1f0268c8
