I am expecting (hoping) to get a message that the "Record is in Use" or something of that nature, when I have the Form open on one PC and try to open that same form on another.

What I am getting is a "Conflicted Copy".

My only objective is to keep someone in Orlando and I from stepping on each others updates.

gina whipp say:

Well, it's not that you are doing anything wrong, it's that you can't share and Access database like that because of exactly what is happening AND that just not the way *sharing* works.  It's Client\Server but DropBox (nor OneDrive) are Servers.  When using one of those two a local copy gets *downloaded* and then when you are done attempts to copy it back.

I would suggest finding a different way.

ken sheridan say: You might want to consider using an SQL Server back end hosted in Azure.  See the following link for an overview:
