justine pel say:

Hi Gary,

Most browsers come with a default search engine. A search engine automatically launches a query through the browser's built-in search box. Please follow the steps below to change your default search engine:

  1. Launch your browser.
  2. On the upper-right hand corner of your screen, click on Settings.
  3. Open the drop-down menu under the Search heading, which is labeled Bing if it is your current search engine and select your desired search engine.
  4. Click Manage Search Engines for more search engine options. Select Make Default option to change your default search engine or select the cross symbol to remove one.
