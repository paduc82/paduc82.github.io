williambramhill say:

Good evening, 

This drove me mad and most posts on Microsoft forums were as useful as a chocolate teapot.

To save you scalding your lap:

The cause:

:: You have two-stage verification turned on in your iCloud account and your phone won't work with this.

The solution:

:: Go to https://appleid.apple.com and log in as usual. Under Security, click bottom-right on APP SPECIFIC PASSWORDS > GENERATE PASSWORD. Note down the password, which can only be used once.

Go back to your phone. Go to SETTINGS > EMAIL+ ACCOUNTS. Delete the iCloud account. Perform a soft restart (hold volume and power button down together for about 15 seconds, until phone buzzes). Go back to SETTINGS > EMAIL+ ACCOUNTS. Click add an account. Click iCloud. Add your Apple ID (usually your email address) and the one-time password. Click the tickbox to view the password to make sure you have entered it correctly. Your iCloud account should now sync, which may take several minutes.
