ap cyberzone say:

DATEDIF Function Error In Excel

Please help or suggest me . . . .

amit tandon say:

DATEDIF formula works correctly in the initial release of XL2007 and its SP1, but does not work correctly in SP2. Being an undocumented function it cannot be counted on to work correctly in SP2 onward in XL2007 & as HansV has pointed you may use this function in the later versions of Excel.

You may use this formula as an alternative which should give correct results in most cases, except negative ones:

=MOD(DAY(C10)-DAY(B10),IF(DAY(C10)< DAY(B10),DAY(C10-DAY(C10)),31))
