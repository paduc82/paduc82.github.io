Hi Dharmesh,

 

It could depend on your locale settings.

 

In Europe, we use ; as argument separator, whille you may need a ,. Try

=TIMEVALUE(SUBSTITUTE(B2,".",":"))-TIMEVALUE(SUBSTITUTE(A2,".",":"))+(TIMEVALUE(SUBSTITUTE(B2,".",":"))<TIMEVALUE(SUBSTITUTE(A2,".",":"))).

Sorry, I had to mention it. Good luck.

Macro solution.

Sub PrintDoubleSided()
Dim TotalPages As Long
Dim pg As Long
Dim oddoreven As Integer
    On Error GoTo enditt
    TotalPages = ExecuteExcel4Macro("Get.Document(50)")
    oddoreven = InputBox("Enter 1 for Odd, 2 for Even")
    For pg = oddoreven To TotalPages Step 2
        ActiveWindow.SelectedSheets.PrintOut from:=pg, To:=pg
    Next pg
enditt:
End Sub
