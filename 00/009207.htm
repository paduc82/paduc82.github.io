    I would write that as the following which should be much more easier to understand.

    =SWITCH(Month([Date_Of_Admission])=1 And Year(Now())<>Year([Date_Of_Admission]),"Annual"
    ,Month([Date_Of_Admission])=10 And Year([Date_Of_Admission])=Year(Now())-1,"90DEval"
