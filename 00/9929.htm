I am guessing that the content that is being cropped is outside the printable area of your default printer, which is what 'guides' the pdf tool in Publisher. For example if you default printer is a B&W printer then your pdf will be B&W.

 

You might try setting up your default printer to 'fit print to page' or 'scale to fit' in the print setup, or maybe as a default setting for the printer via the control panel.

 

You might try a different pdf tool such as primopdf and see if it will automatically scale the page to fit.

 

And of course you could reduce the content on your page to print within the printable area of the page by reducing margins, font size etc.

Dim i As Long

Dim rng As Range

With ActiveDocument
