Hi Grace,

 

It took me awhile to figure out, but I did. When you disconnect your USB cable before it has had a chance to finish downloading files it will erase any songs that you had in your music player, so make sure you STOP any transfers BEFORE you unplug your USB cable.

 

To add songs into your music player using mass storage see below: 

 

  • Link your phone and your computer using the USB cable 
  • On your phone select mass storage
  • On your computer go to explorer and select the F:// drive (that's your phone)
  • Open the folder 'sounds'
  • Open the folder 'simple' -- this is the folder you drag and drop your songs into

 

FYI the 'digital' folder under 'sounds' is for ringtones

 

Now this is an important step, while your phone and computer are still connected go into your music player a message will appear 'USB connection in progress' depending on the amount of songs you have just downloaded it shouldn't take very long for the music to be transferred to your phone.  It won't automatically stop so when you think enough time has passed STOP the transfer by exiting out of music player and then unplug your USB cable from your phone. A message will appear saying 'data may be lossed..." just press okay.  Since you stopped the transfer before unplugging you cable it should be fine. Then go back in to music player and a message will appear asking to refresh select yes and your done.

 

This should solve your problem and if it does I would appreciate a kudos.
