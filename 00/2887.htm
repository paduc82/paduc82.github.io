john - project say:
steel poney,
The main purpose of resource leveling is to alleviate overallocation, that is, shift tasks and resources such that all or selected resources are not assigned to work more hours than are contained in a normal workday. For example, let's say you assigned resource A to work rooms 1 & 2 on Monday. That would mean resource A is overallocated by 8 hours. Depending on the priority assigned (task level), resource leveling will shift the lower priority task to remove the overallocation.

There is no automated way to set up the schedule you want. It's pretty straightforward to do but you'll have to do it manually. Set up your rooms as tasks and assign all four resources to each room. Then go to the Resource Usage view and set each resource's hours to zero for those days and rooms they will not be working. A bit tedious but I can't come up with a quicker way to do it.
