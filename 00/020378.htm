Solution!!!!!! Thank you to everyone who tried to help, however none of the suggestions worked.
 
But I have found a way to fix the problem. This sounds crazy I know, and I have no clue about why this has worked, but this is what I did that fixed the problem.
 
The browser I have been using is Internet Explorer 8.
I had my Hotmail/Outlook emails open in Internet Explorer, nothing was working to move my existing folders from their location at the bottom of the list below the default folders of Junk, Send, Deleted etc., to the main folder list in alphabetical order which is located above the default folders.
