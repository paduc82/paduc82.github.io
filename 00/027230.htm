vincentchoy say:

Likely your SMTP server is not included in the SPF settings.

When Office365 is first setup, you are required to setup your SPF settings which basically states that your emails will be coming from Microsoft's servers. This is to prevent spoofing of your email domain.

Once this setting is set, Anti-Spam engines will check if the mails from your domain is sent via Microsoft servers. If they don't then in all likelihood the mail has been spoofed - and thus blocked.

If that is the case, you would need to add your SMTP server and any other servers into the SPF settings. Will leave that with MS-Support to help you on that.

https://technet.microsoft.com/en-us/library/dn789058(v=exchg.150).aspx
