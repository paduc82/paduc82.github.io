Chart left and right axis scale - Microsoft Community

mgmk say:

Chart left and right axis scale

Hello, 

I have a chart with range of left axis starting from 95% until 105%.

Right axis starts from - 7% until 1% 

How to scale those two sides so that right side fits with left side. 

Maybe I should also say left side should begin with 100% 

And right with 0%.

fin_modler say:

The maths in your case is that you have on the left:

40 0.25% units

20 0.5% units

10 1% units

5 2% units

and so on

So, you need to make the same count of units on the right.

-7 to 1% on the right has

16 0.5% units

8 1% units

etc

You can always make these even number counts work, the question is the steps may seem a bit strange. In this case, you can make 10 steps on the right by having 0.8% steps, going from -7%, -6.2%, -5.4%, -4.6% ... and so on, works but not user-friendly.

So, this is why I suggested leaving your left alone and extending your right to 10 units from -8% to 2%

You can then set 2% major steps for both sides, and 1% minor steps. Grid lines are always controlled by the left or what Excel calls Primary y-axis.

Hi,

try this code...

 

open a new workbook,

export data on sheet1

 

 

Sub FromCSVToXLS()
Dim myWB As Workbook, WB As Workbook
Dim L As Long, x As Long, i As Long

Dim v As Variant

Dim myPath

Dim myFile
Set myWB = ThisWorkbook
Application.ScreenUpdating = False

Sheets(1).Cells.ClearContents
myPath = "C:\Folder1\Folder2\"   '<<< change path
myFile = "myFile.csv"   '<<< change file name
Set WB = Workbooks.Open(myPath & myFile)
ActiveSheet.UsedRange.Copy myWB.Sheets(1).Range("A1")
ActiveWorkbook.Close False
L = myWB.Sheets(1).UsedRange.Rows.Count
For i = 1 To L
v = Split(Cells(i, 1), ",")
For x = 0 To UBound(v)
Cells(i, x + 1) = v(x)
Next x
Next i
Application.DisplayAlerts = False
ActiveWorkbook.SaveAs Filename:= "D:\data folder\1.xls"

Application.DisplayAlerts = True
myWB.Save
Application.ScreenUpdating = True
End Sub
