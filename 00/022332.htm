Not I know what "just making an OS in PowerPoint" means but ...

To change the background you need to do two things:
  1. Tell it NOT to follow the master
  2. Tell it what it should do instead
Sub bckG()
With ActivePresentation.Slides(1)
.FollowMasterBackground = False
.Background.Fill.ForeColor.RGB = vbRed
End With
End Sub
