hansv mvp say:

By using Nz in the line Me.DateCheckedOut = Nz(DMax("DateCheckedOut", "tblTerritoryCheckout", "TerritoryID=" & Me.TerritoryID)), Me.DateCheckedOut will never be Null, so IsNull(Me.DateCheckedOut) will never be True.

You must enclose date values in # characters. Try this

Me.DateCheckedOut = DMax("DateCheckedOut", "tblTerritoryCheckout", "TerritoryID=" & Me.TerritoryID)
If IsNull(Me.DateCheckedOut) = False Then
    Me.DateCheckedIn = DLookup("DateCheckedIn", "tblTerritoryCheckout", "DateCheckedOut=#" & Me.DateCheckedOut & "# AND TerritoryID=" & Me.TerritoryID)
    strCheckedOutPublisherID = Nz(DLookup("PublisherID", "tblTerritoryCheckout", "DateCheckedOut=#" & Me.DateCheckedOut & "# AND TerritoryID=" & Me.TerritoryID), 0)
    Me.PublisherName = DLookup("PublisherName", "tblPublisher", "PublisherID=" & strCheckedOutPublisherID)
End If
