Do you mean External sites v's Internal sites? These terms are commonly referred to as

the Internet (external sites on the internet that everyone can access)

and

the Intranet (internal sites that only ppl in your company can access)

IE treats these two types of web sites differently (see Internet Options>Security tab)

Intranet websites do not apply ActiveX Filtering or Tracking Protection to you company web sites.

In IE Internet websites do apply ActiveX Filtering and Tracking Protection and popup blocking. if they are set in Internet Settings.

To adjust your ActiveX filtering settings for Internet sites please see

IE Blog - ActiveX Filtering for consumers

However..... in November and December of last year MS issued critical security updates for IE and Adobe Flash (Flash is an ActiveX control in MSIE browsers)... that fixed issues with internet websites like MSN and google that use flash, but would not load sometimes in IE...
