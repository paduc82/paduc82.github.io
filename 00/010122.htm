how to configure bluetooth for different voice and music devices - Microsoft Community

acaldera say:

how to configure bluetooth for different voice and music devices

I'd like to configure my WP8 to connect to different devices in my car: voice and music - how can I do this?
sven johannsen say:

I don't think you can configure this. Not to say it can't happen, but you don't have much control. Voice (phone connections) work over the Headset/Handsfree Protocol, and music goes over the A2DP (Advanced Audio Distribution Protocol). These are independent. If you have a device that supports both, and connect it with your phone, it will connect both protocols, and you can't change that. If you then connect another device with both protocols, it will disconnect both on the first device.

 

If you connect a second device with only one of those protocol options, it will (should) only disconnect that one.

 

If you have a car system that supports both music and phone, connect it. Then go into BT and select connect on a BT headset device, that does not support music (A2DP) and it should leave the music connected to the car system and connect the voice to the headset/handsfree device. If both devices support both protocols, and there is no way to shut one off on the device, you can't split them from the phone.

To help anyone else that is having this issue, the solution that worked for me was to do the following.

Get-MsolUser -UserPrincipalName UserName | Remove-MsolUser

Get-MsolUser -UserPrincipalName UserName -ReturnDeletedUsers | Remove-MsolUser -RemoveFromRecycleBin

After verifing that the account was removed, I forced a sync of the user accounts again. Once the user account reappeared, I reassigned them a license.
