suzanne s. barnhill say:
Even when you set header and footer margins to 0, you likely won't be able to print to the edge. Most printers have an irreducible unprintable area, required for paper handling and usually greater on the trailing edge (bottom of Portrait pages, one side of Landscape). Your printer may have a "borderless" setting for photographs, but this may apply only to sizes smaller than Letter or require a special carrier for the paper.
