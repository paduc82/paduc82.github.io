Assuming your employee data is in columns A to D with the header row on row 1, and that your criteria table is in columns E, F, and G on rows 1 to 11 where the first row is a the header row.

Try the following formula in cell D2:

=INDEX(G$1:G$11,SUMPRODUCT(IF(LEFT(F$2:F$11,2)="<=",--(B2<=VALUE(MID(F$2:F$11,3,2))),--(B2>=VALUE(MID(F$2:F$11,3,2)))),--(A2=E$2:E$11),ROW($2:$11)))

Note: This is an array formula that has to be confirmed with CTRL+SHIFT+ENTER rather than just ENTER.

Copy the formula down column D as far as you have employee data in columns A and B.

For the sample data you have posted the values in the D column will be 67000, 63000, 63000, and 65000.

Note: The service column has to have data in all cells from F2 to F11 and the data has to be on the form "<=n" or ">=m" where n and are numbers with at most two digits.
