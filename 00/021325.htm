I gave up trying to find the columns of interest in your post so instead here's a general formula for counting unique value bases upon a criteria.

=COUNT(1/FREQUENCY(IF($A$1:$A$100=A1,IF($B$1:$B$100<>"",MATCH($B$1:$B$100,$B$1:$B$100,0))),ROW($B$1:$B$100)-ROW($B$1)+1))

The formula has line wrapped so ensure it goes into the formula bar correctly. The ARRAY formula (see below for how to enter it) counts unique entries in column B for a given value in A1.
