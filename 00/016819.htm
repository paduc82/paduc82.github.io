I understand that your emails and messages are taking up too much space on your Windows Phone.

To prevent that, you change your email download settings so your phone only downloads recent emails.
You can do that by going to settings, then email+accounts. By ticking on the account you want to manage, you access a menu from which you can choose to 'Download email from' the amount of time you wish.

  3. You'll receive two warnings. If you're absolutely sure you want to restore your phone to its factory settings, tap Yes, then tap Yes again.

language accessory packs are available only for the languages listed here. from this list, we can see the language pack is available for dutch (netherlands) but not for dutch (belgium).


you can install the dutch (netherlands) language accessory pack and then choose dutch (belgium) as editing language but not for display and help language.


i tried to add the dutch (belgium) language pack, as shown in the screenshot below. in the editing language field, i get the option to add dutch (belgium). but for display and help, this option is not available.


to do this, please follow the steps below:
1.    open powerpoint-->file-->option-->language
2.    in the ‘add additional editing language’ drop-down box, select dutch (belgium).
3.    under ‘choose editing languages’, enable ‘keyboard layout’ by clicking on ‘not enabled’.
4.    now to install proofing, click on ‘not installed’ which will direct you to another page. on this page, download the language accessory pack for dutch (netherlands).
5.    click on run after saving the pack.
6.    it will take a few mins to set up office.


note: if a language accessory pack is described as partial localization, some parts of office may still display in the language of your copy of microsoft office.
