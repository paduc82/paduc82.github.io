I have found a workaround.

You need to take ownership and rename two files located under System32.

C:\Windows\System32\MsSpellCheckingFacility.dll

C:\Windows\System32\MsSpellCheckingHost.exe

1. Before you rename, create a Windows restore point for a safe roll back:

https://support.microsoft.com/en-gb/help/17127/windows-back-up-restore

2. Now, to take ownership, follow this guide:

https://helpdeskgeek.com/windows-7/windows-7-how-to-delete-files-protected-by-trustedinstaller/

3. Then rename the files - I have just renamed them into:

C:\Windows\System32\MsSpellCheckingFacility.dllOLD

C:\Windows\System32\MsSpellCheckingHost.exeOLD

4. Reboot, open Skype, type anything. No more spellcheck or autocorrect kicking in!!
