The extra spacing is probably built into the style that you are using. To fix it go to the Home tab on the ribbon.

  1. In the styles group Control + Click on the style name that you are using.
  2. Choose Modify
  3. From the Modify Style dialog choose Format > Paragraph
  4. Adjust the line spacing and/or points before and after setting.
  5. Click Ok on the Paragraph dialog.
  6. Mark the checkbox to "Add to template" on the Modify Style dialog
  7. Click Ok on the Modify Style dialog
  8. Close and quit Word. If you are prompted to save the Normal template ... do so.
  9. When you restart Word your new settings for that style will take effect.
