Since you don't have physical printer that can handle the paper size you want, try selecting a PDF generator printer driver. They don't have the same physical limitation as your physical printer.

I think the root cause of your problem is that Word is still very dependent on the printer associated with the document. When Word was first created in the 1980's ALL documents were printed. Word was an electronic 1:1 replacement for the typewriter. There was no Internet, there were no corporate networks allowing document sharing. Save it to a 360 KB, or 720KB or 1.44MB "floppy disk" and use your sneakers (running shoes) to walk the disk and file over to another computer ... Back then the printers even came with built in sets of fonts, which Word could use to speed up printing and improve print quality.
