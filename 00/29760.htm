rick rogers say: Hi,

If you are losing time when powered off, then you need to replace the cmos battery on the motherboard. This is a relatively easy task, and the batteries can be found in most any store that sells a variety of batteries for cameras, remotes, etc.

1.    If you are in the storyboard view, clickView, and then click Timeline.

3.    Use the playback controls under the preview monitor to find the point where you want to trim the clip.

4.    Do the following:

¬∑        When the playback indicator is at the point where you want the selected video or audio clip to start playing back, clickClip, and then click TrimBeginning.

¬∑        When the playback indicator is at the point where you want the selected video or audio clip to stop playing back, clickClip, and then click TrimEnd.
