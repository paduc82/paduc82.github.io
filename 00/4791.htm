suzanne s. barnhill say:
  1. If you create the rectangle (Insert | Illustrations | Shapes) when the insertion point is in the footer, it will be anchored to the footer.
  2. The shape will be selected, and the contextual Drawing Tools | Format tab will be displayed.
  3. In the Arrange group, click Position and choose More Layout Options...
