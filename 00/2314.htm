I'd like the pic to be the same size as a standard 8 1/2 x 11 piece of paper.

Much appreciated.

Rachael

suzanne s. barnhill say:

There are two issues here.

  1. You won't be able to drag the picture to cover an 8.5" x 11" page without distortion unless the aspect ratio of the picture is the same as that of the page (which is unlikely). And of course if the picture is very small, the resolution may not be high enough to look good at a larger size.
  2. Even if you drag a picture to fill the whole page, you very likely won't be able to print all the way to the edge because most printers require an irreducible unprintable area (usually larger on the trailing edge) for paper handling. Even if your printer has a "borderless" setting, it may be only for smaller sizes (say up to 8" x 10"), for photo printing.
