joeu2004 say:

If dependent formulas in other cells are not updated when you change the "precendent" cells, check the calculation mode.  Click Formulas > Calculation Options, and be sure that Automatic is checkmarked.

If calculations seem incorrect (1*43 = 32.5), temporarily format the cells to display more decimal places, and verify that things are not what they seem due to cell formatting.  For example, "1" might really be 0.7558, and 32.5 might really be 32.4994.  And "43" might also be something different.

PS....  You might also select the cell and try to use Formulas > Evaluate Formula to see what is happening.  However, EF can be misleading.  If it reveals something useful, great.  If not, ignore EF, and use the suggestions above, as well as suggestions that might follow.
