jeeped say:

With your numbers starting in A2 (going down to A64) with the total in A65, select A65 and create a conditional formatting rule based upon the following formula,

=SUMPRODUCT(--(SUBTOTAL(9, OFFSET($A$2, ROW($1:$57)-1,0,7,1))>50))

This will count every time the sum of every possible 7 row range from A2:A8 to A58:A64 exceeds 50. FALSE equates to a zero and TRUE is defined as anything that is not FALSE, so anytime at least one 7 row range of cells totals more than 50, the formula will be regarded as TRUE.
