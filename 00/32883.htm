I ran into this same problem just now. Here are the steps I did to disconnect my Microsoft Account and re-use my Local Account instead.

1) Go to Settings -> Accounts -> Your Account (should be already selected) -> Manage Accounts. This will take you to your Microsoft Account's online profile.

2) Click "See All" and find the "Remove" option for the device you wish to deactivate the Microsoft Account on then follow its steps. Microsoft will say there is a "Disconnect Microsoft Account" option, but as ChrisHughes765 pointed out it's not present. 

3) Create a "Local Account" by going back to Settings -> Accounts -> Your Account (should be already selected), and clicking it's option. Note: If you want to use the same name that's on your Microsoft Account then you first have to go back to the Microsoft Account Profile, click on "Edit name", and change it to something else. After creation you will be logged out, and will have to sign back in. 

4) Go back to Settings -> Accounts -> Your Account (should be already selected) for the last time scroll down, and remove the Microsoft Account from the PC and you should be good to go. 


Let me know if you have any problems.
