theoldwiseman say:

The posted answer from Sandeep is absolutely INCORRECT. You do have to download the app for each account but you do NOT need to pay twice for the same app.

 

You purchase the app in your own account on the PC/Surface. That application can be installed on a further 4 machines. You can also install that app for any other users on those 5 machines. The pain is that you need to install the app for each user that you want to use that app. You do not need to re-purchase the app. Here is how to do it.

 

Once you have bought the app in the parents account, log onto your childs account (mine are all setup with their own MS Live accounts) go into the store on that account and using the settings charm select to login to the store as another user. Choose to login as the parents account that you bought the app with and in the store select My Apps. You will see all the apps you have purchased on your parents account and you can install into the childs account. After you have finished you can revert the store back to the childs account. Repeat this for each account on that PC that you want to install the app for.

 

I hope that all makes sense as I'm not in front of my Win8 PC at the moment.
