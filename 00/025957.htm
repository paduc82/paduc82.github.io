dhookom say: IMO, parameter prompts in queries are never a good idea. I always recommend using controls on forms to filter records.

That said, you can strip the time portion off your field with the DateValue() function. 

WHERE DateValue([DateTimeOfCall]) Between Forms!frmDates!txtStartDate AND Forms!frmDates!txtEndDate


jack stockton say:

You don't need to strip the time off to filter by a date, just add 1 to the end date so that the time of the end date is included.

 

Between [Enter a Start Date] And DateAdd('d',1,[Enter an End Date])
