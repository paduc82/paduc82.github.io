hansv mvp say:

The following version doesn't italicize the quotes:

 

Sub ReplaceQuotesWithItalic_Word_MVP()
    ' from Microsoft Word MVP FAQ Site
    Dim SmartQtSetting As Boolean

    'Make sure smartquotes are turned on
    SmartQtSetting = Options.AutoFormatAsYouTypeReplaceQuotes
    Options.AutoFormatAsYouTypeReplaceQuotes = True

    With Selection.Find
        'Set parameters
        .ClearFormatting
        .Replacement.ClearFormatting
        .Forward = True
        .Wrap = wdFindContinue
        .MatchCase = False
        .MatchWholeWord = False
        .MatchAllWordForms = False
        .MatchSoundsLike = False

        'First do a replace to make sure quotes are all smartquotes
        .Format = False
        .MatchWildcards = False
        .Text = """"
        .Replacement.Text = """"
        .Execute Replace:=wdReplaceAll

        'Then do the wildcard replace
        .Wrap = wdFindStop
        .MatchWildcards = True
        'ChrW(8220) is the open quote character and ChrW(8221) is the close quote
        .Text = ChrW(8220) & "*" & ChrW(8221)
        .Replacement.Text = "^&"
        Do While .Execute
            Selection.MoveStart
            Selection.MoveEnd Count:=-1
            Selection.Font.Italic = True
            Selection.Collapse Direction:=wdCollapseEnd
        Loop

        'Clear dialog of all non-default settings
        .Text = ""
        .Execute
    End With
    'Reset options to the way they were
    Options.AutoFormatAsYouTypeReplaceQuotes = SmartQtSetting
End Sub

How to use the Timeline to view messages, calls, contacts and photos - Microsoft Community

el rey g say:

How to use the Timeline to view messages, calls, contacts and photos from specific times in your life

How to use the Timeline to view messages, calls, contacts and photos from specific times in your life
el rey g say:

You can see what you were doing in a certain month, week, or day using the Timeline on the Studio home page. The Timeline allows you to select a period of time within your phone’s lifetime to quickly see the given messages and photos from that time period. As the contents of Studio grow over time, you will find that the Timeline can show you a more complete picture of their activities than what is provided by a social network. You can look at your history of messages and photos to see what you were doing during a given day, week, or month.

 

Select a new time range (e.g., January instead of February) to view the photos you took on your phone that day. You can select a specific day or a time scale such as a week or a month.

 

Notes:

·         When you sign in, the Default view is this month.

·         If you change the timeline view, the change gets carried forward in other views.

Tip:

If you think you aren’t seeing much content, try expanding the time frame that you are looking at by selecting a month view.

 

 

Get an aggregated view of all the activity on your phone in a certain day or week

1.      Open the Studio home page.

2.      Click a day or time frame on the Timeline.

a.      Click M to view your activity during a specific month. Then select the month you want to view.

b.      Click W to view your activity during a specific week. Then select the week you want to view.

c.       Click D to view your activity on a specific day. Then select the day you want to view.

3.      Allow some time to auto-refresh.

  See only the photos that you took on a certain day or in a certain time frame

1.      Open the Studio home page.

2.      Click Photos & Videos.

3.      Click a day or specific time frame such as a week or month on the Timeline.

a.      Click M to view photos or videos you captured during a specific month. Then select the month you want to view.

b.      Click W to view photos or videos you captured during a specific week. Then select the week you want to view.

c.       Click D to view photos or videos you captured on a specific day. Then select the day you want to view.

4.      Allow some time to auto-refresh.

 

See only the messages that you received on a certain date or in a certain time frame

1.      Open the Studio home page.

2.      Click Messages > Text & MMS.

3.      Click a day or specific time frame such as a week or month on the Timeline.

·         Click M to view messages you received during a specific month. Then select the month you want to view.

·         Click W to view messages you received during a specific week. Then select the week you want to view.

·         Click D to view messages that you received on a specific day. Then select the day you want to view.

4.      Allow some time to auto-refresh.

Note: You will only see the latest message from that person in the time frame

  See only the phone calls that you made or received on a certain day or in a certain time frame

1.      Open the Studio home page.

2.      Click Messages > Call Log.

3.      Click a day or specific time frame such as a week or month on the Timeline.

·         Click M to view phone calls you made or received during a specific month. Then select the month you want to view.

·         Click W to view phone calls you made or received during a specific week. Then select the week you want to view.

·         Click D to view phone calls you made or received on a specific day. Then select the day you want to view.

4.      Allow some time to auto-refresh.

Note: Note: You will only see the latest call from each person in the time frame.
