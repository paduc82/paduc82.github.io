jim.low say:

The amount of 7MB varies from drive to drive (~1MB to 9MB is what I've seen).

 

However, it is endemic to all new drives, and even those that came installed with the system.

 

Reason, this is a system reserved area of the drive. It allows the drive to manage areas that may fail over time, provides a work area for diagnostic routines provided by the drive manufacturer, and further allows proper mapping of the drive by the firmware installed with the drive.

 

The amount of space (indicated 7MB) is a small area for a total of 1TB. (0.7% if my calculations are correct).

 

I hope this helps...
