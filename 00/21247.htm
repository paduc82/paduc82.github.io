    Word assigns alignment at the paragraph level.

    Word keeps wrapping text to a new line inside the margins until the end of the paragraph is marked by the <enter> key.

    A paragraph is shown as the non printing character Â¶ (Pilcrow)

    Every time you want to change alignment left or right you need a new paragraph.

    If you want to center text you have 2 options. One is to define a center tab in the paragraph. The other is to use centered justification to make all of the lines in the paragraph centered.
