jannine_p say:

Hi gionniak

 

Welcome to Skype Community forum!

 

I understand the importance to synchronize your chat history across all your devices.

Please be informed that chat history are being stored on your hard for a period of time, which you can specify in your history settings. And it would not be possible to synchronize the chat history that is more than 30 days on all platforms. Only your chat history for the last 30 days, which is saved on the cloud, can be viewed on other devices once you logged in.

If in case you wanted to transfer your chat history, please be advised that is it possible for desktops and laptops only. For ex. Windows PC to another Windows PC or Windows machine to Mac. You may refer to the steps below on how to transfer your conversation history.

To save and transfer your conversation history:

  1. Press and hold the Windows key 
  2. Type %appdata%\Skype into the Run window and press OK.
  3. Navigate to the folder named after your Skype Name.
  4. Right click on the folder itself and select Copy. This will copy the entire folder including all its contents.
  5. Save the folder to an external media device.
  6. On your new computer, install and sign in to Skype so that the necessary folder is created, then sign out of Skype again.
  7. Still on your new computer, go to the Run window again, type %appdata%\Skype and press OK.
  8. Navigate to the folder named after your Skype Name.
  9. Replace the entire folder (named after your Skype Name) with the folder that you saved in step 4 and 5.
  10. Sign in to Skype. Your chat history will be there when you browse through your conversations.

Please let us know if you have any questions or other concerns.

Thank you!
