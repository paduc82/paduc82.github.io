It is easily fixed by trusting the site the seach accellerator points to; do this by adding it to the Trusted Sites zone in internet options.

You can find out what site you need to trust by hovering over the accelerator (i.e. "Search with Google" with some text selected, and looking at the status bar in the lower left corner.

For me it was http://www.google.nl, to use the Dutch Google search accelerator.

You may need to disable the https requirment TEMPORARILY when adding the url to the Trusted Sites zone.

Alternatively you can edit the (registry setting for) the accelerator to use https.

Best regards,
