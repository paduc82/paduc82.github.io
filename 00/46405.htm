  1. Right click on Start button and choose Device Manager. 
  2. Find your webcam driver under Imaging devices (or Sound, video and game controllers).
  3. Right click on your webcam driver and click Properties.

      - Roll back the driver to its previous version.
      - Look for older drivers in Toshiba site which is for your exact machine and compatible with Windows 10.

How to Roll Back the Driver:

  1. In Device Manager, press and hold (or right-click) your webcam, and then select Properties. 
  2. Select the Driver tab, select Roll Back Driver, and then choose Yes. (Note that some drivers dont provide a rollback option.) 
  3. After the rollback is complete, restart your PC, and then try opening the Camera app again.
  4. If rolling back didnt work or isnt available, uninstall your webcam driver and scan for hardware changes.

Hi MarcGendron,

Dell photo 924 printer is compatible with Windows 8. Dell website does not have drivers for Windows 8.
http://www.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/compatibility/win8/CompatCenter/ProductViewerWithDefaultFilters?TempOsid=win8&Locale=en-US&TextSearch=Dell%2Bphoto%2B924%2Bprinter%2B&Type=Both&CurrentPage=0&TotalPages=1&ShowCriteria=0&SortCriteria=Relevance&Compatibility=Unknown&LastRequested=14

Method 1
I would suggest you to run Hardware and devices troubleshooter.

a. Press Windows Key +R, type Control Panel.
b. Type troubleshooting in the search bar, click on troubleshooting.
c. Click on Hardware and Sound, click on Hardware and Devices.
d. Follow the steps from the wizard to run the troubleshooter.

Method 2
I would suggest you to download and install the drivers for the dell printer in compatibility mode for Windows Vista.
a) Download the Printer drivers from the manufacturers website.
http://www.dell.com/support/drivers/us/en/04/DriverDetails?driverId=R148157
b) Right-click on the driver and click on properties.

c) Click on the compatibility tab and check the box Run this program in compatibility mode for and select Windows 7 operating system from the drop down.

d) Click on Apply and click OK and run the file to install it.
