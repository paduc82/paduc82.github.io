You need to check to see if there is a restore point going back that far.

Go to Start / All Programs / Accessories / System Tools and click System Restore.

Click the Next button. Click the Finish Button to start the restore process.

a. user say:

Hi,


Here's how to use headphone and speaker at the same time in Windows 10:

1. Connect your headphone to your PC and make sure that the speakers are also connected.

2. Right-click on the volume icon in the taskbar and then click Sounds option to open Sounds dialog. And if the Volume icon is not appearing in the system tray of taskbar, please refer to our fix to missing Volume icon in Windows 10 guide.

3. Under the Playback tab, right-click on your speaker and then click Set as Default Device option to make it default. If the Default Device option is missing or greyed out, then it‚Äôs likely because the speaker is already the default audio device on your PC and you can skip to the next step.

Also, you will see Default Device entry just below the device as shown in the picture above.

4. Switch to the Recording tab by clicking on the same.

5. Right-click on your microphone or Stereo Mix and click Properties.

6. Click on the Listen tab. Here, check the box labelled Listen to this device. Select your headphone from Playback through this device drop-down dialog.


7. Click Apply button, the sound should now be coming from both your headphone as well as speakers.

If both headphone and speaker are not playing the sound at the same time, please check if your have actually set Speaker as the default device (and not default communication device).
