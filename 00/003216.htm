And you can tell the status by the icon next to the file name: What do the OneDrive icons mean?

About the OneDrive folder on your assistant's machine, I guess you did not choose the folder when you set up OneDrive. If that is the case, OneDrive "thinks" you will not need all the files in the folder on this machine and there is no placeholder. You can set it up just like you did on your machine. For More:  Sync files with the OneDrive sync client in Windows

Except for that, I want to recommend an approach for you to use OneDrive as document storage and access by two or more users:  share the file with your assistant and let the person sync with his/her account: Share OneDrive files and folders. The benefits of this approach is that you and your assistant can use your own credentials to access files and folders. If you use your own credentials in two computers, your assistant might have issues when he/she open the file with Office client applications. OneDrive will check the account logged in of Office client when opening the file. If your assistant logged in with a different credentials from yours, there might be a conflict. 

I know is complicated, if I am not clear, welcome to share any concerns with me.
