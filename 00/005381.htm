petrib say: WEP = Wireless Equivalent Privacy

It is used to encrypt/decrypt wireless (WLAN/Wi-Fi) data communications.

How to set or change it, check your Wi-Fi router's user guide (manual).

Note, more secure protection than WEP is provided by WPA/WPA2 mechanisms. If your router supports that, use WPA rather than WEP.
