Configure the following settings in Internet Explorer 8 (IE8) to make sure that user id and passwords are saved.

Open IE 8.

1.    Go to Tools > Internet Options.

2.    Navigate to Content Tab.

3.    Click on settings button under AutoComplete.

4.    Put a checkmark on User names and passwords on forms.

5.    Also place a checkmark on Prompt me to save passwords.

6.    Click OK.

7.    On the General Tab make sure that the Delete browsing history on exit is unchecked.

8.    Click OK.

9.    Go to Privacy tab and drag the slider to the lowest so that it accepts all cookies from any website.

This should do the trick.
