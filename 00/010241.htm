toddalbertson say:

The first thing you'll want to do is make a copy so that any further fiddling doesn't get your text further disguised.

In the copy, I would try to clear all formatting from the text.  On the Home ribbon, open the Styles sidebar (by clicking the small arrow in the lower-right corner of Home / Styles).  In your text, click Ctrl-A as you did, above.  Then, in the Styles sidebar, select Clear All.

If you're operating in Print Layout view, you can try Draft view, as many of the things that will shift text out of view don't do so in Draft.  If this works, you can copy the text and place it into the new document, ensuring you paste using the destination formatting or Text Only.

Once, I accidentally made all my text disappear because I had colored it white.  You may want to try Ctrl-A and then select black for your text color.

Also once, I set my paragraph spacing to something silly like Multiple / 0.05 lines.  This will also make text disappear.  Try Ctrl-A, open Paragraph, and select Single for line spacing.

Good luck!
