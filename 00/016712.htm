adrianne buc say:

Hi,

You can easily compare data from separate worksheets in Excel 2010 by splitting. To do so, follow the steps provided below:

  1. Click the split bar located above the scroll arrow at the top of the vertical scroll bar and hold down the mouse button.
  2. Drag downward to your preferred row at which you want your worksheet window divided.
  3. Release the mouse button.

You can also split your worksheet vertically by dragging the split bar located at the right edge of the horizontal scroll bar.
