OK, I spoke with MS Tech Support today and the answer to my problem is..........You can't have search folders on secondary accounts.  This is the way Exchange/Outlook is designed.  It was designed this way for security and privacy reasons.  Because the search folders search "above" the folder permissions, the search functionality is just removed from this part of Outlook so one cannot search for private things. 

I feel this is a very unelegant way to protect private information so I logged my opinion with development.

I have revisited the delegate's PC and removed the secondary account and added another Exchange account to the profile.  Permissions have been given the to the delegate at the server, so I don't have to assign priviledges to 50+ folders one-at-a-time.  Now I have Search Folders for the delegate to use.
