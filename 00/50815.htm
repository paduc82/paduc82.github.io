The updates are not 'forced.' That's exactly how Windows 10 was designed to work.

Windows 10 consists of regular updates that will continue indefinitely. That's how it was designed from the very beginning. If you don't want an operating system with continuous updates, Windows 10 is the wrong operating system for you.
