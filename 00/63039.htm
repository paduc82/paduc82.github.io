Sure, you'll be able to do it.

1. From an elevated command prompt, type slmgr /dlv and hit Enter. You'll get a script window with your activation ID, make note of that.

2. Now, type slmgr /upk your product ID here and hit Enter. The product key is now uninstalled from the system.

3. After you upgrade your hardware or if you have a new PC you want to transfer the license to, open an elevated command prompt, type slmgr /ipk your product key here and hit Enter.
