deepa raj say:

Hi Chris,

Thank you for posting your question in Microsoft Community. As per the description you are facing issues in accessing the Office program. Let me assist you with the issue.

 

To assist you with the issue need some details:

What happens when you try to access the Office program, do you get any error message?

Have you tried to access the Office application from the program files location?

 

Try the steps below and check if it helps:

Step 1:

Try to open Word or Excel in safe mode and check if it opens fine.

·         Hold Windows key + R.

·         Copy and paste, or type the following command in the Open box, and then press Enter:

WinWord /safe

Note: There is space between WinWord and /

For Outlook: Outlook /safe

Excel /safe

 

If the applications open fine in safe mode, try to disable the add-ins associated with Word and other applications and then check the result:

·         Click on File menu, Options and then Add-ins.

·         Click on Go button besides Manage: Com-in Add.

·         Check if there are add-ins listed, clear the checkbox to disable them.

·         Click OK.

·         Open Word to check if it opens

 

If you are able to open the applications in safe and failed to open when clicked on the shortcut icons, then try accessing the applications from the programs files location and check the result:

http://office.microsoft.com/en-us/excel-help/find-and-start-office-applications-in-windows-8-or-windows-rt-HA103581103.aspx

 

If the application open fine from the programs files location, then you may delete the Office application shortcuts which you have on the desktop and then re-create the shortcut or pin it to desktop.

 

If the above step fails, repair the Office suite and check if it helps fix the issue:

http://office.microsoft.com/en-us/project-help/repair-or-remove-office-2010-HA010357402.aspx

 

I hope the above steps helps. If you need further assistance reply and we will be glad to assist you.

Thank you.

Hi and Welcome to the Microsoft Answers Forums!

The @onmicrosoft.com is associated with Microsoft's new Office 365 subscription that business and home users can purchase. Typically an email address will come across as *** Email address is removed for privacy ***.

If you do not know the sender of the email, I advise that you delete the email and/or block the domain. Never open or respond to an email that you are not expecting or do not know the person.
