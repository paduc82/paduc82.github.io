I am scheduling appointments, but do not want to over-book days/time slots ... Would like to insert formula in B2 on down -- that will count the number of times I have an appointment for that specific date & time from Sheet 1 ...?

If your dates are real Excel numerical dates (e.g. integers) and your times are real Excel numerical times (e.g. decimals), then they can be added together for comparison.

                

The formula in Sheet2!A2 (time of day) is,
=TIME(ROWS($1:8),0,0)
... with a number format of h AM/PM_) and filled down till the end of the business day. B1 uses =TODAY() and the formula in B2 is,
=COUNTIFS(Sheet1!B:B,">="&B$1+$A2,Sheet1!B:B,"<"&B$1+$A3)
... then filled down to one row short of the hours in column A.
