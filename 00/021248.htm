Your inquiry is a little short on specifics but perhaps you can transcribe this array formula for your own purposes.

=IF($A$2:$A$99="ABC Inc",SUMPRODUCT(($B$2:$H$99<>"")/COUNTIF($B$2:$H$99,$B$2:$H$99&"")))
