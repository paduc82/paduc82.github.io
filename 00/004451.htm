The red-and-underlined text means you've turned on Track Changes, probably by pressing the shortcut Ctrl+Shift+E by mistake. The disappearing text could be because you pressed Ctrl+A (which selects everything) and then pressed the spacebar, which replaces the selected text with a space. You can display the distance from the top of the page in the status bar:

- Right-click anywhere in the status bar.

- Click on 'Vertical page position' to place a check mark next to it.

If you also click on Column, you'll see the character position of the insertion point (e.g. 37 means that there are 37 character on the same line before the insertion point).
