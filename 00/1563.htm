Windows 10 upgrade (should /will ) keep all you data including documents, pictures, favorites etc.

Windows 10 upgrade (should /will) keep all apps installed or bought including trials from windows app store.

Windows 10 upgrade (will try to) keep most programs you had installed before the upgrade.

Programs it cannot carry over will not be converted to your new program files folder to use in windows 10.

(in either an  Xml or html document - which you can read with internet explorer)

A copy of your old programs are kept in c:\windows.old , in Program Files or Program Files (x86) folders.

You will need to reinstall those missing programs via the original install CD/DVD or setup files.

Sometimes you may need to go to right-click start, control panel, programs and features

and click on the  program and REPAIR your old program.

why is keypad not coming up when on a call? - Microsoft Community

michaelhocke say:

why is keypad not coming up when on a call?

When I take the phone away from my ear to hangup the screen appears to be on the fritz - it flashes and won't allow me to press anything.

a. user say:

Hi MichaelHocke,

 

There may be some dust or fingerprints on the proximity sensor. This can sometimes cause the phone to think it's still up against your ear so the screen turns off to prevent any accidental button presses.

 

If you clean the sensor does your phone respond normally again? If it doesn't, a soft reset of the phone may be able to help clear any potential software related issues up. The steps for performing a soft reset can vary by phone make and model.

  • If your phone has a removable battery, you may take it out for a minute or two to reset the phone.
  • If your phone is unable to remove the battery you may need to look in the phone's manual, or online, for how to soft reset the phone.

As a final option if either of the previous suggestions don't work, i would suggest taking the phone back to your mobile operator's store to have it looked at for any potential screen issues.
