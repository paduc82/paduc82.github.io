windows 10 languages

is it possible to change my licenced swedish windows 10 version to another language version (within the same licence) ?
ian ser say:

Hello,

We'd like to clarify if you're referring to changing the display language of your Windows 10 device. If yes, then follow the steps below to change the language used in Windows 10:

  1. Press Windows key + I on your keyboard to open the Settings app or you can also click Start to open the Start menu and then click Settings.
  2. Click Time & language, and then click Region & language.
  3. Under Languages, click Add a language.
  4. Choose your preferred language from the list.
  5. Select the added language and click Options. Download the language pack and other necessary options for the language.
  6. Go back to the previous window by pressing the back arrow from the left part of the window. Click the added language then click Set as default.
  7. The change would be effective on your next sign-in.

Let us know how the language change went.
