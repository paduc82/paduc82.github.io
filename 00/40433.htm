When the Xbox doesn't see of connect to any wireless network, the Xbox usually just needs to be power cycled.


If the Xbox is not seeing the network, power cycle the Xbox by unplugging the Xbox from the wall outlet and let it sit for about 5 minutes.

Plug the Xbox back into a wall outlet and not a power strip.
