jay freedman say:

Word's Find mechanism can find text in the body of comments (along with the same text in the main part of the document), but it can't find reviewer names or the date/time of the comments. If the tracked changes are displayed, their text can be found, but again there's no way to find them by date or reviewer. In both cases, if you want to see changes and comments made by a specific person, click Review > Show Markup > Specific People and uncheck all the names you don't want to see.

As an alternative, you could use the macro that I posted 11 years ago to generate a table of revisions with their dates/times, and that table could be sorted by any of its columns. With a little effort, the macro can be expanded to include the name of the person who made the change, and to include comments as well as tracked changes.
