woodg say:

No, they DO NOT have the SAME email address as you. They may be using your email address when registering on sites, and so you get the email, or they may have a similar email address to you, and have used it incorrectly,but their own email address is most definitely not the same as yours.

For example, each of these are unique email addresses:

john.smith @ Hotmail.com

johnsmith @ hotmail.com

john-smith @ hotmail.com

So, if my email address is john.smith @ hotmail.com but I register at a web site with johnsmith @ Hotmail.com, then I won't get the emails, but someone else will. That's why many websites send you a verification email when you register.
