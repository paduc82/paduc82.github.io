argel obm say:

Hi Kevin,

You can set up a rule in Outlook.com to forward all of the messages in your old Outlook.com account to the new one. This way, you will be able to transfer your emails to the new account all at once. To do this, follow these steps: (NOTE: The steps below is for the new version (beta) of Outlook.com)

  1. Access your old account.
  2. In the upper right part of Outlook.com, click the gear icon.
  3. Select View full settings.
  4. On the left pane, click Mail.
  5. On the middle plane, choose Rules.
  6. On the right pane, click Add new rule.
  7. In number 1, input the name of the rule.
  8. In number 2, click the drop-down menu and choose Apply to all messages.
  9. In number 3, click the the drop-down menu and select Forward to.
  10. In the text in number 3, type in the email address of your new account.
  11. Click Save.
  12. Wait for Outlook.com to finish applying the rule in your old account.

Let us know how it goes.
