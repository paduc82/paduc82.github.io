emredrum say: The charge rate on a 6288 charger differs to that of the other nokia chargers that ALSO use the same connector to the phone to power.

The difference is the AC-3A has a 65mA setting.
Chargers like AC-4A fit but have a 125mA setting.

I have found that using a 65mA charger is the first step in troubleshooting this support error.

Next noticable thing is that the phone will also at times charge to full, then fail to continue to trickle charge while connected to a power source. Perhaps some discharge feature of the phone?

So if you have this problem, Do consider the charger as the problem. Just find someone with the AC-3A charger to test with, not someone's N80 charger.
