daniel g. grau say:
Word takes the format you copy from and faithfully applies it to the whole selection. You'll need to do a global replacement at the end:
  • Edit > Find > Advanced Find and Replace
  • Click on the Replace tab at the top
  • Find what: ^f (this is the wildcard character for footnote references, also available in the Special dropdown menu at the bottom right)
  • Replace with: Leave blank
  • In the Format dropdown menu at the bottom, select Font…
  • Place a checkmark next to "Superscript" and click OK
  • Click on Replace All
