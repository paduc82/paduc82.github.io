Hi, it's not 7740, as that is a software update related to Exchange email.

What you need is the HTC firmware update for pre-Mango 'phones.

You may in fact find that when 7740 gets round to you (mine arrived this morning) you get the firmware update at the same time. (I found, despite having had the update notification on my 'phone, that I had to check for updates manually through PC Zune Settings>Phone>Update before I got the opportunity to install the 7740 update. So that check might be worth a try.)

If no firmware update turns up with 7740, or if 7740 doesn't become available to you soon, contacting HTC is possibly your best bet, as you don't have a carrier.

Others with carrier unlocked 'phones have asked this question (re HTC and re other manufacturers) on this forum, and I can't find that there have been clear answers, despite some having, it seems, received the firmware update somehow. Therefore, could I request that you keep us posted with progress?

Anyway, post back if you need any more info, hopefully someone will be able to help you.
