There are several ways, including:

1. Use only 1 form, with a tab control with 4 tabs, one for each subform.

2. When opening the second form, use the WhereCondition argument of DoCmd.OpenForm to restrict that form to the current CustomerID:

DoCmd.OpenForm "mySecondForm", WhereCondition:="CustomerID=" & Me.CustomerID
