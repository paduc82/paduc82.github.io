This is a print setup issue, not a Word problem:

  1. With your document open in Word, choose Format>Document and click on the Page Setup button.
  2. Click on the Paper Size dropdown and choose Manage Custom Sizes...
  3. Click on the Plus sign to add a new format, then double-click on Untitled and give the format a name like A4 Borderless.

  5. Double-check that your new size is set in the Paper Size dropdown, then OK out.
  6. Print a borderless PDF using your new page format.

Let's say the formula is in B2, and let's say your condition is true if B2=A2. Write =IF(B2=A2,"",C10*$C$7).
