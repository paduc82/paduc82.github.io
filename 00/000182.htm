Ok, so what you are saying is you want the FIELDNAME for each field that has an X in it to appear in a comma separated list. Now it makes sense. This would be easier if the data was more normalized, but you can do it with a series of IIFs:

IIF([School Supplies] = "X", "School Supplies, ","") & IIF([HMWK ASST] = "X","Homework Assistant, ","") & IIF([0-5 Tardies] = "X"."0-5 Tardies, ","") etc. 

Now this will leave an orphan comma at the end but you can eliminate that with a Left() function.
