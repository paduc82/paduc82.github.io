In MSP2010 (I can't say about MSP2013 or MSP2016), there is no built-in filter for "Not Summary". No such filter appears on the menu of built-in filters, and if you go to more filters, no such filter appears in the complete list of filters.

I don't know where John got his but I suspect that he made one, the same as I have done.

It is easy. Just drop down the list of filters, go to more filters, click new. Call it "AA Not Summaries", and then fill in the field name, test and value on the first line. Summary equals No.

Then sort the result like John says.
