cmdrkeene say:

Sure, it's easy to switch, unlink, or re-link anytime.  In the Settings app, click Accounts, then click the "sign in with a local account" link.  After confirming, you'll be back to a "Windows 7 style" local login with no connected Microsoft account.

As for how it happened, well, there's a few ways.  For example, if you try to use something that requires an online account (like the app store, skype, email/calendar/address book synchronization, cloud backups, and more), it will suggest that you link an account. There is a "no thanks" option at the bottom of those suggestions, but if you just click "next" instead and key in the online password, it will link it and use it for logging in later.
