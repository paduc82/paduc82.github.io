sororke say:

Hi JkMuster,

I had a similar problem (except with my trackpad, and also had a couple of issues with the screen). It turns out that the driver software for some of my hardware needed to be updated to work properly with Win10.

The following should solve your problem, just make sure you're connected to the internet before you follow the steps:

  1. Right click on the Start Menu icon
  2. Choose Device Manager from the list
  3. Scroll down the items & find your mouse (see img)
  4. Right click and choose Update Driver Software...

Another thing you can do is scan for hardware changes from the Device Manager:

  1. Select your computer/device (the first item at the top of the list, usually has your name in it)
  2. Click Action
  3. Choose Scan for hardware changes

This should update any other hardware so it all works smoothly.

Hope this helps!
