Excel takes its cue from System Preferences even though they don't provide specific options for negative values [as well as Currency, Dates, etc.]. What is offered by Excel is determined by the standard conventions of the Region specified in System Preferences.

If you want to use a format that is not conventional for the selected Region you'll have to use a Custom Format.
