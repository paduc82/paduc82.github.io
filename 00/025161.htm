This indicates that the character involved is not available in the font being used to display the content. It could be caused by any of several factors, including;

  • The version of the font called for is different (probably newer) that the version you have installed on your Mac. The newer version includes characters not included in the older version.

  • There are unresolved font issues on your Mac.
