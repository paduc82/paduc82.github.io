Two suggestions:

  1. Run each selection find separately against a test document, do not combine them with if/else commands until after you have fully debugged them. This will help you isolate the problematic portion of the code.
  2. Since you are using the Selection method, you must remember to Collapse the selection range when found. In other words, if you search and find "XYZ" and then change the X to a W, you have to execute a Selection.Collapse WdCollapseEnd statement before looping. And make sure you collapse at a position which is beyond where you originally located something. You don't want your search finding the same string at the same location, over and over again.
