As PC-Learner notes, you cannot activate a clean installed copy of Windows 10 without a retail product key.   The pre-Windows 10 release Insider builds are no longer available.  Now the procedure is to upgrade to Windows 10 from a qualifying OS (or buy Windows 10 at retail) and then elect to receive Insider builds.

"Evaluation" copies of Windows 10 Pro or Home are not "trial" copies.  All .iso files are standard Windows.  Your need is to license a copy so it activates.
