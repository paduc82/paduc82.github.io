julie sheets say:

Shane:

Quick point of clarification -- the detail Gantt does not display lead and lag times.  It displays, "Slippage" and "Free slack".  Lead and lag are part of the dependency relationships (predecessors and successors) and have nothing to do with baselines.

 

JP-PM,

If you've saved a baseline there is already calculated a Work Variance field that calculates the difference between Baseline Work and Work.  If the Work Variance is a positive number, you are overbudget as far as work.  If the Work Variance field is negative, you are under budget as far as work.

I hope this helps.
