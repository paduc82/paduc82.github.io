jay freedman say:

If you can still open Word 2007, then you don't need to reinstall it.

Instead, go to the Windows Control Panel and open Programs & Features. Right-click the Office 365 trial item and choose Uninstall. When that finishes, right-click the Office 2007 item and click Change. In the dialog that opens, make sure the Repair option is selected, and click Continue. When that process finishes, Office 2007 should behave normally.
