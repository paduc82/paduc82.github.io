1. Sign in to your account at https://mail.live.com or https://outlook.com.
2. On the upper-right corner of Outlook.com page, click Gear icon then select More mail settings.
3. Under Managing your account, click Email forwarding.
4. Select Forward your mail to another email account and enter the email address in the box where you want your messages to be sent.
5. Click Save.
