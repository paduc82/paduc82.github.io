anthony.b say:

Here is one way maintain a high image quality, for your PDF:

[1]  Copy the chart image from Excel into PowerPoint, reformat as desired, and save it as a bitmap image.

[2]  Copy the data table into Excel into PowerPoint, reformat as desired, and save it as a bitmap image.

[3]  Open a large blank bitmap image, with Microsoft paint, and copy in the two other images (from steps 1 and 2 above).

[4]  Crop away any unwanted white edges, with Microsoft Paint, and save as a bitmap image (which has the data table, and the chart image).

[5]  Open the bitmap image (data table and chart image) with IrfanView (see note below), and save it as a PDF (when saving, leave at the default "lossless" compression).

The above steps create a very high quality PDF, where the diagonal pattern fill looks very good, no matter how the PDF is zoomed on the screen (also looks very good printed).

Note: IrfanView is free for home usage.  If you use at your business, you are asked to purchase it.  Here is the link for IrfanView:

http://www.irfanview.com/

I hope that the above might be of some help.
