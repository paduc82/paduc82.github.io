kergga say:

Replace all the D21 below with your source cell. I'm sure there is a more elegant way to do this, but this is quick & easy.

 

Do this in a helper column, then just sort on the helper column or copy/paste special values over your original column

 

=TEXT(LEFT(D21,FIND(".",D21)-1),"000")&"."&TEXT(MID(D21,FIND(".",D21)+1,(FIND(".",D21,FIND(".",D21)+1)-FIND(".",D21))-1),"000")&"."&TEXT(MID(D22,FIND(".",D21,FIND(".",D21)+1)+1,(FIND(".",D21,FIND(".",D21,FIND(".",D21)+1)+1)-FIND(".",D21,FIND(".",D21)+1))-1),"000")&"."&TEXT(RIGHT(D21,LEN(D21)-FIND(".",D21,FIND(".",D21,FIND(".",D21)+1)+1)),"000")

Hi

To clarify, what Skype are you using? Is it a regular Skype or a Skype for Business? Because these two are different platform. Or, are you using Skype for your business?

Regarding your queries, please see below:

Q: When I want to send an sms I cannot find the contact?

A: As mentioned by Sergei_P's post, it is possible that you may have signed in to a different account by signing in using your email address instead of your Skype name. To rectify, kindly sign out of Skype, and then sign back in.

 

You can also, identify the username of the account you're signed in with by following the steps in this article to find your Skype name. 

You can only lose your contacts if you manually remove them from your contact list.

Q: I can't correct a wrong number just by going to the digit as I used to be able to easily with the old version so if I make a mistake, I have to delete the whole number!! This is basic!

A: If you're using the new Skype for Android, once the number is saved as your contact option to edit the number is no longer possible. You can only edit the display name.

Q: Most importantly, when clients call us, we do not receive the call and just get a missed call. 

A: For troubleshooting please follow the steps below:

  • Go to your phone settings and look for Applications or Application Manager.

  • Look for Skype and then select Force Stop > Clear cache > Clear Data > Uninstall.

  • After uninstalling kindly do a reboot on your device.

  • Once it is up please install Skype using the Google Play Store.

  • After installing Skype please do another restart before signing in.

 

Please let us know if you have any questions or other concerns.
