hansv mvp say:

You can use COUNTIFS for this. For example, to count the number of values in B1:B100 that are greater than 10 and less than 20:

=COUNTIFS(B1:B100,">10",B1:B100,"<20")

or if the lower and upper bounds are in A1 and A2, respectively:

=COUNTIFS(B1:B100,">"&A1,B1:B100,"<"&A2)
