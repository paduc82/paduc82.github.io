lemp say:

HP serial numbers do not have 25 characters.  You're probably referring to the Windows Product Key.

There is essentially no way to retrieve a Windows Product Key from an OEM computer like your HP laptop.  Although there are utilities that retrieve a product key from Windows, when these utilities are used on an OEM computer (Windows pre-installed at the factory), the key that is retrieved is the generic key used by the manufacturer.  This key cannot be used to do a reinstall of Windows.

If you really need your Product Key and there is only one character you can't read, perhaps knowing that Windows Product Keys use only 24 characters will help:

The 24 used are:
2346789BCDFGHJKMPQRTVWXY

The 12 unused are:
015AEILNOSUZ

On the other hand, HP provides a means to "restore the computer to factory conditions" that does not require the use of a Product Key.  See https://support.hp.com/us-en/document/c01867418
