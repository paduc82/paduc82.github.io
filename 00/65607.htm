Hearthfire FAQs - Microsoft Community

pirate duck say:

Hearthfire FAQs

Hearthfire FAQs I've noticed a lot of confused people on the interwebz about the new skyrim hearthfire DLC. To help clear this up I've compiled some of the most common questions that have been asked and I will answer them to the best of my knowledge.
Q: I downloaded the new DLC but nothing is loading when I go into my game and no courier is coming to me. Why?
A: if you downloaded it from within the start menu, you must go to your dashboard and re-load the game. The easiest place to find the courier is in whiterun.
Q: how come I only got 1 letter from the courier about upgrading my breezehome or adopting a child, but not about where to go?
A: this seems to be one of the minor glitches in the game. Don't fret, as all of the DLC content will still be available to you.
Q: where do I go to get the land deeds and how much are they?
A: the 3 towns are morthal, falkreath, and dawnstar. You must talk to the jarls steward and they are 5000 septims each.
Q: the jarls steward(s) won't give me an option to buy land. What gives?
A: as with being able to buy a house in any other town, you must do a favor for the jarl or become thane.
Q: it doesn't show where my plot of land is on my map. Did I even buy anything?
A: yes, don't worry. To find your land, look under the "misc quests" tab and select it, then select your misc missions. It will plop an arrow right where you need to go.
Q: can I put the land anywhere I want?
A: no. All three are predetermined for you. 
Q: where are the quarry and clay deposits, sawn logs, and glass and straw at? And where do I get the building materials like nails and hinges?
A: quarries and clay deposits are within a very close proximity to the house. Quarry is a very light grey with white lines (similar to normal ore deposits) and clay is a brown/maroon color on the ground. Sawn logs can be purchased from any saw mill owner. They are 200 septims for 20 logs. Glass and straw can be found in general goods stores only (if you've played oblivion, it's similar to buying furniture), and every once and awhile, out in the wild or caves/dungeons. They will also sell child's clothing and toys. The other building material can be made at an anvil (usually next to the carpenters table) or any smithing pit (can think of the name, drawing a blank). They all take iron ingots and locks take an extra corundum ingot to make. You will make 2 hinges per ingot and 10 nails per, where everything else is 1:1 ratio.
Q: how can I make someone my steward?
A: some followers will be made into stewards once you create the "main hall" part of the house. Bring a follower to the house and they will ask if you want them to be your steward for that house. Not all followers can do this, mainly townsfolk like faendal, and for the most part, no warrior type followers.
Q: what kinds of things can I make for the house?
A: there are 3 options per wing, and 3 wings per house. 3 of the options are for a more homey feel like bedrooms and kitchen, while others can be for the warrior or Mage, like the armory or enchanting wing. Since you have 3 houses, you are able to mix and match what you want.
Q: how do I buy a horse for the stable and animals for the pens?
A: talk to your steward and select "I want to upgrade the house". You can buy a horse, cow, chickens, carriage, and a bard. You can also give him money to buy upgrades or make renovations in your home.
Q: I want to start adopting kids. Where can I find them?
A: the orphanage in riften has a big selection, but each city has a few kids running around after dark that you can adopt. A good time to find them is around 1-2 am when everyone's asleep. Talk to them and you will get the option to adopt them.
Q: how can I find toys for the kids?
A: some toys, like dolls and wooden swords, can be found while dungeon diving. Others can be found in the general goods store after you adopt kids.

This issue can be caused if any of the following situations exist:

The currently loaded USB driver has become unstable or corrupt
Your PC requires an update for issues that may conflict with a USB external hard drive and Windows
Windows may be missing other important updates hardware or software issues
Your USB controllers may have become unstable or corrupt
Your external drive may be entering selective suspend
Your PC motherboard may need updated drivers

Resolution
To resolve this issue, follow the steps in the methods below in the order listed. If the first method does not fix the problem, proceed to the next.

Method 1: Uninstall and then reconnect the external hard drive
This method resolves issues where the currently loaded USB driver has become unstable or corrupt.

Select Start, type Device Manager in the Search box
Select Device Manager from the returned list.
Select Disk Drives from the list of hardware
Press and hold (or right-click) the USB external hard drive with the issue, and select Uninstall.
After the hard drive is uninstalled, unplug the USB cable.
Wait for 1 minute and then reconnect the USB cable. The driver should automatically load
Check for the USB drive in Windows Explorer

Note Connecting your USB external hard drive into a non-powered USB hub can cause a lack of enough power to operate the external drive. Instead, plug it directly into your computer.

If your problem still persists, proceed to Method 3.

Method 2: Install the latest Windows Updates
This method will install the latest device drivers for your USB external hard drive.

Select the Start button, type Windows Update in the Search box, and then select Windows Update in the results pane.
Select Check for Updates. After the scan is complete, select Review optional updates.
Select the check box next to the updates, then select Install updates.
If prompted, review the license agreement, then select I Accept.
Follow the onscreen instructions to download and install the updates.
If prompted, reboot your computer.

If your problem still exits, proceed to Method 4.

Method 3: Reinstall USB controllers.
This method resolves steps where the currently loaded USB driver has become unstable or corrupted.

Select Start, then type device manager in the Search box, and then select Device Manager.
Expand Universal Serial Bus controllers. Press and hold (or right-click) a device and select Uninstall. Repeat for each device.
Once complete, restart your computer. Your USB controllers will automatically install.

If your problem still exists, proceed to Method 5.

Method 4: Disable USB selective suspend setting.
This method prevents your USB external drive from powering down.

Select the Start button, type power plan in the Search box, and then select Choose a power plan.
Next to your currently selected plan, select Change Plan Settings.
Select Change advanced power settings.
Select the box to expand USB Settings > USB selective suspend settings.
Select Plugged in, select the drop down menu, and then select disabled.
If you're using a laptop, select Battery, select the drop down menu, and then select disabled.
Select Apply > OK.

Method 5: Install your motherboard's latest chipset drivers.
This method updates your motherboard's chipset drivers, so your computer will recognize your USB external hard drive.

Review your computer's documentation which should contain the name of the motherboard manufacturer.
Visit your computer manufacturer's support website. For a list of computer manufacturers' support sites, see Computer manufacturers' contact information.
Navigate their website to find the appropriate drivers for your motherboard. For assistance, contact your computer manufacturer.

Not surprising that you were getting a File not found (-1811) error. The bit inside quotes is supposed to be the complete path to the database file. If the Windows user name for the user whose database needs defragmenting is Victoria, and the Windows Live Mail store folder is in its default location, the command would be:
esentutl.exe -d "C:\Users\Victoria\AppData\Local\Microsoft\Windows Live Mail\Mail.MSMessageStore"

Be prepared to readjust the ownership of the file to the Windows user concerned; after defragmenting it will have reverted to System and attempting to launch the program will throw error 0x800C0155.
