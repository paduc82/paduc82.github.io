Assumption for this reply: everything was normal and "all of sudden" you're getting these messages

Cause:

The email address has been hijacked by a spammer and email is being sent to the universe to "appear" as if it's coming from your email address.

Solution:

#1 - Change email addresses OR

#2 - Just wait it out - it will stop and things will return to normal. Just a real nuisance (pain) until it does.

Have had this happen to a number of email accounts over the years. For the short-term, set up a "rule" to deal with (delete) those kinds of messages. The last time I had this occur - woke up to some 2500 + messages of that sort - finally stopped during the subsequent 24 hrs.

One thing to help minimize hijaccking from occurring - is NEVER use (post) your email address publicly on any website where it can be harvested. If you want to include your email address - do it by using something like <myname AT mydomain com> instead of using the <proper> notation.
