Hi Jamie,

You can use Conditional Formatting to do this. The way I would suggest to do it is to have a separate cell that you can call whatever you wish. Give this Cell a value of

=TODAY()+30

That would give you a cell that always has a value of 1 month away. After that you can use a simple conditional formatting rule like below.

Format Only Cells That Contain
Cell Value > greater than or equal too > Cell used above

Then set format as desired. You could use a formula in the conditional formatting but its a little trickier that way and less reliable!
