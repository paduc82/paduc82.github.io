Try this:

=SUMPRODUCT(LOOKUP($B$3:$F$5,{"hi","lo","mid"},{100,1,50})*(A3:A5=B9)*((B1:F1=A9)*B2:F2))

The formula refers to A9 for the "Monday" and B9 for "Paul".

susan_s. say:

Hello,

We, at Skype, no longer have an option to delete accounts. Microsoft aimed to bring convenience on your end and that means you do not need to contact us to process the cancellation.

To set your expectation, if you proceed to delete your Skype account, the associated Microsoft account will be closed as well. This is due to the full migration of Skype to Microsoft and one credentials to all Microsoft products and services.

Your account will be closed after 30 days, but you if you change your mind anytime during the waiting period, all you have to do is sign in to your account again to cancel the closure.

The 30- days waiting period helps keep hackers from changing your account‚Äôs security info as a way to completely takeover of the account. This waiting period gives the real account owner enough time to be alerted of the change and to cancel it in case they didn‚Äôt start it.

A possible reason why the account closure process takes you to your Hotmail email address was you're signed in to Skype using the email address and not the correct Skype account.

To get the issue resolved, using in-private browser, manually signed in using the correct Skype account (not the email address) and follow the steps on how to close your Skype account.

Hope this helps! Let us know if you need further assistance.
