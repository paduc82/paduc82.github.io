jack cook - aka help_line say:

If your text was on the phone and a sync completed (so it was placed on the MyPhone web portal) and THEN deleted from the phone, it will automatically be placed in the archive.  If you delete an item BEFORE it has sync'd with MyPhone, there is no way to get it back via the MyPhone service.

From the MyPhone FAQ:

 

How do I know if my data was synced?

After your phone has synced its content with the server, you‚Äôll see a message telling you that the sync was successful.

When you click Show details, you‚Äôll get detailed information about what was synchronized.

Sign in to your My Phone account to see the data that you synced.

 

What does it mean to ‚Äúarchive‚Äù something?

Archiving is a way to free up space on your phone. An ‚Äúarchived‚Äù item remains in the archive section of yourMy Phone account, but is removed from the phone. To restore an archived item to the phone, sign in to yourMy Phone account, select the Archive view from the left panel, click or tap the item, then clickRestore. The item will be transferred to your phone the next time that you sync.

Previously synced items that are deleted from your phone are automatically moved to the Archive folder in your My Phone account.

Items deleted from the Archive are permanently deleted.
