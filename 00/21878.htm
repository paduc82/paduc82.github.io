franky chen msft say:

Hi Reginald Andre,

 

From your description, you are using the same domains for Office 365 and Mandrill app as the sending domains.

 

Given the situation, you need to set one SPF record as below.

 

Values: v=spf1 include:sharepointonline.com include:spf.mandrillapp.com -all

 

For Office 365, "sharepointonline.com" in an SPF record includes email from both Exchange Online (Outlook) and SharePoint Online, you can refer to the following article to check the detailed information.

https://support.office.com/en-us/article/External-Domain-Name-System-records-for-Office-365-c0531a6f-9e25-4f2d-ad0e-a70bfef09ac0#BKMK_SPFrecords

Franky
