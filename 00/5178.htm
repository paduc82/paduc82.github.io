john w. vinson say:

Access databases are not "documents", and they are handled differently than (say) Word documents or Excel workbooks. A database (.accdb file) is a container for multiple Forms, Reports, Tables, Queries and code. If you have such a database open, then you cannot open it again as a different instance (well, not without some expert gyrations). You can create new Tables, or Forms, or whatever, but they are objects within the database file, not external disk files.
