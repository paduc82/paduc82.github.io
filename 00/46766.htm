original title: Desktop Extention

I have 2 acer P166HQL monitors and have it connected to a video splitter which is connected to my laptop via a VGA cable. The screens are currently being recognized as 1 screen not as 2. I want 1 screen to duplicate my laptop's display and the other to extend it. How do I do this?

trekdozer say: The VGA output on your laptop can only send one video signal - if you use a splitter both monitors will show the same image. Your laptop wont recognize both monitors with a video splitter, just one.

Some laptops have both VGA and HDMI out - if yours does then you can use the HDMI port for a different image then the VGA.
