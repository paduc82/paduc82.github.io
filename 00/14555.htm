john mcghie say:
It sounds to me as if you were using the "AUTONUMLGL" field-based numbering that is built in to Word.  You may be using the ListNum field with the "LegalDefault" parameter.  It's the same thing: they are both "ListNum" fields, the older name was used in Word 2000.

Go to Insert>Field...

In "Categories" choose "Numbering"

In "Field" choose "AutoNumLgl"

For instructions, look here:
http://office.microsoft.com/en-us/word-help/field-codes-autonumlgl-field-HP005186136.aspx

and here:
http://office.microsoft.com/en-us/word-help/field-codes-listnum-field-HP005189547.aspx

More complete instructions are here:
http://support.microsoft.com/kb/162895

and here:
http://www.word.mvps.org/FAQs/Numbering/NumberingExplained/NumberingInAction/FieldNumbers.htm

Have a play.  When you get them working, save the result as an AutoCorrect named "ara1" and you will get the behaviour you are used to. (You must have four characters in the name for AutoCorrect to work properly).
