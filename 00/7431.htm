charles kenyon say:
Not good news I'm afraid.

Mirror margins apply only to the left and right margins.
Different even and odd pages has to do with headers and footers.
Sections / Headers and Footers in Microsoft Word 2007-2010

Chances are you have a bunch of manual page breaks and empty paragraphs. You need to turn on display of non-printing formatting characters to see these.

Page breaks should normally just happen. If needed for organizational reasons, they should be a part of paragraph style definitions - Keep with next for headings, keep lines together for paragraphs that need to all be on one page, and page break before for headings that should start on a separate page.

There should be no empty paragraphs. 2.2 Why you should press Enter only once to end a paragraph

If you have done your formatting using Styles and do not have manual page breaks, your pagination should be good. There may still be some blank pages if you used odd-page section breaks, but they should be where you intend them to be.

charles kenyon say:
Do your work on a copy!

You can get rid of manual page breaks and empty paragraphs using the Replace function.

Replace empty paragraphs by replacing ^p^p with ^p and repeating.

Replace manual page breaks by replacing ^m with ^p.

Then apply appropriate paragraph styles.

Refer to your original to make sure you are getting the look you want.
