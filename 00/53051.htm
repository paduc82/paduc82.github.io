a. user say:

Hi,

 

If it is not working on your Live Mail, please check it on Microsoft Hotmail.

 

It is possible that the account that you've added on your blocked senders list is still on your contacts, please try to delete it first by following the steps below.

 

1. Login to http://people.live.com/.

2. Tick the check box of the contact that you want to delete -> Click Delete 

 

In addition, please see the solution below on how to block all the junk emails or unwanted senders.

 

It is possible that your account is not secured enough for junk e-mails. To help you with this, please set your Junk e-mail filtering level. You can use the junk e-mail filter to help decrease the amount of junk e-mail that appears in your Inbox. Messages from senders not on those lists are automatically sent to the Junk e-mail folder.

 

 

Please follow the steps below on how to do this:

 

1. Sign in to the Microsoft Hotmail website with your Microsoft Hotmail account.
2. In the upper-right corner of the page, click Options, and then click More options.
3. Under Preventing junk email, click Filters and reporting.
4. Under Filters and reporting, select the options that you want, and then click Save.

 

Note: You should occasionally check your junk e-mail folder to make sure that good messages are not sent there by mistake.

 

 

You can also use message rules to manage your inbox especially if you are receiving multiple spam messages from the same sender. To do this, please refer to the steps below:

 

1. Sign in to Account.
2. Click Option, then More Option (inside Inbox page).
3. Click on Rules for sorting new messages under Customizing Hotmail.
4. Under Rules for sorting new messages, click New.
5. Create your own rule to apply.
6. Click Save.

 

I would also recommend that you scan your computer with the latest anti-virus software immediately and change your password immediately to protect your account.

 

 

Thanks!
