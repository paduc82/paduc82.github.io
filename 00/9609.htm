john korchok say:

If the placeholder has Shrink text on overflow turned on, over-filling the text box will cause both the text size and line spacing to shrink. To change this behavior, open the presentation or template and use View>Slide Master to open the Slide Master view:

  1. Right-click on the placeholder on the Slide Master and choose Format Shape.
  2. Select the Text Box pane.
  3. Change Shrink text on overflow to either Do no Autofit or Resize shape to fit text.
  4. Go though the slide layouts to check whether all placeholders have inherited your preferred Autofit setting and manually change any that didn't change on their own.
  5. Close Slide Master view and try pasting in lots of text to see the effect.

hi andrew,

from your description, i have run a test and found:
1. if i send from a dg to an office 365 account (with auto-reply set up), the original email can be received but not the auto-reply. if i view the office 365 message trace result, it shows “failed”.
