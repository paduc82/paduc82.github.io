To search the entire C: drive in Vista go to Start / Search Box and type insimplex EXT:TEX and enter.  As you're typing you'll see two hyperlinks appear just above where you're typing and one will say Search Everywhere.  Click on that.  That will do a quick indexed search and bring up a dialog box.  Click on Advanced Search.  Click on the location drop down menu and find Drive C: and click on it.Check the box "include non-indexed, hidden, and system files (may be slow)."  Then click on Search.  You will now be searching your entire hard drive for the specified word in TEX file types.  And yes, it's not a very efficient way of searching the entire drive for a file but that's how it's done in Vista.

mister wizard say:

ligriV,

To expand on Lorien's reply, your syntax is correct but a default search searches only indexed locations and only content whose file types have been specified for indexing.  If TEX is an extension that you work with regularly, you should specify the parent folder(s) for indexing and specify that this extension be indexed for content.  Only the most common file types are indexed for content by default.

These options are found in the Indexing Options dialog.  Type "Indexing Options" into the Start menu search window to find the dialog and click on the result.

To change your display name on the Skype website,

1. Log in to your account using to the Skype website: Skype website
http://www.skype.com/

2. Click on Sign in and choose My Account.

3 Scroll down and locate Edit profile under Account details.

4. Under Personal information, you will see your current display name, click on Edit profile on the right part of the page.

5. Enter your desired display name and click on Save.

Note: If this doesn't work on your current browser, you might want to try to use a different browser to change your name.


To change your display name using your Skype client, follow these:

1. Open up the Skype application and click on your profile picture.

2. On the right pane of the client, you will see your account details, above your Skype name will be your account's display name, click on it and the display name will be highlighted.

Note: To distinguish the Skype name and the display name, your Skype name will be after the word Accounts while your display name will be in a larger font size.

3. Change the display name to a name you prefer and click on the check icon on the right side of the box.
