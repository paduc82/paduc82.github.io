Yeah, it does occur on occasion.

 

A soft reset will often fix it.  If not remove the memory card , soft reset again then re-insert.

 

Some have founf that if they rename storage card to something else, turn the device off and on again they then find storage card 2 becomes storage card you can then delete the one you renamed.

Why does it occur?  I believe it is an issue with the speed of loading the info on the card.  Something gets wacked, it recognizes info on the card that must be used so it sets up a new card.
