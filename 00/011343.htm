steven. s say:

Hey Rob,

 

The behavior of Internet Explorer only remembering the very last window closed is by design. The two workarounds have already been mentioned, grouping tabs and having pop-ups open as a new tab instead of new window.

 

This happens because each new window becomes a new session. Internet Explorer is designed to remember the last, not necessarily the most important, window/session that was open. Having all pop-ups open in new tabs will give you the session restore functionality that you are looking for, but will take some adjusting to when browsing.

 

One thing that may help you to adjust to having new windows open in a new tab, is to enable Always switch to new tabs when they are created.  This option is found under Tools > Internet Explorer > Settings (under the Tabs heading)

 

Hope that helps.
