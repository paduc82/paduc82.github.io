Just select Format > Document, select the Layout tab at the top and click on "Different first page." That will give you an independent header/footer for the first page, and a common header/footer for the rest of the document.

As for the page numbers being bold, just select them and cancel the bold attribute (Command B or use the Ribbon's Home tab and click on the B button).

If you choose one of the Built-In header styles from the dropdown menu in the Ribbon's Header and Footer purple tab (which only shows after you do View > Header and Footer), pay attention to the clarifications in parenthesis, as Word can also give you different headers/footers for even and odd pages.
