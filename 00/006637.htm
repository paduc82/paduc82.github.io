By default, when you paste a paragraph of text from one document to another, the style definition of the target will take precedence. Try reversing the process, that is, paste into the numbered document instead of pasting from it (create a copy of the document if necessary).

First of all, though, make sure that numbering is correctly setup; see http://www.shaunakelly.com/word/numbering/OutlineNumbering.html.

Rows in Excel go from top to bottom - row 1 at the top, row 2 below that etc.

But the vertical axis of ALL charts in Excel goes upwards by default, i.e. the lowest value at the bottom and the highest value at the top:

So by default, the vertical axis is in the opposite direction to the rows.

Double-click the vertical axis.

Select Axis Options > Axis Options.

Tick the check box 'Categories in reverse order'.

This will move the horizontal axis to the top. If you don't want that, select 'At maximum category' under 'Horizontal axis crosses'.

If you want to have your emails and folders sync to all of your devices, remove your email account and add them again as an IMAP email account.

  • Outgoing mail server: smtp-mail.outlook.com

    Encryption: TLS
