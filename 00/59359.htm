    randyau93 say:

    If you pre-ordered the game in January using an attached payment method, that payment method would have been charged only in the last week or so. You can still pre-load the game, but if the payment didn't go through, the license wouldn't be added to your account. You should check your bank statement to see when Microsoft tried to take payment (probably as early as 12 February) and your Microsoft account purchase history to learn more.

    To expand on this: pre-ordered games are not charged immediately upon pre-order, especially if you pre-order weeks/months in advance of the launch. You won't be charged for the game until about 10 days from launch, to protect you in case the game is delayed or (at worst) cancelled. If you pay using just Microsoft account funds, then you would be charged immediately. However, if you purchase using any attached payment system (such as a credit/debit card, Paypal, etc.), even if it's a partial payment along with MS account funds, the charge won't be attempted until about 10 days out.
