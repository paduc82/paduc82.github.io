If the music files are in MP3 or WMA format, then most information is also stored in the files themselves, so this information will be preserved when the files are moved. There is a Windows process called...

that sometimes causes high CPU when playing video... Which may be happening on one of the websites you have open... It can do this under other circumstances as well...
