I am just stumped..  Any ideas?????

john korchok say:

PDF is not one of Publisher's graphic formats. You have several options:

  • In Publisher, choose Insert>Object>Create from File>Browse and insert the file. This depends on having compatible PDF software installed, some people have had problems with Acrobat Reader DC not supporting this operation.
  • In Reader, use Edit>Take a Snapshot. This creates a screen grab of the area you drag your mouse over, it can be a larger area than what shows on your screen. Then either paste the picture directly in Publisher, or paste it into an image editor and save as a compatible format like JPEG or PNG. The larger you zoom before taking a snapshot, the higher-resolution the final file will be, up to the original resolution at which you scanned the image.
  • If your image editing software supports it, import the PDF and save as a compatible format.

Hardware acceleration is a feature in Internet Explorer 9 and later versions that lets Internet Explorer move all graphics and text rendering from the CPU to the Graphics Processing Unit (GPU).
