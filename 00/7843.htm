Sub ConvertTextToNumber()

  On Error Resume Next

  Set rRangeToConvert = ActiveSheet.Cells(1, 1).CurrentRegion

Hi Gary, put the 3 numbers in a column, say A1, A2 and A3

Basically it is:

=AVERAGE(First Cell + colon + last cell)

Hi Gary,

Select the Cells you will be adding times into (A1 - A3 for example)
Right click and choose 'Format Cells'
Choose Time Format and the second one down in the example time formats
Then in your cells for the above times, enter:

00:12:34

Then in the cell where you want the formula to be enter:

Since there is a red (x), it means there is no corresponding image in the Facebook service and, therefore, the issue has a downstream effect on the phone. The error won't go away unless the contact has a valid photo.
