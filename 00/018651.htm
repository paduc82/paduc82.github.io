hpholm say:

It comes down to the IPS LCD (Lumia 1320) versus AMOLED (Lumia 950) screen:
Lumia phones with AMOLED panels don't have the screen time-out option 'never' since this screen technology is burn-in prone.

The maps app should wake up the screen when needed - along with the voice guided navigation instructions?
