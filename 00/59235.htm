If your Windows 10 is activated you can reinstall with the 64bit Media Creation Tool.  Go to 

https://www.microsoft.com/en-gb/software-download/windows10

and be sure to choose the 64bit version.  Create a dvd or usb.

Do a file backup to an external device.  Do not do a system backup since that would simply restore the 32bit system.  You will have to reinstall your programs and restore your files from the file backup.  Insert the media you created and restart the computer.  

This is a clean install.  Migrating between 32bits and 64bits requires a complete reinstallation of Windows.  When you see a screen offering you an opportunity to enter a product key, do not make any entry (you don't have a key) and click SKIP or DO LATER to proceed.  Your computer is already registered for Windows 10 so it will activate as soon as Windows connects to the internet.
