    Bluetooth and wireless devices (like a mouse) signal is stronger than a WiFi signal, also they use more channels than WiFi to transfer less data (especially Bluetooth - that's why you can't stream music via WiFi from YouTube and stream audio to a Bluetooth speakers/headphones).

    When the interference is low (sometimes all you need to do is change the USB port for a wireless device, so it's not too close to your network card/antenna), the network speed slows down.
