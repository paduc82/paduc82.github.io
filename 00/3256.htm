When inserting a spreadsheet in OneNote, all the changes will only appear in OneNote and not on the original file. This happens because you have already downloaded the spreadsheet into OneNote which is equivalent of just making a copy of your spreadsheet. 

Having said that, all the modifications will only be saved in OneNote's temp folder in C Drive and not on your OneDrive or on the original file location. Likewise, if you change the original Excel spreadsheet, your changes won't appear in the copy in OneNote.
