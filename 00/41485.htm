If none of those work try to System Restore to a point before the problem began: http://home.bt.com/tech-gadgets/computing/how-t.... Make sure System Restore is always enabled for C with plenty of disk space apportioned (5-10%) in its Configuration settings.

If this was caused by Version 1709 you can roll back Fall Creators Version 1709 in Settings>Update & Security>Recovery and then hide it until it matures using the Hide Updates Tool downloaded from here: http://download.microsoft.com/download/f/2/2/f2...

I posted this on another thread that explains what I did to get my network functional again after connecting with all of my network adapter to limited functionality.  I am not saying this is the only cause for this problem but it is what worked for me.

AS I am seeing this is a very common problem, one of which I have now encountered on two of my computers and have now got working on one of them, I am hoping this may be a possible solution for the majority of people out there. I attempted to contact customer support, which the level one tech support staff could not help, unfortunately I could not get through to the level 2 support staff as the hold time killed the battery on both of my cordless phones waiting on an available support agent, so trust me I know everyone's frustrations.

Make sure to run the updates those apps as they will be reset to original virus and malware definitions.

I hope this helps, as it was the only thing I could figure out that did work for me.
