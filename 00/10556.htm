Here's one way...

Open two instances of Publisher side by side.

Open your flyer in one instance and create a new blank
5"x7" publication in the other instance.

Copy/paste the content of your flyer to the 5x7...and
adjust where necessary because the aspect ratio is
different and the content will not be a perfect fit.

Two ways to copy the content...

2) Left click / drag a box around the entire publication
and it will select everything in it...then...type...Ctrl+C
to copy.

Next...right click your blank 5x7 and choose...paste.

I HAVE NOW GOT THEM WORKING THOUGH:

I never had to delete any rules. What I did was:

- make sure all rules had "arrived from specified account" (half exchange, half gmail).
- removed some conditions that were on the rules that were no longer necessary due to the "arrived from specified account" part.
- ensure all rules were client-side rather than server rules.
- deleted a few rules that were looking for spam that's so rare it wasn't worth having a rule (if a rule isn't triggered at least once a week, no point having it).

After all that tweaking and pruning of rules/conditions, they are all now working. I don't know which specific step it was that resolved it during my epic rule-editing session.

Lesson learned:

When you have multiple rules, beware when switching an account from IMAP to Gmail if you have an exchange account also. The merging of the rules can go wrong.
