I always keep my Exchange Account in its own profile to keep Outlook from tripping. Outlook tends to consider Exchange as the default account no matter which account you are using or which you've set as your default. So when you need to do an activity that requires connecting to the Outlook Address Book Service for resolving a new address and your Exchange Account is not connected, Outlook will just fibrillate.

You have a pretty unusual setup here. When you posts questions about a profile like this, it would be good to include your configuration with your first post. It will save a lot of sleuthing time for the volunteers here.
