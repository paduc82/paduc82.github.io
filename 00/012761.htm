nokia asha 311

abi99 (2) say:

You want to enable the loudspeaker. After placing the phone on a table, you should see six icons below the called number, one is the loudspeaker.

From your description, I guess, your display stays off and does not reveal those six icons. The Nokia 311 has a proximity sensor. That sensor tries to detect your ear and turns off the display while calling. That saves energy. That sensor is placed on the upper right corner of the display. When you place the phone against light, you see two round circles there. The left one is the proximity sensor. Please, make sure nothing does obstruct that sensor (like a screen protector or dirt). In that case, you have to press the key-lock button twice. Now, you should see those icons and the loudspeaker button.

in a cell, say in D9. In the cell where you want the hyperlink, enter the formula

=HYPERLINK("mailto:"&D9)

referring to the cell with the VLOOKUP formula. This should work even when the workbook BrokerLog info.xlsx is closed.
