paul herber say:

Use of the phrase 'tile' will not be understood.

There is no function to return the printed page the shape is on. That cannot work and the shape may overlap many printed pages.

However, if your purposes are very simple then you could do some arithmetic and work it out. You know the shape position (use the PinX and PinY values to get the shape centre) and from the Page properties you know the PaperKInd as well as the total width and height. FRom them you can calculate which printed page any shape is on.
