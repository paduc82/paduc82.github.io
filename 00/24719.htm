Welcome to Microsoft Community. I recognized that you need a separate Inbox and your main account had become an alias address. I am sorry to inform you that it is not possible to have separate inbox for your primary and alias account. However, you can create a message rule that can sort out your incoming messages for your accounts.

 

Please refer to these instructions, if you want to have a message rule on your account.

 

1. Sign in your account and go to your Inbox.

2. Click the cog icon located at the upper right of the page and select More mail settings.

3. Under Customizing Outlook, click Rules for sorting new messages.

4. Select New.

5.Choose this condition in step no. 1 To or Cc line, contains your preferred account name.

6. Under step 2, choose your preferred condition to Move to Inbox, any existing folder  and new folder.

7. Click Save.

 

For your main account that became an alias address. You can revert it back to your main account by following these steps:

 

1. Sign in here http://account.live.com/.

2. Under Overview and  Account aliases, select Add or change aliases.

3. Look for your primary account name and select Make primary.

4. Follow the on-screen instructions.

If the documents you upload to SharePoint Online are checked out automatically, I suggest you check if the document library is set to require check-out of files for editing:
