Section breaks can be "Continuous," which means that a new section begins in the middle of a page. The headers and footers for these sections are not accessible, but the Page Number Format settings within the sections can affect the headers/footers in subsequent sections. You need to correct the page numbering problems where they start, which is in those "landlocked" sections.

dann ave say:

Hello,

If you wish to move emails not coming from your Outlook contacts, you can perform the steps in Outlook 2016:

  1. Make sure that your Inbox is highlighted on the left side pane.
  2. From Home, click on Rules.
  3. Select Create Rule...
  4. Click on Advance Options to open the Rules Wizard.
  5. Keep the condition(s) blank and click Next.
    *A pop-up will show informing you that the rule will be applied to every message you receive. Click on yes.
  6. For the action(s), put a check on move it to the specified folder.
  7. Select Junk folder and click OK.
  8. Click on Next.
  9. For the exemption(s), put a checkon except if sender is in specified Address Book.
  10. Select Outlook Address Book as the address list and click Add.
  11. Click on Next.
  12. Give the rule a name and select the rule options that you deem appropriate.
  13. Click on Finish.

Should you have further questions, or need further assistance, feel free to reply on this thread.
