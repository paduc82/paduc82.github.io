If your phone was able to use a wifi connection but not mobile data, we advise you to contact your service provider as there might be a problem either on your SIM or on the configuration of your data connection.

Some of the reasons why mobile data doesn't work are:

1. Geographical location - There are certain places where there are either no data connection or the connection is poor, possibly due to the area being surrounded by towers that obstruct the signal coming from your service provider's towers.

2. SIM card malfunction - There are cases where SIM cards need to be replaced. Failing to make a phone call or connect to the internet are some of the symptoms of your SIM malfunctioning.

3. Access Point Names (APN) - Mobile phones need to be properly configured in order to connect to the internet. In this case, even if the phone can make a call or send a text message, the phone cannot access the web. This is usually requested by people who do not want to use their phone to surf the web, to prevent casual charges.
