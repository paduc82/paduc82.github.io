tom ogilvy say:

pinehills

Say your data is in row 3 from A3:BB3.  Assume n = 5: 

=SUM(INDEX(A3:BB3,1,LARGE(IF(A3:BB3<>"",COLUMN(A3:BB3)),5)):BB3)

Enter with Ctrl+Shift+Enter rather than just enter since this is an array formula.

Assumes you will have at least 5 (n) values in the row.
