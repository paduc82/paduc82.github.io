Hi Toby,

Thanks for your clarifying.

However, I’d like to explain that OneDrive-personal and OneDrive-Business are integrated into the OneDrive app. Therefore, there will be the following behavior:

1.OneDrive-personal and OneDrive-Company are both synced.

In this situation, you will not experience the behavior you mentioned and everything is OK.

2.OneDrive-personal is not synced(no matter it is unlinked or has never been synced as you said) and One-Drive-Company is synced.

In this situation, as long as you click the OneDrive-personal icon in file explorer, it will launch a new file explorer Window displaying the synced One-Drive for Business library, since OneDrive-personal is not synced. Even if you click OneDrive-Personal first, the phenomenon is the same.
