john korchok say:

I am assuming that the title is the text appearing in the Heading 1 style on page 1. If that is true, then you can relay that title to the header by using a StyleRef field:

  1. In Word, open the template, then choose View>Header and Footer.
  2. Click where you want the title to appear.
  3. Choose Insert>Field.
  4. In the Categories list, select Links and References.
  5. In the Field names list, select StyleRef (at the bottom).
  6. Click on Options, then on Styles.
  7. Select Heading 1, then click on Add to Field. OK out.

Anything typed in Heading 1 will be relayed to the header field, so instruct your users to only use Heading 1 for the title.
