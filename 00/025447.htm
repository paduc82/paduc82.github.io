how do you turn off bullets in word 2013? - Microsoft Community

andrewburnettql say:

how do you turn off bullets in word 2013?

how do you turn off bullets in word 2013?
mark_79vz say:
To turn off bullets and numbered lists in Word 2013, do the following:

Go the File tab in the top left of the Document.

On the left side, click on 'Options' (bottom of list, under Account)

When the "Word Options" dialog box pops up, select "Proofing" from the list on the left side.

Click the "AutoCorrect Options" button on the right side of the dialog box.

When the "AutoCorrect" dialog pops up, choose the "AutoFormat As You Type" tab at the top

In the middle portion, where it says "Apply as you type", UN-check the box for "Automatic bulleted lists" AND "Automatic numbered lists".

Click the "OK" button on the "AutoCorrect" dialog box, and then click "OK" on the "Word Options" dialog box.

Hope that helps!

According to a OneNote engineer the error 0xE00000007 most of the times means, that there HAS BEEN a syncing error some time ago. But this error is cached, so the message may remain for quite a while (he said something about up to 30 mins). When you see this error, I would recommend that you check, if your notebook is really not syncing. For example apply any change and login to www.onenote.com using a web browser. If you can see the change in OneNote Online, all is good (except for the annoying message). The dev team is currently looking up for ways to clear this message sooner or another solution to this irritation.

The overlap may occur when text paragraphs have a negative left indent.

The workaround is to remove the negative left indent for the affected text paragraphs.

Another possibility is that the left margin is too narrow, in which case you will have to increase the left margin.
