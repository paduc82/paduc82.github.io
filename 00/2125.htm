joël séguin say:

Hello Lee,

What you get is expected behaviour ("by design"). Let me explain why...

First, remember that the baseline fields has no calculation whatsoever in Project.

The "project" rows you see in a master project actually belongs to the master project, not to the sub projects. You can see it when you look at the IDs. All the information you see on there also belongs to the master project, not to the sub projects. That includes notes, custom fields, and baselines. Even the calculated fields values belong to the master project, but because they are calculated (rolled-up), we don't realize that the value we see is actually calculated in the master project instead of being pulled-up from the project summary task.

In other words, the rows you see in a master project ARE NOT the project summary tasks of the sub projects.

That said, I personally agree that it would've been nice to have baseline rollup. I do it with a macro.

Hope this helps,
