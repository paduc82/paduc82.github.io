Private Sub Worksheet_Change(ByVal Target As Range)
     Dim lnLastRow As Long
    
     If Not Intersect(Target, Range("A:B")) Is Nothing Then
        lnLastRow = Range("A" & Me.Rows.Count).End(xlUp).Row
       
        With Me.Sort
            .SortFields.Clear
            .SortFields.Add Key:=Range("A2:A" & lnLastRow), _
                SortOn:=xlSortOnValues, Order:=xlAscending, DataOption:=xlSortNormal
            .SortFields.Add Key:=Range("B2:B" & lnLastRow), _
                SortOn:=xlSortOnValues, Order:=xlAscending, DataOption:=xlSortNormal
            .SetRange Range("A1:B" & lnLastRow)
            .Header = xlYes
            .MatchCase = False
            .Orientation = xlTopToBottom
            .SortMethod = xlPinYin
            .Apply
        End With
    End If
 End Sub

What you need is to convert the footnotes to endnotes. Without knowing what version of Word you have, I can't provide exact instructions, but this action is performed by clicking the Convert... button in the Footnote and Endnote dialog (ignore the asterisk; this screen shot was created for another purpose):

In Word 2007 and above, open this dialog by clicking the dialog launcher (small arrow in the bottom right corner) in the Footnotes group on the References tab.

In Word 2003 and earlier, click Insert | Reference | Footnote...
