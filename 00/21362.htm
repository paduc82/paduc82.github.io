Card Template in Word: middle/ centre opening

I am trying to make a A5 card that is centre-folded hence one side opening to the right, and the other one to the left.  I want to print something on the outside, and then have a text on the middle/ inside of the card once opened. How do I do this?
suzanne s. barnhill say:

One way is to set up each page in three columns, separated by column breaks so that text can be entered in each column independently of the others (you could use a three-column table, but this comes with many limitations on content and formatting). Put the text for the outside of the card in Column 3 of the first page and the text for the inside of the card in Column 2 of the second page, then print both sides. If you want something on both "fronts," you can also put text in Column 1 (this would normally be the second leaf opened).

Make permanent the non p1 ranges unless they are as text (within brackets)  by adding $

Example A1 to drag down as A1 should be A$1

                      to drag right as A1 should be $A1
