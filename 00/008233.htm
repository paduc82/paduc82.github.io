lars-Åke aspelin say:

Try the following macro:

Sub synch_sheets()

Dim ws1, ws2, ws3 As Worksheet
Dim firstrow1, lastrow1, firstrow2, lastrow2, firstrow3, lastrow3 As Integer
Dim lastcol1, lastcol2, lastcol3 As Integer
Dim r1, r2, r3, c1, c2, c3 As Integer
Dim data_found As Boolean

Set ws1 = Worksheets("Sheet1")
Set ws2 = Worksheets("Sheet2")
Set ws3 = Worksheets("Sheet3")

firstrow1 = 1
lastrow1 = ws1.Cells(firstrow1, "A").End(xlDown).Row
firstrow2 = 1
lastrow2 = ws2.Cells(firstrow2, "A").End(xlDown).Row
firstrow3 = 1
lastrow3 = 0

ws3.Cells.Clear
For r1 = firstrow1 To lastrow1
   lastcol1 = ws1.Cells(r1, "A").End(xlToRight).Column
   For c3 = 1 To lastcol1
     ws3.Cells(firstrow3 + r1 - firstrow1, c3).Value = ws1.Cells(r1, c3).Value
   Next c3
   lastcol3 = lastcol1
   For r2 = firstrow2 To lastrow2
     If ws2.Cells(r2, "A").Value = ws1.Cells(r1, "A").Value Then
       lastcol2 = ws2.Cells(r2, "A").End(xlToRight).Column
       For c2 = 2 To lastcol2
          data_found = False
          For c1 = 1 To lastcol1
            If ws1.Cells(r1, c1).Value = ws2.Cells(r2, c2).Value Then
               data_found = True
               Exit For
            End If
          Next c1
          If Not data_found Then
             lastcol3 = lastcol3 + 1
             ws3.Cells(firstrow3 + r1 - firstrow1, lastcol3).Value = ws2.Cells(r2, c2)
          End If
        Next c2
     End If
   Next r2
Next r1
lastrow3 = lastrow1 - firstrow1 + firstrow3

For r2 = firstrow2 To lastrow2
  data_found = False
  For r1 = firstrow1 To lastrow1
    If ws1.Cells(r1, "A").Value = ws2.Cells(r2, "A").Value Then
      data_found = True
    End If
  Next r1
  If Not data_found Then
    lastrow3 = lastrow3 + 1
    lastcol2 = ws2.Cells(r2, "A").End(xlToRight).Column
    For c2 = 1 To lastcol2
      ws3.Cells(lastrow3, c2).Value = ws2.Cells(r2, c2).Value
    Next c2
  End If
Next r2

End Sub

techfreak_ say:

Skype would not charge her.  However, since she is using a mobile network and not the wireless provided in the hotel, the option to reach would be:

1.  Using her data plan on the phone while she is signed into Skype on her mobile providers network.  This may be expensive.

2.  Using her existing phone plan to answer your incoming audio call.  Depending on her provider, roaming charges may apply.  You would pay the Skype rates based on her phone number.  So for example, if she has a UK phone number, you would pay UK rates while she is in Turkey.

For those two solutions, she would have to contact her provider to find out how much it will cost.  If her phone accepts sim cards, she may be able to purchase a sim card in Turkey that is compatible with her phone that will assign her a Turkey phone number.  She would pay for the sim card and recharge it with credit with the sim card provider and you would be able to reach her by dialing that Turkey phone number (when that sim card is in her phone).  You would have to pay Turkey rates in order to reach her.

Her finding reliable wireless Internet may be the least expensive but possibly make her less accessible when not in range of that network.
