darleen pen say:

Hi Jill,

One of the possible reasons why you are experiencing issues with syncing files with OneDrive is due to some damaged software components of the application. To address this concern, we suggest that you unlink the OneDrive application and then relink it to refresh the syncing process of OneDrive. To do so, follow these steps:

  1. On the System Tray, click the arrow up icon.
  2. Right click the icon for OneDrive, then click on Settings.
  3. Under the Account tab, click Unlink OneDrive button. 
  4. Relink OneDrive by resetting up OneDrive on your device. Enter your Email address and password.
  5. Choose what folders you would want to sync on your computer then click Next.

With regard to the issue you are experiencing with the storage of the account where you shared your Office 365 Home subscription, it is best to contact our Answer Desk Department as they are the support group who could best handle your concern. To be able to contact our Answer Desk Department, visit this link and then click on Get started button. Provide the information needed by the virtual agent for you to get in touch with our Answer Desk Accounts and Billing Department.

If further assistance is needed, don't hesitate to reach out to us.

jim gordon mvp say:

This message displays when the update utility can not locate Microsoft Office components. The usual cause is that Word, Excel, or PowerPoint application files were moved from their proper place in Applications:Microsoft Office 2011 folder.


All you have to do is find the applications on your Mac and drag them back into the proper folder.
