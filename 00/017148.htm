For the certain users, I suggest you follow the steps below to grant them the Reviewer permission:

Add-MailboxFolderPermission -Identity *** Email address is removed for privacy ***:\Calendar -User *** Email address is removed for privacy *** -AccessRights Reviewer

1. Log in to EAC (Exchange admin center) with an admin account.

2. Click recipients > shared.

3. Double click the shard mailbox and then select mailbox delegation.

4. Click the add icon to add users to the Full Access box.
