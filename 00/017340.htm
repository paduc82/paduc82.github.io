rob koch say: Alex,

There's nothing wrong, you're concerned about the wrong things.  That 40,000K is the amount of memory (RAM) being used and is actually lower than the typical figures of 58,000K on Vista and 65,000K for Windows XP that I currently see.  My guess is you are using Windows 7, which may use even less RAM on average, though I haven't checked mine that closely yet.

In fact, most modern anti-malware programs similar to MSE use anywhere from 50 to 100MB (1024K) RAM and sometines even more.  The larger concern with MsMpEng.exe has always been its processor (CPU) utilization running at high numbers close to 100%, which though relatively rare does happen to some individual systems.
