The issue is that in random cases the battery discharge rate goes really high and stays there until the phone is restarted with some tricks. I've observed this issue for quite some time and it is not some small issue but occures quite often and is really annoying.

So what happens is this. Normally in my phone with my settings the Nokia Diagnostics shows the battery draining at rate of 70-80 mAh (excluding spikes). Hello Patrice,

Greetings, I'm sorry that I didn't set the buffer time in the picture I uploaded, which brings you some confusion.

Since the buffer time is the period of time when other customers can't book the same service and the same staff. So, about your question "If i have a tech assigned from 1-2 would i choose the buffer to be 1 hour after?", the answer is after staff 1 is being assigned with the tech service, other customer cannot select staff 1 during 1-3 when booking the tech service. 

So, after do more research and tests, my suggestion is: (1) set a Office 365 group. (2) add all tech department staffs to this group. (3) add the Office group as a staff. (4) create a new service for tech department. (5) only assign the Office 365 group to this service. (6) enable the option "Notify the business via email when a booking is created or changed" for thie service. (7) set the during time and buffer time according to your demands. 

By doing so, I think this can meet your requirement: " We don't want the Tech department to be scheduled for any more than 1 item during any specific timeframe.".

Note: when adding the Office 365 group as the staff, you cannot sync the Office 365 group calendar with the Booking calendar, but after a booking is created, this new booking will auto add to the group calendar and all staff’s personal calendar.
