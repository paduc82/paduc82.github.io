cmdrkeene say:

You can make it bigger by increasing the User Interface Scale (how much it increases the size of the parts of the operating system interface).  This is known as DPI Scaling.

  1. Right-click your desktop wallpaper, go to Screen Resolution.
  2. Do not adjust the resolution. To maintain a crisp clear graphics and fonts, this needs to stay at the full/native resolution.

  3. At the bottom, choose "make text and other items larger or smaller".


  4. Try 125% or 150% scale.  Note that you need to logout/in before you see the changes.  You don't have to use these sizes, you can use any size you want (like 101% or whatever) from the custom size options.
