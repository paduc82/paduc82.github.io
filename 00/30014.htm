Design mistake Surface GO - Microsoft Community

svenvan de velde say:

Design mistake Surface GO

I bought the Surface GO 4 weeks ago. 

I was very happy with my purchase. This is a great product and fit for frequent travellers who spend a lot of time on airplanes, like me ...

That being said, I had to return my new Surface GO for repair after one week! There is a design flaw that everyone should know when considering to buy the Surface GO!

The loudspeakers on the GO are visible on the front pane. You'll find on the top left and right side small carves in the glass at the place where the loudspeakers are ...

These carves are not really visible, as they are covered with a very small strip of black matte cloth, that is covering these loudspeaker openings. 

The design flaw is that these cloth strips are glued on the top of these openings.

This would normally not be a problem,  weren't it that it is a problem due to the unintentional usage that I had with the Surface.

On my travels, I carry one of the latest Samsonite backpacks. These are professional travel assistants that allow me to carry my personal devices without much hassle from airport to airport.

Within the backpack, there is a small sleeve specifically designed for tablets about 11 inch wide. I used this sleeve to store my surface and carry it with me. That sleeve has soft cloth and is a bit "thick". And that is a problem for the Surface GO.

The hairs of the sleeve give friction and touch the cloth of the loudspeakers when gliding the tablet in and out of the sleeve!!!!

As a result, the 2 strips covering the loudspeakers detach and after a week of random usage, you'll find the GO without these strips. They will have come loose and the loudspeakers only grey covers will be visible, making the GO less pretty. 

I returned my GO after a week of usage @ media markt for repair and have been now 3 weeks without the tablet. 

I would like to highlight my DISAPPOINTMENT.

And also to warn future users of this defect. 

I've put this post under the category SCREEN as this is a screen problem!!!!

I can provide the repair details I got from Media markt if needed. My tablet is now @Microsoft for repairs and have received ZERO communication from Microsoft till now.
