don varnau say:

Hi,

 

There is not a setting that will open every link in a new tab.

 

This will open links in a new tab when the link was clicked in another program:

Internet Options> General tab> Tabs> Open links from other programs in:  A new tab.

 

You can open a link in a new tab by:

1. Right-click> open in a new tab

2. Ctrl key + click

3. Click with the mouse wheel button
