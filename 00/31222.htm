spiritx say:
Hi,

Could be a hardware issue that is heat related...... try using a small fan to add to ventilation. If a PC
you can try removing and reseating cards, memory, and cables (do not just snug - remove and replace
any cables on both ends when able) - be sure to remove ALL POWER.

OnBoard sound can also be affected by heat and proper ventilation is needed. One cure for that is to
disable the OnBoard sound and add a sound card. The equivalent of the onboard sound in an outboard
card is pretty cheap. Another possibility is a USB sound card (device) replacement.
