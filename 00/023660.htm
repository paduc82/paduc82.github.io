suzanne s. barnhill say:

When you insert a picture In Line With Text, it behaves like a large font character, so it sits on the text baseline. Since it is taller than the number, it will naturally stick up. If you want it to align with the top of the number, you will have to change the wrapping to, say, Square, and define the position relative to the paragraph (this may require some trial and error.

 

Another approach would be to have text (such as Step 1, Step 2, etc.) as the numbered paragraph and put the picture below. You can either use a line break to position the inline picture or you can put the picture in a separate paragraph formatted with the List Continue style. As a general rule, inline graphics cause a lot less trouble than wrapped ones.
