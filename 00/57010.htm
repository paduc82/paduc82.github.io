Hi,

Please note that if a Microsoft Account has been created, and it was NEVER used ever since it was created, the account is automatically deleted after 10 days. This is to prevent account spammers from creating multiple accounts, and reserving usernames that others that would legitimately like to use.

To prevent this from happening, it's best that after creating the account, log it in at https://account.microsoft.com just a few days after. This just means that the account is active and is in-use. You may also want to login the account at https://mail.live.com which is basically Outlook.com. You can do this within a week, so that the account won't be marked as a spammer and will exist for a long time.

Extra: With the new Services Agreement, Microsoft accounts are now deleted automatically if it's not been used for 5 years(It was only 365 days before). So, it's best that the account is logged in online once in a while as proof that the account is active.
