This information includes the list of websites you have visited, cookies, information you've entered into web forms (for example, your name and address), website passwords, and other temporarily saved information. When you are unable to delete some of your temporary files, that means the particular files is running in background and that will not harm your computer in any way.

I suggest you to follow the steps below to delete temporary files.

Step 1

  1. Press Windows key + R
  2. Type temp and click OK
  3. Press Ctrl + A and click Delete

Step 2

  1. Press Windows key + R
  2. Type %temp% and click OK
  3. Press Ctrl + A and click Delete

Step 3

  1. Press Windows key + R
  2. Type prefetch and click OK
  3. Press Ctrl + A and click Delete
