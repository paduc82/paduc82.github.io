1. As far as I can tell,

msoDataStore/streamforPart1/Item

msoDataStore/streamforPart2/Item

msoDataStore/streamforPart2/Properties

2. according to the [MS-OSHARED] standard, each of streamforPart1, streamforPart2 has to be a unique name (within the .doc, I think )
