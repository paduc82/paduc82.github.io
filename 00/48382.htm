To cancel your auto payments for calling subscription:

  • Log in to Skype website (www.skype.com) using your Skype user name.
  • On the blue panel, click the subscription you want to cancel.
  • Click on settings and you will see the cancel button.

 

For Skype number auto payments:  

  • Log in to Skype website (www.skype.com) using your Skype user name.
  • Under manage features, click on Skype number tab.
  • Click settings and you will see the cancel button.

 

For auto recharge of Skype credits:

  • Log in to Skype website (www.skype.com) using your Skype user name.
  • Under the Account settings, click on auto recharge button.
  • Then click on Cancel button.
