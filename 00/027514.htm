graham mayor say:

Custom sized envelopes (custom sized documents generally) have always been a problem. Word will only print what the current printer driver allows. If the custom size is not available from the driver, then Word will adopt a standard size, which may or may not be suitable. The way to avoid the problem is to use a larger standard paper size and make up the difference with margins.

There may also be an orientation problem which results in the settings in the envelope dialog for orientation not matching what the printer requires. This used to be a common issue with Epson inkjets. See http://www.gmayor.com/envelope_orientation.htm
