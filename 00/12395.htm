karl timmermans [outlook mvp 2012-2018] say:

#1 - Outlook '2013 (Office '365) does not utilize MAPI for Outlook.com accounts which can be configured as EAS (Exchange ActiveSync), POP or Imap). MAPI accounts are created by earlier versions of Outlook using the Hotmail Outlook Connector which does not apply to O'2013.


#2 - Your question is really not related to Office '365 as it is to the type of email accounts that can be utilized by Outlook '2013 (part of O'365). If you want to get a full Exchange backend - you would need to subscribe to a business plan. That said, if you configure an Outlook.com account as EAS - all devices will reflect the current data.
