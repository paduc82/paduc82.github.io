praseetha k say:

Hi,

 

You may try the following steps to copy the files and folders from DVD.
a. Right-click the "Start" button and select "Explore" to open Windows Explorer.
b. Click the "Computer" link in the left navigation pane of Windows Explorer. The drives, including the drive hosting the DVD, will be listed.
c. Click on the DVD drive. The folders and files on the DVD will list in the right navigation pane.
d. Right-click on a file or folder and select "Copy" from the context menu, or press the CRTL + C keys, to copy the file or folder to the clipboard.
e. Navigate to a location on the destination drive and right-click. Select "Paste", or press the CRTL + V keys, to paste the folder or files into the new location.
