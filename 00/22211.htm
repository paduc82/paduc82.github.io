Outlook Hotmail Connector will no longer function properly after switching to Outlook.com and creating a Outlook.com email address if it has not been configured to use the Outlook.com address.  To configure Outlook Hotmail Connector, you can perform the following steps:

 

Outlook 2010

1. In Microsoft Outlook, click File, and then click Add Account.

2. In the Add New Account dialog, click Manually configure server settings or additional server types and then click Next.

3. Click Other, click to select Microsoft Outlook Hotmail Connector, and then click Next.

4. In the ‚ÄúWindows Live Hotmail Settings‚Äù dialog, enter your name, Outlook.com address, and password, and then click OK.

5. When the confirmation dialog appears, click Finish.

 

Outlook 2007 and Outlook 2003

1. In Microsoft Outlook, click the Outlook Connector menu, and then click Add a New Account.

2. In the ‚ÄúWindows Live Hotmail Settings‚Äù dialog, enter your name, Outlook.com address, and password, and then click OK.

 

Optionally, you can safely remove your previous Hotmail account from your Outlook settings.  All your previous Hotmail messages will be in the new Outlook.com mailbox you created in the steps above.
