amit tandon say:

You need to use the $ sign for your cell references, to keep the range constant.

Ex. your formula A2:B438 needs to be updated to $A$2:$B$438. This way even when you copy your formulas to other cells, the range will remain fixed / constant at $A$2:$B$438. If you enter =A1 in a cell & copy this formula down, the next cell will display =A2, but if you copy A$1 down the copied cell will display the same ie. A$1. To keep the cell reference constant, you will need to use the $ sign, so using $A$1 will keep the reference constant to cell A1 irrespective if you copy it down or sideways.

In case this is what you mean.
