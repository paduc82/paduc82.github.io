EA

e.j. gun say:

Try the formula below.  Assuming you have something in A1, then B1 would be:

 

=DATE(LEFT(A1,4),RIGHT(A1,2),1)

 

This will give you the first day of the month.  If you want the last day of each month instead, you can use:

 

=DATE(LEFT(A1,4),RIGHT(A1,2)+1,0)
