If you want to configure Office 365 account on your Outlook client and use Gmail account as connected account in this Office 365 account (the email data in Gmail will sync to Office 365 cloud), please do the following steps:

1. Sign into Outlook Web App with your Office 365 account.

2. Navigate to Options > Mail > Accounts > Connected accounts.

3. Add your Gmail account as connected account. For more information, you can refer to this article: Connect email accounts in Outlook on the web (Office 365)

Note: Before adding your Gmail account as connected account, you may need to enable 2-step verification in Gmail. Here is an article for your reference: Prepare your Gmail or G Suite account for connecting to Outlook and Office 365.

4. Open Control Panel > User Accounts > Mail > Show Profiles… > Add a new profile. Configure your Office 365 account (If you have already configure the Office 365 account on the Outlook client, you may skip this step).

5. After adding profile, open your Outlook client and choose the newly created profile. Create a new email, navigate to Options > Show Fields > Click “From” and you can change the From address. Then the sender will be user@contoso.com on behalf of user@gmail.com, like this:

 

If you want to reply like “user@gmail.com”, not “user@contoso.com on behalf of user@gmail.com”, you can directly add your Gmail account into the Outlook client, but Office 365 will not sync the message data in your Gmail account to the cloud. For details, refer the “Step 2: Add your Gmail account to Outlook” part in this article.

 

If I’ve misunderstood what you mean, please provide us with an example of what you want to achieve.
