mary sauer say:

Some printers will not pick up the stock properly. Could be the rollers are not working well.

When this happens the best way is to create the business cards manually.

Arrange, Layout Guides, Left & right margins --.75, top and bottom -- .5

Grid guide tab, 2 columns, 5 rows. No spacing

Create rectangles around the spaces, using plain paper, print. If the alignment is wrong nudge the rectangles until the print is correct. Save this publication. Create your card within the one of the spaces, copy/paste to fill all the spaces. Delete the rectangles.

macropod say:

If, after doing your large-scale Find/Replace, you temporarily disable Word's 'Autoformat as you type' conversion option for straight quotes to smart quotes, you could do another Find/Replace to change any opening smart quotes followed by a space to closing smart quotes followed by a space. The Find/Replace expression for that would be:

Find = ^0147^32

Replace = ^0148^32

Depending on whether you're confident you don't have any legitimate opening smart quotes followed by a space, you may or may not be able to use 'Replace All' for this.
