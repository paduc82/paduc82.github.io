I'm a Product Manager for sync on OneNote. Just as an FYI, our mobile clients don't have background sync quite yet, but it's something we're working hard on building. The following instructions are what we recommend doing is the following: 

Reset your credentials for the notebook

  1. Go into settings and logout of OneNote
  2. Then make sure OneNote is not running by force closing it
    1. iOS double tap the home button and swipe up on an app
    2. Android  touch the recent apps button on the bottom of your screen and swipe sideways, on Windows Phone hold the back button and press the "x" on the top right hand corner of the app)
    3. Windows Phone: hold the back button and press the "x" on the top right hand corner of the app
  3. Then re-open OneNote and sign in again.

 Wait for OneNote to sync

  1. Plug in your device(s) into a wall outlet/computer so they’re charging.
  2. Turn off Auto-Lock
    1. iOS:  go into “Settings”, then tap “General”. Tap “Auto-Lock” and choose “Never”
    2. Android: go into "Settings", then tap "Sound & display", then tap "Screen Timeout", and select Never
    3. Windows Phone: Press Windows Key, type settings and select Setting from the list, Navigate to Account />Sign-In options/>Lock Screen />Screen time out Change the value as required under Screen as per requirement.
  3. On an iOS device(s), go into “Settings” again and tap “Battery”. Make sure “Low Power Mode” is turned off (this option will only appear if you’re running iOS 9, so no worries if you don’t see it).
  4. Open OneNote and navigate to the notebook where you’re experiencing these issues.
  5. Force OneNote to sync
    1. iOS: In the upper right hand corner there’s a sharing button (a box with an arrow coming out of it). Tap it and then tap “Sync Now”. Do this for each iOS device you own.
    2. Android: Tap the overflow menu in the upper right hand corner and select "Sync all"
    3. Windows Phone: From the menu tap "Sync"
  6. Leave the iOS devices on overnight to let them sync.

How to setup Microsoft Exchange with Mac Mail - Microsoft Community

jonathan mc. say:

How to setup Microsoft Exchange with Mac Mail

How can I setup my Outlook.com account to sync with Mac Mail using Exchange ActiveSync? The settings provided currently return a "server not responding" error.

a. user say:

Hi,

You are Welcome and I will be glad to assist you further.

The following steps might help you configure Microsoft Exchange account on Mac Mail:

Step 1.

Open Mac Mail. If this is the first time you have run Mac Mail, the Add Account wizard will be displayed, in which case you can skip steps 2 and 3.

Step 2.

Click Preferences from the Mail menu.

Step 3.

Click the Create an account button to open the Add Account wizard. This is located under the Accounts list, on the bottom-left of the window.

Step 4.

Enter your name in the Full Name text-box. This is what most people will see when you send them an email. Type your email address in the Email Address text-box. Enter the password for the mailbox in the Password text box.

Step 5.

On the Incoming Mail Server page, enter the following information:

  • Account Type: Select Exchange from the drop down menu.
  • Description: Enter a name for this account so you can identify it. This is only visible to you.
  • Incoming Mail Server: Enter exchange.livemail.co.uk.
  • User Name: Enter your full email address.
  • Password: Enter the password for the mailbox.
  • Outlook Web Access Server: Enter exchange.livemail.co.uk.

Step 6.

Leave Authentication set to Password and click Next, then enter the following information on the Outgoing Mail Server screen:

  • Outgoing Mail Server: Enter smtp.livemail.co.uk.
  • Use only this server: Tick this box.
  • Use  Authentication: Tick this box.
  • User  Name: Enter your full email address.
  • Password: Enter the password for this mailbox (the same password that you entered on the previous screen).

Step 7.

Change Authentication to Password. Click Next and then confirm the details you have filled out and click the Create button. Change Authentication to Password. Click Next and then confirm the details you have filled out and click.

Please reply with the status.
