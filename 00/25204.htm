Hi Praful,

Let's discuss with your questions one by one.

Moreover, when we create a team or an Office 365 group, a site collection related to this team and group will be automatically created. This kind of site collections cannot be seen in SharePoint admin center. To learn more, see Create a team site in SharePoint Online.

2. How to add group or user to these site collections?

a. For the site collections in SharePoint admin center, see Customize SharePoint site permissions. A simple method is to directly share items in the site to that end user or group.

b. For the site collections which is related to an Office 365 group or a team. Normally it should be managed by the owner of that group or team. As global admin, you may check in Office 365 admin center>Groups>find the group>add yourself as owner of the group. Use SharePoint Online PowerShell. Get-SPOSite command.

a. For sites in SharePoint admin center, see Manage administrators for a site collection.

b. For site collections cannot be seen in SharePoint admin center, you may let group, team owner share the site collection with you and give you full control permission.

    Hi Marie,

    Office 2016 for Mac can be used with multiple languages, all of which are installed by default.     If you still want to change the language of your operating system, follow these steps:

    1. Click the Apple menu, and then click System Preferences.

    2. Click Language & Region.

    3. Click the + sign under Preferred languages, select a language from the list, and click Add.

    4. When asked if you want to make this language your primary language, click Use [Language Name].

      You can also set a primary language by dragging a language to the top of the Preferred languages list.

    5. You may have to restart your Mac for language changes to take effect.

    maritzacapiro say:
    Hi.

    To delete your Yammer account, follow these steps:

    1. Delete your messages:

    - Near the bottom of one of your publications, click More and then select Delete.

    - Repeat this for all messages that you want to delete.



    2. Delete your account:

    - Log in to your Yammer account.

    - In the lower right corner, click on the settings icon and then on View profile.

    - On your profile page, in the upper right corner, click Edit profile.

    - In the panel on the left side, click on Preferences.

    - In the upper right corner of the Preferences page, click Delete your Yammer account.


    Note After deleting your account, it is unrecoverable and can not be reactivated.


    Check this link:

    https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/help/281729...
