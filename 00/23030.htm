I don't believe the font or color of the preface comments is separately controllable.  The font and color of your reply text is controlled at File>Options>Mail>Stationery and Fonts>Replying or forwarding messages.  The color you choose there will be for all the text you enter, including the preface text added automatically.  The preface text will be bolded and italicized, while what you type will be normal.  They'll both be the same color, however.
