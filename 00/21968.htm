Support for Office 2007 has ended and unfortunately it is no longer available for download from MSFT. If you do not have the installation disc and the keycode you cannot reinstall on a new computer.

You also might want to read this article: https://answers.microsoft.com/en-us/msoffice/forum/msoffice_install-mso_other-msoversion_other/copy-move-or-transfer-an-office-installation-to/956244a0-385d-4e21-a21e-87b4179111d1

If you don't want to buy a new version of Office then you can download and install the freebie https://www.libreoffice.org/
