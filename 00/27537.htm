Getting Captions to Repeat on Multiple Pages - Microsoft Community

feliciac. say:

Getting Captions to Repeat on Multiple Pages

I have a Table that goes on for nine pages. I need the Table Caption (not the table headings) to appear on all nine pages. How do I achieve this? I want the Table Caption (For example, Table 1 List of Post-apocalyptic films) to appear just on those nine pages. The entire document is 200 pages long and has nothing to do with the table.  Thank you for your help.

doug robbins - mvp office apps & services (word) say:

There is no reason that you cannot have the Styleref field and the page number in the footer.

What you will need to do is

  1. Insert a Section Break before the table and another one after it.  To insert Section Breaks, put the selection where you want the break to be located and then click on Breaks in the Page Setup section of the Page Layout tab and select Next Page.
  2. Then in that Section (the one containing the table), double click in the Footer area to open the Footer pane and then on the Header & Footers Tools>Design tab of the ribbon that appears, click on the Next button in the Navigation section and then click on the Link to Previous to unlink the footer in that Section, (the one after the one containing the table) from the footer in the Previous Section.  You have to do this so that you can get the changes that you make to the footer of the Section containing the table apply only to that Section of the document.
  3. Then click on the Previous button to return to the Section containing the table and once again, click on the Link to Previous to unlink the footer in that Section from the first part of your document.
  4. Then click on the Different First Page button and then click on the Next button to move to the Footer pane that will appear on the second and subsequent pages of the Section containing the table.
  5. Then with the selection in the footer at the location where you want the caption to appear, go to the Insert tab of the ribbon and click on Quick Parts>Field to display the Field dialog and scroll down through the list of fields until you come to the StyleRef field.  When you click on that, a list of the Styles in your document will appear and form that list, select the Caption Style and then click on OK.

Your caption should then appear in the footer pane, which you can close by clicking on the Close Header and Footer button in the Header & Footer Tools>Design tab.
