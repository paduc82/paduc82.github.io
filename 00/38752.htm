kiran jayakumar say:

Hi,

 

There is no option to pin the Power setting to taskbar, but as a workaround you may create a shortcut for 'Shutdown' and 'Restart' and pin the shortcut to taskbar. To create a shortcut, refer to the following steps:

a) Right-click an open area on the desktop, point to New, and then click Shortcut.

b) To create a shortcut to shutdown the computer, type in 'shutdown /p' (without quotes).

c) Click on 'Next', type in a name for the shortcut.

d) Click on 'Finish'.

 

After the shortcut is created, right-click on it and select 'Pin to Taskbar'. You may also change the icon of the shortcut. To do so, refer to the following steps:

a) Right-click on the shortcut and select 'Properties'.

b) Under 'Shortcut' tab, click 'Change Icon'.

c) Select the icon you want and then click on 'Ok' to apply changes.

d) Click 'Ok' to close the properties windows.

 

Hope the information helps.
