tammy.m say:

Hello jgy2001,

You can try the following and see if that corrects the problem.

Your problem may be that you do not have the Outlook Address Book service.

Here are the instructions to add the Outlook Address Book service.
Outlook 2010
1. Click on the File menu, click on Account Settings
2. In the Account Settings dialog click on the Address Books tab
3. Click on the New or Add button.
4. In the Add Account dialog box click Additional Address Books, click Next
5. Click to select Outlook Address Book click Next.
You’ll be prompted that you must close and restart Outlook for changes to take effect.
Press OK and close and restart Outlook.
The service is now added.
Once the service is added the option "Show this folder as an email address book" should no longer be greyed out.
To turn on this service
1. Right click on the Contacts folder that you want displayed in the Address Book.
2. Click to select the Outlook Address Book tab
3. Click to enable the option "Show this folder as an email address book"
Note: You will need to repeat the instructions to "Show this folder as an email address book" for each contacts folders.
If after completing these instructions you still cannot add your Contacts folder to the Address Book then creating a new profile is recommended.

How to create and configure an email profile in Outlook 2010, Outlook 2007, and Outlook 2003
http://support.microsoft.com/kb/829918
