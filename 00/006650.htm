janchan say:
Sub MakeImages()
    Dim pic As Picture
    Dim rng As Range
    
    For Each rng In Selection
        Set pic = ActiveSheet.Pictures.Insert(rng.Value)
        pic.Height = rng.Height
        pic.Width = rng.Height
        pic.Left = rng.Left + 200
        pic.Top = rng.Top
    Next

End Sub


Good luck

Simply add this macro into your workbook, select the cells with a full filename and path and run the macro, you may want to play with 200 to fit your particular spreadsheet.

I have suggested using rng.height as both height and width to make your thumbnail look small, but the image will still be as large as it was originally, in other words your excel file size will be huge.

It removes also your browsing history, but other settings are preserved.
