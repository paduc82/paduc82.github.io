carlosasduarte say: Hi there,

I had the same problem: When reply through WP8 it would generate two sent emails.

By examining Gmail's web client, I noticed a new label called "Sent Items", in addition to the standard (Gmail native) "Sent Mail" folder. This was the reason for the "duplication", the same message in both folders.

After many tries I think I've found a solution:

1 - In WP8 stop synchronizing the new "Sent Items" folder (native from WP8). If you want to keep track of the sent items in your phone just synchronize the "Sent Mail" folder, under Gmail section;

2 - In Gmal web client, go to Settings > Labels and locate the Sent Items folder. There you'll find a tick box called "Show in IMAP". Untick that option! It will prevent Gmail to receive that extra message from WP8;

3 - Synchronize the Google account in WP8 device and perform a new test.
All the new sent messages should stop duplicating!
